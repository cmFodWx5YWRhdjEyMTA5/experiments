Steinel Traditional PIR Wall Light L15 Black

Product Questions

Q1: What is the minimum range and detection angle?
A1: Detection angle 180° and Reach max. 10m

Q2: 1. Is the height shown just the lantern element or is it the whole unit ie. Top tip of lantern to bottom of wall bracket plate.
2. What is the height and width of the wall bracket plate at maximum points.

A2: The height stated will be the total height from the top of the lantern to the lowest point of the back plate, probably with the sensor pointing straight down.
The back plate dimensions are 160mm height x 110mm wide. If you have the sensor pointed straight down the height becomes 185mm

Q3: How adjustable is the pir? Our current lantern comes on every time someone walks along pavement in front of house or when a car goes past. We want to direct it down more onto our front path.
A3: The PIR sensor on this light is adjustable, and can be pointed downwards if required

Q4: Does the light come with a manual override option, So that it can be permanently lit?
A4: Unfortunately, this fitting does not have a manual override I'm afraid. Please enter 'manual override' into the search-bar to find all the fittings with this feature

Q5: Hi. Will this light work with low energy e27 bulbs/ led bulbs.

regards mark


A5: Yes, this fitting will work with low energy E27 lamps

Q6: The sensor is infra red. Does this mean that it will not activate by the movement of leaves or branches. I have ivy on the wall adjacent to the position i wish to place the lamp
A6: The sensor is infrared so will only activate the light upon the detection of heat and movement. Trees wouldn't normally trigger this type of sensor

L15 traditional style black outdoor polycarbonate wall mounted lantern.

This wall light is supplied with a 90° vertically tilting infra-red sensor which also has a 180° angle of coverage and a maximum detection range of 10M.

This light also has a twilight setting of 2-2000 lux, with a time setting of 8 seconds - 35 minutes.

Requires 1 x 60W Max E27 Gls Lamp (Not Included).

Comes with a 3 year manufacturers warranty.

This is part of the L15 Range.
