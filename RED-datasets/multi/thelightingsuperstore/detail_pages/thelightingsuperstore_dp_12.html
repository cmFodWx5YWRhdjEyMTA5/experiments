Seran LED Exterior Flush Wall Light 55689

Product Questions

Q1: Is this rain proof?  Can it be installed at back of the house (with no cover)?
A1: Yes this is rain proof and can be fitted to the back of the house with no cover

Q2: I want to buy a few pieces of this lamp. However i have a concern if the light source inside is defective, Can i change it?


A2: This light runs with a built in LED Board so is unable to be changed, it does however come with a 5 year warranty. And is estimated to have a lifespan of around 25,000 hours.

Q3: Is the led driver housed inside the light fitting?
A3: Yes, the LED driver is housed inside the light fitting

Q4: How many led,s are on the board?
A4: According to the supplier there is 24 LED chips on the 12 watt board

Q5: If used this on ceiling of an open porch, How open is the light to the ingress of insects? How easy is it to remove the diffuser lens for cleaning ?
A5: This is IP44 rated so is capable of preventing the ingress of objects sized at 1mm plus. The lens is removed by removing the four retaining screws on the front

Q6: Can this light be . Mounted on the inside wall of a shower cubicle? Thanks
A6: No this light only has an IP44 rating. You need IP65 for a shower cubicle.

Seran LED bulkhead flush outdoor light fitting.

This product can be fitted to a wall or ceiling of a porch, comes with an ingress protection of IP44.

Manufactured from polycarbonate with a textured matt black surround, with an opal diffuser.

The fitting is circular in shape.

Runs with a 12 watt LED module (LED driver included), which gives out 1000 lumen of light, with a colour temperature of 6000 Kelvin (cool white).

This product comes with a 5 year manufacturers warranty.

This is part of the Seran Range.

Class 2 Double Insulated. No Earth Required..

Finish: Black.

Light Source: LED.

Dimming Info: Not Suitable For Dimming.

Size:
  • Height: 300mm
  • Width: 300mm
  • Projection: 90mm
