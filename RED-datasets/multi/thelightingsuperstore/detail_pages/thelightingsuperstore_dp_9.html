A1: 35w is the maximum the lights can take so you couldnt have a 50w unfortunately, but you could have a 35w in the top and a 20w in the bottom.

Q2: How is the main body of the light fixed to he back plate? How is the light fixed to the wall?
A2: The main body of the light is screwed to the backplate and this is then attached to the wall using the supplied screws and plugs. We would strongly recommend using an electrician to install the fitting.

palin double outdoor halogen spotlight.

This product is manufactured in stainless steel 201 (austenitic chromium-nickel-manganese) and finished in brushed stainless steel.

It is tubed in shape and is attached to a circular back plate.

There is also a circular clear glass cover on each end of the light.

Comes complete with fixing accessories and has a two year warranty.

Requires 2 X 35 watt GU10 halogen lamps (not included).

The wall bracket measures 85mm.

The tube body of the light fitting measures 60mm.

N.B. From September 2016, All light fittings that have a GU10 lamp holder will not be automatically supplied with halogen bulbs. However, there may be a small amount of residual stock left over which still have the free of charge halogen bulbs included.
