Icarus exterior wall light.

IP44 rated modern LED outdoor wall light that is vandal and corrosion resistant making this a very durable and long lasting fitting.

It has a single spot that shines downwards that is fixed to a round wall bracket.

Supplied with 1 x 2.5 watt LED GU10 bulb which emits a total of 200 lumen of light, with a colour temperature of 6500 kelvin (Cool white).

Constructed from black polypropylene, with a clear polycarbonate diffuser.

This is part of the Icarus Spot Range.

Class 2 Double Insulated. No Earth Required..

Finish: Black.

Light Source: LED with a GU10 lamp holder. Fittings that are supplied with non-dimmable GU10 LED bulbs or those supplied without any GU10 bulbs can, in most instances, be made dimmable by installing a dimmable GU10 LED.

Dimming Info: Not Suitable For Dimming.

Ingress Protection Rating: IP44.

Size:
  • Height: 120mm
  • Width: 80mm
  • Projection: 90mm
