Halbury LED PIR Sensor Exterior Wall Light 54553

Product Questions

Q1: Hi is this a dawn till dusk light?
A1: The sensor will only turn the light on when it senses movement and when the light has dropped to a certain level.

Q2: Is the sensor range adjustable? As i want it very low, Probably about 1 metre, And can you override it to make it stay on when required?
A2: The sensor is adjustable up to a maximum of around 8 metres. Unfortunately it does not have an override function but it has a timer to make it stay on for up to five minutes. Below is a link of our lights that do have an manual override function.

Search.Aspx?Search=Override&Submit=Search

Q3: Hi there i am unclear how much light will be given off

-can you confirm if it is equivalent to a traditional bulb 40/60/100w?
A3: This is the equivalent to around 50watts

Q4: Is there a dawn to dusk light that compliments this light? I need 3 pir but one d/d too
A4: No PIR or without only, this product does not come with a dusk till dawn sensor.

Q5: Can this light be wired into the mains using a standard 3 pin plug?
A5: Yes this can be wired into the mains using a 3 pin plug

Q6: Does the mains cable enter the unit at the top or bottom?
A6: The mains cable on the light enters near the bottom of the light, there is a PDF sheet on our website linked to the product showing a diagram of installation that may help also

Q7: Hi could you please tell me what the lamp is made of? It is plastic or merely casing?
A7: The fitting is made from a durable polycarbonate which is a strong plastic

Halbury security exterior half lantern LED wall light.

This product comes with a PIR (passive infrared) motion detector which comes on at 20 lux and 2000 lux off, with a detection angle of 130°, and has a 8 meter maximum range with a 5 second to 5 minute timer function.

Manufactured from polycarbonate with a black surround and opal diffusers.

Runs with 1 x 7 watt LED module (driver included) which gives out 500 lumen of light, with a natural white colour (4500 kelvin.

Comes with a 5 year manufacturers warranty.

This is part of the Halbury Range.
