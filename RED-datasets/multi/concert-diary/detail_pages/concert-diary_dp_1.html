  2. Requiem Mass in D minor K 626 - Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart

Sheku Kanneh-Mason (BBC Young Musician 2016) performs Haydn's Cello Concerto in C with his peers from the Junior Royal Academy of Music. After the interval the Laudate Choir join the Academy orchestra and four outstanding young professional soloists to perform Dr Timothy Jones's completion of Mozart's Requiem. All performed in the glorious surroundings of Dorchester's ancient Abbey.

Join Dr Timothy Jones for a free pre-concert talk at 6.30pm.
