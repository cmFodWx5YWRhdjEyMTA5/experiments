Description

9W Dimmable PAR30 LED LED Flood Light


This new dimmable LED flood lamp has a 120 degree beam angle and consumes only 9 watts!! It is a direct replacement for a 75W E27 conventional flood lamp. The adjustable range of this LED lamp is very good. The cool white version of this bulb produces 565 lumens. PAR30 lamps are designed for installation in an E26/27 socket track light and spot light application. It uses 120-volt AC power and a state of the art aluminum sacked-fin technology for maximum heat dissipation. This LED PAR30 is available in day white and warm white temperatures for your everyday needs. Ideal for LED lighting applications that need adjustable dimming control.
