Bright LED PAR38 Dimmable Flood Light

This is a fully Dimmable LED light bulb. The natural white puts out 672 lumens and the warm white generates 588 lumens and they consume only 9 watts of energy! It replaces any household 100W incandescent/halogen bulb. This bulb will give you 90% energy savings!

Replaces up to a 100 Watt - SAVE ENERGY NOW BY ORDERING

This 9 watt LED bulb replaces up to a 100W conventional flood lamp with a E27 or E26 medium screw base (100R38). These PAR38 LED lamps are designed for installation in standard North American screw-in sockets. This bulb can be used in recessed lighting, lamps, enclosed landscape lighting, and many more applications. It generates a 100 degree beam angle for general purpose wide-spot and narrow-flood applications. It uses 120-volt AC power and is in a glass enclosed housing. This adjustable dimming LED PAR38 is available in warm white and natural white color temperatures for your everyday needs. Glass Housing on this LED flood lamp is water resistant, No UV or RF interference, and optical diffuser, longer life than incandescent & fluorescent light bulbs. No mercury, no ballast required, wide voltage and constant current design, solid state, Mercury free, 90%+ savings in energy consumption, quiet, no noise, no flickering, The products are in accordance with CE testing standards.
