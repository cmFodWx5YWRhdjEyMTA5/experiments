Description

6W LED PAR30 Dimmable Flood Light

This new dimmable 6 watt LED light bulb replaces up to a 60 watt light bulb. This LED flood light produces 410 lumens in it's natural white version. These PAR30 LED lights are designed for installation in standard North American screw-in sockets (E26 and E27 base types). This bulb can be used in recessed lighting, lamps, enclosed landscape lighting, and many other applications. The LED PAR30 flood light generates a 120 degree beam angle for general purpose flood applications and uses 120-volt AC power. This new adjustable LED PAR30 is available in warm white and natural white color temperatures for your everyday needs.
This is a fully Dimmable LED light bulb. It replaces up to a 60W incandescent/halogen flood light bulb. This bulb will give you up to 90% energy savings!
Glass Housing, No UV or RF interference, and optical diffuser, longer life than incandescent & fluorescents .
