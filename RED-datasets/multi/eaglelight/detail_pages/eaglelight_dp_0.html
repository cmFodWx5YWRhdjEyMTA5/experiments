Description

9W Dimmable PAR30 LED Spot Light

Dimmable PAR30 LED spot light with great dimming range. This 9W LED Bulb replaces up to a 75W conventional spot lamp with a E27 medium screw base (60R30 or 75R30). The day white version of this bulb produces 515 lumen. These PAR30 lamps are designed for installation in standard North American screw-in sockets. This bulb can be used in recessed lighting, lamps, enclosed landscape lighting, and many more applications. Offering a 60 degree beam angle for general purpose wide-spot and narrow-flood applications. It uses 120-volt AC power and advanced heat management. EagleLight's LED PAR30 is a super bright LED system and is available in warm white, natural white, day white and commercial white color temperatures.
  • Dimmable PAR30 LED spot light 60 degree beam angle
  • UL approved LED PAR30 LED spot light
  • Excellent light quality and wide variety of white colors
  • Over 500 Lumen in LED PAR30
