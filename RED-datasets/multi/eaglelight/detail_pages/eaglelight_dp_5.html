Description

11W Dimmable PAR30 LED Flood Light - New Light Weight Design

This new dimmable PAR30 LED flood lamp has a 120 degree beam angle and is very light weight. LED lighting just got better, at less than 6 ounces it is the lightest, and brightest LED PAR30, R30, 75R30 lamp we have seen on the market. It replaces up to 75 watts. The cool white version of this bulb produces 610 lumens. This LED flood light has a 120 degree beam angle and is designed for installation in an LED E26 or E27 socket. It is 4.5 inches long and can be made to fit deeper recessed fixtures using an optional 1" extender (available separately). This extremely bright LED PAR30 is available in cool white, day white, natural white and warm white temperatures for home LED lighting, commercial LED retrofit projects including retail lighting, hospitality lighting, school lighting and university LED lighting applications.
This bulb is lighter in weight and brighter in shine!!
