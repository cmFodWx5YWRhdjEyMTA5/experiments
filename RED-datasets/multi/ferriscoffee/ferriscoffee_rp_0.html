    Specialty coffee 
    crafted for taste

    We have both a privilege and a responsibility in what we do. It's our privilege to see you, the consumer, taste and enjoy coffees that many people have worked hard to produce. It's is our responsibility to roast and brew each coffee to its greatest potential.

    The direct trade difference.

    We believe in the need for sustainability in the specialty coffee industry. It is imperative that we understand the needs and production costs of the producers we work with, and therefore we are working hard to source as many coffees as possible through direct trade. By visiting farms and working with producers, we can ensure that the relationships formed through great coffee can last for years to come.
