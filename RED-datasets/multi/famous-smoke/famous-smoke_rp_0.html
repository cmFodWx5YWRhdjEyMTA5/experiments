    Premium Cigars

    Premium cigars are what Famous Smoke Shop is all about. But to buy premium cigars online, smoke them and enjoy them, you need to know what makes 'em different. What distinguishes a "premium" from a "non-premium" is that premium cigars are made entirely by hand with long leaf or mixed fill (Cuban sandwich) tobaccos - wrapped in whole tobacco leaf, a 100% leaf tobacco binder and has no filter, tip or non-tobacco mouthpiece (among other qualities). Want us to drop some more stogie knowledge? Stick around, we'll tell you everything: where to buy cigar boxes, how to season a humidor, and more - but let's start with how to score a tremendous deal on premiums like these. See, what sets us apart from the rest is that we smoke what we sell, and we wouldn't sell it if we wouldn't smoke it. So if you want to know where to buy good cigars and want the ins and outs on achieving premium cigar satisfaction, you start at Famous. We're right here at the hottest corner in the neighborhood: the intersection of best premium cigars and discount prices.

    And now, a word about Cuban cigars...

    With U.S.-Cuba relations getting warmer, there's a ton of news (and healthy amount of speculation) going around lately with people wondering, can you buy Cuban cigars online. Short answer: no. While the restrictions for bringing Cubans back for personal consumption have eased, it's still illegal to sell them here. But the rules seem to be evolving pretty quickly, and we're still not sure where this whole "are Cuban cigars legal" debate is going to end up. So can you buy Cuban cigars now? Not yet - but we'll keep you posted if and when it happens.
