    Inferno By Oliva 660

    Whoa baby! This Inferno by Oliva 660 cigar is loaded with hearty Nicaraguan ligero for a heady, yet amazingly smooth, full-bodied smoke. Thicker Habano wrappers keep this big boy from unraveling on you, too. Savor it slowly and you're in for about 2 hours of intense tobacco pleasure. Get yours now.
