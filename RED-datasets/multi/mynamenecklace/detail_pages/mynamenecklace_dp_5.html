Enjoying the latest fashion trend that movie stars and famous singers have been wearing has never been so easy with the Sterling Silver Celebrity Monogram Necklace!
One of the hottest trends in personalized jewelry, this round monogram necklace is a funky way to show off your own style and uniqueness.

You can choose up to three initials to create this picture perfect monogram piece. This Alexis monogram necklace is definitely going to turn heads when you pair it with any outfit, from casual day time looks to nights on the town outfits.

The necklace is made of 0.925 Sterling Silver and hangs on a Sterling Silver Rollo Chain.
This monogram initials necklace is also available in 18k Gold-Plating, 18k Rose Gold-Plating, 14k Solid Gold and Colorful Acrylic.
Also check out our XXL Celebrity Monogram or Small Celebrity Monogram Necklace in Sterling Silver.

Buy this necklace as part of a SET and save 16%.
A monogram is typically structured with the last name as the large initial in the center, the left initial is the first name, and the right initial is the middle name.
If you only choose 2 initials for your monogram, they will both be the same size.
