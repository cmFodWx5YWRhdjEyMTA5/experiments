Residential/Commercial Technicians

  • Security Equipment, Inc
  • Kansas City, KS, United States
  • Mar 22, 2017
Full time Installation-Maint-Repair Retail

Job Description

Minimum experience or requirements: Qualified candidates must possess a minimum of

2 years of installation or service related experience with Burglary, Fire, Closed Circuit

Television, and Access Control. In lieu of the aforementioned experience we will consider

individuals who have completed classes in Electronics and have full understanding of AC/DC

theory. Computer networking experience is required.

COMMERCIAL INSTALLATION TECHNICIAN

Job Summary

The purpose of this position is to install and test electronic security systems including burglar and fire

alarms, IP Video, Access Control systems and all related electronic security equipment in commercial

and industrial environments and to provide excellent customer service to our clients.

Essential Job Functions

The essential functions include, but are not limited to the following:

 Install Access Control, IP Video, Burglar Alarm, and Fire systems

 Understands specifications as indicated in work order to complete the installation of commercial

alarm systems

 Prefer candidate with proficient knowledge of computer software, hardware, network

infrastructure and IP schemes.

 Utilizes a variety of hand tools to install equipment in locations as requested by customer or

identified on work order.

 Utilize technical skills to read blueprints and schematic wiring diagrams

 Ensures worksite is clean after completion of job.

 Loads inventory to ensure the necessary materials for assigned job is on the truck and transports

to job site.

 Tests installed systems following company protocols.

 Provides necessary end user training for customers and their designees.

 Take care of other tasks as needed to complete the installation.

Requirements

 High school diploma. Technical School Training a plus.

 Minimum one year of experience installing Burglar Systems, and IP Video.

 General knowledge of International Building Code, Life Safety Code and the ability to read

blueprints and schematic drawings.

 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.

 Networking experience specifically with cable termination combination

 A proficiency  to install and program alarm security systems

 Proficiency with running and pulling cable, programming and calibrating equipment while

adhering to codes, regulations and standards

 Ability to install program and trouble shoot wireless devices.

 Able to climb ladders and manipulate hand tools.

 Skill and ability to meet with customers and determine their expectations.

 Ability to provide the necessary skills for troubleshooting a variety of systems.

 Ability to interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral or schedule form.

Confidential Information

This position has access to and must maintain the confidentiality of the company.

Note: The statements herein are intended to describe the general nature and level of work being performed by employees

assigned to this classification. They are not intended to be constructed as an exhaustive list of all responsibilities, duties,

and skills required of personnel so classified. This job description may be changed or updated at any time without notice.

RESIDENTIAL INSTALLATION TECHNICIAN

Job Summary

The purpose of this position is to install and test electronic security systems including burglar and fire

alarms, IP Video, Access Control systems and all related electronic security equipment in residential

and small commercial environments while providing unmatched customer service to our clients.

Essential Job Functions

The essential functions include, but are not limited to the following:

 Install Access Control, IP Video, Hard wired and Wireless Burglar Alarm Systems.

 Ability to interpret specifications as indicated in work order to complete the installation of work.

 Utilize technical skills to read blueprints and schematic wiring diagrams

 Ability to utilize a variety of hand tools and specialty equipment with proficiency

 Ability to identify, understand and utilize basic networking equipment and practices to complete

necessary job functions.

 Ensures worksite is well organized during installation and clean after completion of job.

 Tests and troubleshoots installed systems following company protocols.

 Provides necessary end user training for customers and their designees.

 Perform other duties as assigned

Requirements

 High school diploma. Technical School Training a plus.

 General knowledge of International Building Code, Life Safety Code and the ability to read

blueprints and schematic drawings.

 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.

 Networking experience specifically with cable termination combination

 Prefer candidate with proficient knowledge of computer software, hardware, network

infrastructure and IP schemes.

 General understanding or ability to learn electrical principles and circuits

 Able to climb ladders and manipulate hand tools.

 Ability to interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral or schedule form.

 Must be able to push, pull and lift up to 50 pounds.

 Ability to perform on-call duties as assigned

This position has access to and must maintain the confidentiality of the company.
