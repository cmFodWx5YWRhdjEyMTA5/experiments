Description
A beautiful character cottage set in a unique position offering glorious views of the River Thames and walking distance of Abingdon town centre. Situated over three floors with three bedrooms, kitchen, dining room, living room, bathroom, cloakroom, a pretty balcony area overlooking the river, private garden and parking. The property must be viewed to appreciate the uniqueness and character on offer. No onward chain.

Entrance
The property is approached via a wrought iron gate, with steps down leading to the entrance door.

Inner Hallway
With stable door entrance leading into an entrance hall with radiator and stairs leading to the ground and second floors. Doors to Lounge and Bedroom One

Ground Floor

Kitchen L-Shaped Room 13' 5" x 12' 11' 5" x 4' 5" (4.09m x 3.66m3.48m x 1.35m )
Full of character with feature wooden beams and seating 'alcove'. Fitted kitchen comprising wooden wall and base units and with tiled work surfaces over. Stainless steel one and a half bowl sink drainer. Tiling to walls. Electric oven and gas hob. Plumbing for washing machine and space for fridge. Windows.

Dining Room 11' 4" x 9' 9" ( 3.45m x 2.97m )
Feature sash window to front aspect and door leading to the side of the property. Wall lights, storage cupboard and feature built in shelving. Radiator.

First Floor

Cloakroom
Window to side aspect. Low level WC. Wash hand basin. Tiling to walls and radiator.

Living Room L-Shaped Room 15' 2" x 12' 6"9' 6" x 5' 11" (4.62m x 3.81m2.90m x 1.80m )
Window to front and side aspect. Door to balcony with space for table and chairs, perfect for al fresco dining and with glorious views over the river. Feature fireplace with electric fire and exposed brick surround. Radiator.

Bedroom One 12' 2" x 9' 5" ( 3.71m x 2.87m )
Sash window to front aspect. Built in shelving and feature ceiling beam.

Second Floor

Bathroom
Velux style window. Suite comprising bath mixer taps with shower over, wash hand basin and low level WC. Partly tiled walls and shaver point.

Bedroom Two 15' 11" max inc stairwell x 10' 4" max ( 4.85m max inc stairwell x 3.15m max )
Dual aspect windows. A room of restricted head height with exposed wooden beams and a range of built in cupboards. Radiator.

Bedroom Three 15' max x 11' max ( 4.57m max x 3.35m max )
Window to front. Exposed wooden ceiling beams and built in cupboards. Radiator. Restricted head height.

Front Garden

Private Garden
