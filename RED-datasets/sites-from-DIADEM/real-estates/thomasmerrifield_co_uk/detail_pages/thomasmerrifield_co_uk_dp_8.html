Newport Terrace, Bicester£275,000
On MarketThomas Merrifield Bicester - +44 (0) 1869 253253BIC0000384

An extended and improved Edwardian House in an excellent central location. The property which is just over 100 years old offers extended and improved accommodation over three floors.  On the ground floor there is a spacious living room, together with a well proportioned kitchen diner with double doors opening on to the rear garden.  On the first floor there are two well proportioned bedrooms with refitted bathroom, whilst on the second floor there is a further double bedroom. 

 

The property benefits from gas fired central heating and there is residents permit on-street parking.  No onward chain.
