Oxford Road, AbingdonGuide Price £525,000
On MarketThomas Merrifield Abingdon - 01235 538000AB00007062
A substantial and extremely handsome 4-bedroom Victorian semi-detached house in good decorative condition, with delightful larger-than-average well-established gardens, which offer a high degree of privacy to the rear—combined with a level of quiet, belying its proximity to the town centre.

This distinctive bay-fronted family residence provides generously proportioned accommodation, which is thoughtfully arranged and extremely versatile over its ground, first and second floors—with the addition of an roomy and dry basement/cellar with comfortable 6 ft headroom, which could be used as a family room or study.

There are lofty ceilings throughout, as well as great views from the top bedrooms. There is also the potential, if desired, for the pleasant kitchen/family room to be extended to the rear (subject to required planning consents)—in order to take full advantage of its splendid gardens.
