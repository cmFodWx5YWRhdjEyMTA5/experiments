Tumbling Bay Court, Henry Road, Oxford For Sale | £129,950
 
    
 
Views Over Botley Park, 1 Bed Ground Floor Apt. | 1 1  
 
Property Description
Pleasantly Situated Ground Floor One Bedroom Leasehold Apartment Enjoying Views Directly Over To Botley Park, & Located Within This Welcoming Development Aimed At the Over 60's. The Accommodation Comprises Of Hall, Living/Dining Room (With Door Leading Out To Communal Gardens), Double Bedroom (With Built In Wardrobes) Kitchen, Shower Room, Electric Heating, PVCu Dble Glazing, Communal Facilities, No Chain.

Tumbling Bay Court is a purpose built complex of apartments constructed by McCarthy & Stone in the early 1990's. Number 11 is situated on the ground floor with direct access out to communal gardens and views over to Botley Park. The development has a selection of both one and two bedroom apartments, all with 24 hour emergency 'Careline' system providing additional peace of mind. Residents of Tumbling Bay Court have the use of well tended grounds, parking, lift to all floors, laundry room with washing and drying facilities, guest suites for visitors, entry phone system and a large communal lounge. During the working day the resident house manager is on call. The required age of a single purchaser must be 60 years of age or over. If a couple would like to purchase, one partner must be at the minimum age of 60 years and the spouse 55 years and over. Tenure Leasehold. Council Tax 'C'
