Tumbling Bay Court, Henry Road, Oxford Sold STC | POA
 
 
 
One Bedroom Apt With A Southeasterly Aspect | 1 1  
 
Property Description
*Considering Downsizing? * A Welcoming Development Aimed At the Over 60's * Communal Entrance Hall * Facilities Including Lift To First And Second Floors * Hall * Sitting/Dining Room * Kitchen * Double Bedroom With Built In Wardrobes * Shower Room * Storage Heating * PVCu Double Glazing * No Onward Chain * Communal Parking And Gardens * The Apartment Is Set Back Away From The Botley Road * Generally Southeasterly Facing Aspect *


THE PROPERTY: Is a part of a purpose built complex of apartments constructed by McCarthy & Stone in the early 1990's. Number 37 is situated on the second floor with communal stairs and lift to the first, second and third floor levels. The property has an emergency alarm system providing additional peace of mind. Residents have the use of well tended grounds, laundry room with washing and drying facilities, guest suites for visitors and a large communal lounge. During the day the resident house manager is on call.

The accommodation is arranged as follows (all measurements are approximate).

Part glazed communal doors with entry phone system, opens to;

Communal Reception area with residents Lounge to one side, and access to other communal facilities such as the laundry room, House Manager's office, stairs and lifts.


HALL Coved ceiling, fitted smoke alarm (connected to the mains) emergency alert pull cord. Access to main accommodation through internal panelled doors.

LIVING/DINING ROOM 14' 7" x 11' 4" (4.44m x 3.45m) PVCu double glazed window has a generally west facing aspect. Coved ceiling, emergency pull cord, storage heater, telephone point (subject to regulations) and television point. Two wall light points. Walk in shelved storage cupboard housing the hot water tank and immersion heater. Archway leads to;

KITCHEN 7' 4" x 5' (2.24m x 1.52m) PVCu double glazed window. Coved ceiling. Range of floor standing and eye level units on three sides, with the wall units having both pelment and cornice finishing with lighting under. Easy to clean work surfaces extend over the base units and incorporating a single drainer stainless steel sink unit. Space for electric cooker (raised plinth and slot in electric cooker which maybe available subject to negotiation) and under counter space fridge/freezer. Extractor fan and hood. Tiled walls to three sides.

BEDROOM 8' 9" x 17' 7" max. (2.67m x 5.36m) Coved ceiling, double glazed PVCu window, storage heater, emergency pull cord, double fitted wardrobe. Wall light point. Telephone point.

SHOWER ROOM Coved ceiling, fully tiled, and offered with large shower tray Mira shower unit over, wash hand basin set into vanity unit, and low level WC. Wall mounted Dimplex electric room heater. Electric shaver socket. Extractor fan.

RESIDENTS: The required age of a single purchaser must be 60 years or over. If a couple would like to purchase one partner must be at the minimum age of 60 years and the spouse 55 years and over

TENURE Leasehold 125 years from 1st March 1990. This property is subject to a leasehold interest. Any intending purchaser is advised to consult their solicitor regarding any particular terms relating to this leasehold interest, including service charge, leasehold term, ground rent and building insurance etc. Council Tax 'C'
