Marlborough Court, Oxford Sold STC | POA
 
 
Delightfully Positioned, Views Over Playing Fields | 4  
 
Property Description
Beautifully Presented & Maintained Four Bedroom Home Positioned At The Very End Of This 'No Thru Road' & Enjoying 'Open' Views Out To King George's Playing Fields, Bulstake Stream & A Tree Lined Lane To The Front From The 1st Floor Balconies. Marlborough Court Offers Convenient Access To The Railway Station (Some 1 km Distance). The Accommodation Has Been Improved From The Original Five Bedroom Design With Quality Fitting Including Hardwood Floors (hall & main receptions), Double Glazed Hardwood Windows, Refurbished Kitchen (German Made Units), Double Glazed Conservatory & Luxurious En Suite Bathroom.

West Oxford is some 15 minutes' walk west of the heart of Oxford city and a few minutes from the busy intercity rail station. This area is located amongst several green spaces and canal/riverside walks. 'Modern' West Oxford, was built on the site of Osney's ancient Abbey, and now extends west across from the cottages of Osney Island. These short streets comprise of both Victorian, Edwardian and 1930s houses, all close to the open parks/green areas. The community supports a good range of shops, several public houses, allotments, an excellent primary school, local businesses and community centre.

Marlborough Court is a riverside development of 15 town houses constructed in 1990 by Martin Grant Homes. Number 15 is at the very end of the street with first floor balconies and windows enjoying views out St. George's Recreational Field & Bulstake Stream, a tributary of the River Thames. Purchased from new by the present owners, the house has been much improved including hardwood flooring to the hallway and main receptions, conversion of the garage into a utility, refurbishment of the kitchen with German made units, quality double glazed conservatory, hardwood double glazed windows, luxurious en suite bathroom with quality flooring, sanitary ware and fixtures, bright double aspect first floor living room, with south facing 6ft balcony overlooking St. George's Field, and further, 'Juliet' balcony to the front elevation. Additional fitted cabinets, wardrobes and book shelves have also been added as well. There is gas central heating with additional loft insulation installed. Externally there is off road parking for two vehicles, wide side gated access, plus neat, well maintained gardens to front and rear bordered by mature hedging. The rear garden measures some 41ft in length by 24ft in width. Rear gated access.
