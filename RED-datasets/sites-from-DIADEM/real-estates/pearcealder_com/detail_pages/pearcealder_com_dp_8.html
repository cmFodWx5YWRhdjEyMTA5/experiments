Harley Road, Oxford Sold STC | POA
 
 
3 Storey, 3 Bedroom House, West Facing Garden | 2 3  
 
Property Description
Modern Three Storey House Constructed Approximately 20 Years Ago & Forming Part Of A Row Of Similar Residential Properties Located On the Western Side Of This 'No Thru' Street. Harley Road Offers Convenient Access Both To The Railway Station, The City Centre Beyond, Plus There Is A Public Park At The End Of The Street. The Property Has First Floor Living Room With Dining Room & Kitchen (including Fitted Appliances), Sealed Unit Double Glazing & Gas Central Heating. Integral Single Garage With Off Road Parking To the Front.

Harley Road leads off the Botley Road, which is an important artery road affording access in and out of the city. The Botley Road offers convenient vehicle access onto the A34 ring road & A420 which in turn lead out to both the M40 and M4 motorways. Several bus stops are located on this road providing frequent access into the city centre, and also access to the mainline intercity rail station. West Oxford which includes Botley Road, is not far from several green open spaces and canal/riverside walks. 'Modern' West Oxford, was first built on the site of Osney's ancient Abbey, and now extends west across from the cottages of Osney Island and out along the causeway towards Botley itself, with the streets comprising of both Victorian, Edwardian and 1920'/1930s houses. The community supports a good range of shops, several public houses, a primary school, (secondary school in Botley), plus local businesses and community centre.
