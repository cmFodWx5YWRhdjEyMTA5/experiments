Harley Road, West Oxford For Sale | £439,950
 
    
 
Three Bedroom Home, Access To The Railway Station | 2 3  
 
Property Description
A Three Storey Three Bedroom Modern Home Offering Convenient Access To The Railway Station (within ½ mile distance) & Providing Handy Access By Car Onto The A34/Ring Road & The M40 Beyond. The Property Has An Integrated Garage And Off Road Parking For Two Cars & An Enclosed West Facing Rear Garden. The Arrangement Of Accommodation Briefly Comprises Of Hallway, Shower Room, Utility & Bedroom/Study On The Ground Floor, With 2 Receptions, Kitchen (Integrated Appliances) Bedrooms & Bathroom On The 1ST & 2ND Floors. Gas Central Heating. Harley Road Is A Short 'No Thru' Road Leading To Playing Fields.

Harley Road leads off the Botley Road, which is an important artery road affording access in and out of the city. The Botley Road offers convenient vehicle access onto the A34 ring road & A420 which in turn lead out to both the M40 and M4 motorways. Several bus stops are located on this road providing frequent access into the city centre, and also access to the mainline intercity rail station. West Oxford which includes Botley Road, is not far from several green open spaces and canal/riverside walks. 'Modern' West Oxford, was first built on the site of Osney's ancient Abbey, and now extends west across from the cottages of Osney Island and out along the causeway towards Botley itself, with the streets comprising of both Victorian, Edwardian and 1920'/1930s houses. The community supports a good range of shops, several public houses, a primary school, (secondary school in Botley), plus local businesses and community centre.

This property was constructed in 1997, and we are instructed to market the property on behalf of the original and only owners. The property benefits from gas central heating, sealed unit double glazing and with neutrally decorated walls throughout
