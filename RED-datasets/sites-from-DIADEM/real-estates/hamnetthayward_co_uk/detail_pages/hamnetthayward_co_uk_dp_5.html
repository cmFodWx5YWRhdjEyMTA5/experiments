Sold STCHamnett Hayward - Aylesbury Vale and Chilterns - 01296 62505000000552
A most attractive detached family home of quality in this extremely popular location. Since the house was finished in 2011 the current owners have had the roof space converted into a highly impressive bonus room suitable for a variety of uses. The house was built to an excellent design which blends in nicely with the established homes in the vicinity. The kitchen/dining/family room together with the play room/study are at the rear of the property in order to enjoy the outlook over the gardens. The sitting room is well proportioned with an impressive fireplace as the focal point. Upstairs two of the five bedrooms have en-suite facilities. The house is light, bright and airy and totally immaculate from top to bottom. The location is really convenient with the village centre and railway station all within an easy level walk.
