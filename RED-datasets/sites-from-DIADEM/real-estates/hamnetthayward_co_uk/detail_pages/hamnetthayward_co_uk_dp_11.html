On MarketHamnett Hayward - Thame - 01844 21537100001072
A very rare opportunity to acquire a stunning Grade II listed cottage that in more recent years has been lovingly renovated and restored to now provide character accommodation of the highest quality. Set behind the High Street with additional vehicular access off Bell Lane, ground floor accommodation includes a bespoke solid oak fitted kitchen with granite worktops and integral appliances, living room with stock brick working fireplace and separate reading area, a wonderful vaulted dining room complete with log burning stove and a guest bedroom being served by an adjoining shower room. On the first floor, the cottage features a vaulted master bedroom with extensive storage, a further double bedroom and a beautiful bespoke bathroom suite complete with double basins and thermostatic shower and under floor heating. Externally, Yew Tree Cottage has the benefit of vehicular access to the rear, which in turn leads to off street parking for at least three vehicles and a recently constructed self contained store and home office which again, is fitted to a very high standard. A particular feature of the cottage are the delightful high walled mature gardens offering a good degree of seclusion laid predominantly to lawn with two terrace areas. Yew Tree Cottage is an exceptional property situated in the most sought after location rarely seen on the open market.
