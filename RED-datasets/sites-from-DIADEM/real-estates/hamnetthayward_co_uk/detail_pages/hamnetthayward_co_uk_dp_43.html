Nestling at the foot of The Chiltern escarpment within an area of outstanding natural beauty, is The Cottage, Chinnor a beautifully presented three bedroom detached cottage enjoying spacious internal accommodation with a multitude of original features. Dating back to the XVIII century, the property has been extended in more recent years to provide a modern, yet traditional kitchen/breakfast at the rear overlooking the gardens and a recently re-fitted ground floor shower room. Of particular note is the substantial open plan reception room, extending to approximately 23' in length to offer versatile living with a multitude of exposed beams and a stunning open fireplace. The kitchen/breakfast room is fitted with a range of cream fronted units with timber work surfaces and again is a lovely room for cooking and entertaining with double casement doors leading onto a rear terrace and view into the garden. A formal dining/family room provides a private reception with exposed beams. To the first floor, a lovely spacious landing with exposed beams provides access to three pretty bedrooms and a family bathroom. The family bathroom is beautifully presented and has a classic roll top bath with ball and claw feet. Outside, at the rear are mature gardens offering an excellent degree of privacy enclosed with a lovely brick and flint wall. The gardens have three terraces for entertaining and gated access to the rear leading to a detached garage with private parking. This wonderful home is conveniently located on the fringe of the village, within close proximity of the Chiltern Hills providing delightful walks of the ridgeway and a large swarm of wildlife including the local Red Kites.
