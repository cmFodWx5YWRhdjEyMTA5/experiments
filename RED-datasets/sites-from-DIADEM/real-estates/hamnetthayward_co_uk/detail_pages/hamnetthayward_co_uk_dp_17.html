Sold STCHamnett Hayward - Thame - 01844 21537100000982
A stunning Grade II listed detached home steeped in history and enjoying a prominent position close to the town centre with fine views over the adjoining cricket ground and St Mary's church. Corner Close Cottage in the historic market town of Thame dates back originally in parts to the XVI century and has retained a multitude of character whilst enjoying beautifully presented accommodation. The current owners have extensively refurbished this wonderful home including a modern kitchen/breakfast room with range cooker, a delightful panelled formal dining room and an elegant sitting room with a cosy open fireplace. To the first floor, Corner Close Cottage has a substantial and very well appointed family bathroom, a vaulted landing with exposed beams and three double bedrooms. Particular mention is made of the master bedroom enjoying a double aspect with views towards St Mary's church and a lovely en-suite bathroom. Outside, the property enjoys a substantial area of off street parking to the front and direct access is available to the detached timber framed office/annexe. The office has power and light, views of the formal garden and an en-suite shower room. The remainder of the formal gardens encompass the property and have been particularly well maintained and manicured by the owners. Two large area's are laid to lawn at the front and rear with a number of flower and shrub borders and mature hedging. Located within a very short walk of the town centre, this lovely home really offers a very unique opportunity to acquire one of the very few detached period houses in the town with a generous plot and views.
