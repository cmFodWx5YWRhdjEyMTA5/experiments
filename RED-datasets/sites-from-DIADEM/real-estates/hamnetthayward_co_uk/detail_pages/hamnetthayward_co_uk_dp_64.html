Offering the very best of both worlds in a beautiful Chilterns setting, this property is a must see, only then can this amazing house and location be appreciated. With views spanning the Chilterns hills and Wendover woodlands, The lay out is spot on and functional. With a kitchen big enough for a family to eat in and a spacious, comfortable sitting room overlooking the enclosed rear garden. The sitting room itself is extremely light, with a substantial improvement being that the former conservatory roof has been changed to a solid ceiling whilst retaining the same outside appearance, it is significantly quieter and also easier to heat or cool – season permitting. On the first floor are two generous sized double bedrooms and a family bathroom whilst on the second you will find a further two equally generous double bedrooms including an en-suite walk in shower to the principal. This being an end of terrace house means you are indeed blessed with rather more garden than most, the gardens at both front and rear of the property are beautifully presented with lawn, patio areas & well stocked beds. This outstanding home offers style, value and with this location – it really is a fantastic place to live. In the immediate area you will find with plenty of open Green space with access to some of the most exquisite and spectacular walks in the Chilterns. There is a local ‘village’ store as well as an open play area for the children.
