A superb country residence located within a small picturesque rural village , enjoying outstanding formal gardens and panoramic views of open countryside and the Chiltern hills. Formerly a farm workers cottage dating back to the 1940’s, Farm Cottage, Aston Sandford has been considerably improved in recent years and now boasts substantial accommodation extending in excess of 2,000 sq.ft arranged over two floors, including a self-contained annexe. Internally, the property enjoys four reception rooms to the ground floor comprising a lovely sitting room with open fireplace, Dining room, study, kitchen/breakfast room and a bespoke garden room. A self contained annexe has recently been constructed to provide a sitting room, bedroom and shower room.
Outside, this attractive property is located within a most exquisite location, accessed via a single track ‘no-through’ lane and surrounded by lovely period homes and un spoilt countryside. Outstanding manicured gardens are located at the rear and are laid predominantly to lawn with a number of mature flower beds, the garden id bordered by an open post and rail fence to take full advantage of the wonderful outlook. Undoubtedly a peaceful and secluded retreat, the property is also conveniently located for access to Haddenham & Thame Parkway providing communications into London Marylebone in under forty minutes.
