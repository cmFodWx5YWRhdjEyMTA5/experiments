Sold STCHamnett Hayward - Thame - 01844 21537100001011
Located in one of the most sought after residential streets within this historic market town, is an incredibly stylish three bedroom home that has been both extended and refurbished throughout. Particular mention is made of the beautifully presented sitting room benefiting from a lovely cream painted wood burner, the recently replaced kitchen fitted in white high gloss units with timber work surfaces and a well appointed family bathroom with shower. Further accommodation includes a generous entrance hall fitted throughout with a lovely oak flooring, continuing into the dining room and sitting room. The family room is a recent extension, flowing from the dining room. To the first floor are three beautifully decorated bedrooms, a generous landing and a lovely modern bathroom suite, recently replaced in white and benefiting from a shower over the bath. Outside, the property enjoys a generous corner plot with large gardens to the front and side and a private rear garden with a westerly aspect to enjoy the late afternoon and early evening sun. The rear garden itself is enclosed and laid predominantly to lawn with a paved terrace for outside entertaining. A garage with off street parking is also located at the rear. Located within a short walk of the town centre this lovely home is made for a modern family with the many shopping facilities, schools and tennis club within immediate reach. With the generous corner plot this wonderful home also offers further scope to extend.
