On MarketHamnett Hayward - Thame - 01844 21537100001027
Situated in a superb location on the edge of Cuddington village, this wonderful Georgian home offers beautifully presented accommodation and lovely formal gardens adjoining open countryside. Extended and refurbished in recent years by the present owner, Mill Cottage offers a very unique opportunity to acquire a period house within this highly sought after village enjoying both a generous plot and panoramic views extending towards the Chiltern Hills and the outstanding village of Nether Winchendon. Internally, this fine home offers very versatile accommodation over two floors and particular mention is made of the wonderful sitting room with a wood burner and the elegant master bedroom providing outstanding views, stretching for miles and a wonderful modern en-suite. Further accommodation includes an extended entrance hall, kitchen/breakfast room, family room, study and utility room. To the first floor are three double bedrooms and two bathrooms, both re-fitted to exacting standards. Outside, the property is approached over a driveway and into a substantial area of off street parking, together with a generous detached double garage. Formal south facing gardens encompass the property to three sides and offer a wonderful outlook over adjoining countryside. The gardens are laid predominantly to lawn and a recently constructed stone terrace provides an ideal position for entertaining. A small detached outbuilding is located to the side of the property and offers scope for conversion into a home office or workshop. Mill Cottage offers a most unique opportunity to acquire a fine period house in both a highly sought after and picturesque village, within magnificent gardens yet within close proximity of Haddenham & Thame Parkway, providing immediate access to London Marylebone.
