A former Victorian school house, extended and refurbished to a particularly high standard and located within an exquisite setting off the picturesque village green. School Lane House in the quintessential village of Aston Rowant is a beautifully presented home enjoying elegant family accommodation arranged over two floors. Particular mention is made of the triple aspect sitting room, extending to 20’ in length and enjoying a fireplace housing a wood burning stove. Further accommodation includes a classic kitchen/breakfast room with stable door onto the rear, a formal dining room and a cosy snug/family room. To the first floor are four generous bedrooms comprising a master bedroom with en-suite shower room, a lovely guest bedroom, two further beds and a stunning family bathroom. Outside, the property is approached after circling the pretty village green and located tucked away along a single track lane providing complete privacy. To the front are attractive cottage gardens providing a seating area for morning sun and a driveway providing amplr parking in front of the double garage (planning permission has been previously granted to erect a timber framed double garage, but has recently lapsed). To the rear are stunning gardens laid to lawn with a generous terrace offering wonderful seclusion.
