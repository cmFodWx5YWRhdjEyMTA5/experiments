Sold STCHamnett Hayward - Aylesbury Vale and Chilterns - 01296 62505000000570
We have sold many of these bungalows over the years and they are clearly very popular. The reason of course is that they are ideal as they are but have huge potential to extend if you want, the choice indeed is yours. This particular example is an absolute cracker. In the last couple of years the kitchen has been re-modelled and the bathroom updated . The sitting room has an open fire to lend some cosy ambience and the views of the rear garden are really pleasant. Both bedrooms are good doubles. The carpets have been replaced and it has been decorated throughout recently. The whole place feels fresh, bright and clean. The extension potential is of course huge, some people just extend to the rear and add a conservatory, some convert the roof space, others have undertaken both. Outside the gardens are a real credit, well stocked and private with a nice sunny aspect. Needless to say, the big plus here is the location. with a leafy stroll to the railway station and the many specialist shops, popular restaurants and cafés in the unspoilt High Street.
