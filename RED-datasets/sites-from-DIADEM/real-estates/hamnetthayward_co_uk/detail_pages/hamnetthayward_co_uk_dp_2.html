On MarketHamnett Hayward - Aylesbury Vale and Chilterns - 01296 62505000000413
Bacombe Lane is one of those rare places that people tend to stay for many years. The current owners of Long Meadow have been there for over thirty years and many have been there for much longer. With an acre of grounds and an open aspect this home has a genuine sense of freedom and space. 1930's in origin Long Meadow has grown over the years with an extension and really super detached two storey coach house which has served as a games room for years but has obvious annexe potential. Light floods into the house and your eye is drawn to nearly every window with truly lovely views over the outstanding gardens and the countryside beyond. The living space is fluid with the two main receptions, adjacent a good sized study and fab kitchen flowing into a breakfast room with utility beyond. All four bedrooms are good and don't forget the potential for overspill in that coach house. A stroll over the field to the station, hop on the train and have a pleasant journey getting there on time and have a great house to come to.
