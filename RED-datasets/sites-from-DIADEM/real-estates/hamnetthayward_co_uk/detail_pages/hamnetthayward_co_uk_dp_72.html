Sold STCHamnett Hayward - Thame - 01844 21537100000947
Stylish modern day living within the heart of this historic market town, all within a beautiful Grade II listed building. This spacious two bedroom house within Michaelis Road is a stunning conversion within this historic building which was formerly a Victorian workhouse and in more recent years an agricultural college. Each unit has been carefully restored to provide unique and characterful accommodation, with a large twist of modern day living. Internally, 18 Michaelis Road offers particularly light and airy accommodation over both floors and has a unique panoramic outlook to both the lovely communal gardens at the rear and the attractive turrets to the front. Of particular appeal is the magnificent living room offering a dual aspect with views to the front and rear. The property is complemented by two bedrooms located to the first floor with a very well appointed bathroom enjoying a separate shower. Outside the property has private allocated parking to the front and the communal grounds to both the front and back are regularly maintained. Both the front and rear area's directly adjoining the property provide an ideal area for pots and a small level of planting. The rear garden has a paved area suitable for a table and chairs providing the ideal position for late afternoon sun with it's westerly aspect. So, if you are looking for spacious modern day living with oodles of character, a property with zero maintenance yet all within a very short walk of a beautiful historic market town, then this wonderful home is for you.
