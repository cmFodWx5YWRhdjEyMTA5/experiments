Sold STCHamnett Hayward - Aylesbury Vale and Chilterns - 01296 62505000000563
This is one of those imaginatively styled three bedroom mews homes on Hampden Hall. Hampden Hall is proving to be a very popular choice in recent years. The development is quite small with attractive designs and a good school catchment. The position of this house is really good, towards the end of the cul-de-sac and so quiet with very little traffic. The layout is up to date with the hub being the light and bright kitchen dining room supplemented by the double aspect sitting room with both a bay window and french doors to the garden. Upstairs the three bedrooms are all of generous size, with the principal bedroom being 19’6 in length with both an en-suite shower room and a dressing area. Outside the gardens are neat and private and there is both a garage and a covered driveway for parking. The surrounding countryside is criss-crossed with footpaths ideal for walking now the clocks have gone forward and of course the railway station at Stoke Mandeville is less than a mile away.
