Sold STCHamnett Hayward - Aylesbury Vale and Chilterns - 01296 62505000000569
This ground floor apartment is pure quality. The location is quite sublime with views of Wendover Woods. The setting has the feeling of an upmarket mews or stable yard. Very classy and stylish. You cannot fail to be impressed by the interior and there are windows all around the property meaning it is really light and airy. The room sizes are impressive and the quality of all the fixtures and fittings is evident. The kitchen which is open plan to the living room is elegant and restrained, very contemporary yet discrete. Directly adjacent to the living room is a piece of outside space 12’ x 4’ belonging to number 5 which could be paved. Its equivalent to a balcony on the upper apartments. Both bedrooms are generous doubles and the bathroom is absolutely as new. With nicely maintained communal gardens and two car parking spaces right next to the entrance, it really couldn’t be anymore convenient if it tried.
