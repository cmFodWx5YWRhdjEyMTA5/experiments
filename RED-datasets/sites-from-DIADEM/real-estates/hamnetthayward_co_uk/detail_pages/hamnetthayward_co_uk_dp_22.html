Sold STCHamnett Hayward - Thame - 01844 21537100001003
Situated in a superb location on the edge of an attractive Oxfordshire village, this unique property has three acres of land and stabling, whilst offering complete seclusion and uninterrupted views over glorious countryside, all within easy reach of Haddenham & Thame Parkway and the city of Oxford. Originally constructed in circa 1950, this predominantly single storey equestrian home has been extended in more recent years to provide generous accommodation, but also offers an opportunity to extend by adding further first floor accommodation. Alternatively, timber weather boarding fitted to the exterior would transform this property to create a barn style appearance in keeping with the surrounding area. The current internal format offers very generous and adaptable, open plan accommodation and of particular note is the superb sitting room extending in total to almost 30' and enjoying the outstanding views over countryside at the rear and a lovely wood burner for cosy winter evenings. Further accommodation includes a large entrance hall, kitchen/breakfast room with utility, dining room, a master bedroom with en-suite shower room, three further bedrooms and two bathrooms. Outside, the property is approached via a timber five bar gate opening to a substantial area of off street parking. The formal gardens encompass the property and two fenced paddocks are located at the rear and side. The equestrian yard has two separate stable blocks that also incorporate a tack room and hay store and there is also water to the paddocks. A detached garage/workshop is also located to the front of the property with power. This really is a wonderful opportunity to acquire a rural home offering complete seclusion and yet scope to further develop. NO UPWARD CHAIN
