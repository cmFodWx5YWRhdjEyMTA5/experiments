2 Wainwrights is a modern cottage style end of terrace home forming part of a small cul-de-sac development located just of the picturesque High Street. Originally built in the 1980's, the property in more recent years has been the subject of a modernisation program to now offer bright and airy contemporary living space. On entering the cottage, a modest hall with cloakroom provides access to a double aspect 23' living room with fireplace and a contemporary fitted kitchen/dining room with a good range of base and wall units and integrated oven, ceramic hob and extractor hood. On the first floor there are four bedrooms served by a bespoke Constable Phillips family bathroom complete with power shower. The interior has also been decorated to a high standard with attractive cottage style interior doors. Externally 2 Wainwrights is approached by a pretty open plan front garden whist to the rear the cottage enjoys a lovely secluded enclosed south facing garden that has been landscaped to include a patio area, generous flower and scrub borders and access to a single garage with the benefit of light and power.
