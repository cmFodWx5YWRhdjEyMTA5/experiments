1 Roundhill View is one of only four contemporary new homes forming part of private development located in a favored setting just of the picturesque Townsend Green. Constructed by the M G Partnership to a very high specification, the property features a wonderful 20' open plan kitchen/dining room with 'Kloeber' bi-fold doors which integrate seamlessly with the outside terrace. Offering clean lines the kitchen is fitted with a bespoke German 'Hacker' range of base & wall units with integrated 'Neff' appliances and Silistone worktops. The property also boasts a part vaulted living room with fireplace, unusually large reception hall, and the advantage of two additional reception rooms. First floor accommodation features a stunning master suite with comprehensive en-suite facilities and fitted wardrobes, guest bedroom with en-suite shower room and two further bedrooms served a generous family bathroom. Externally 1 Roundhill View features an open plan front garden and driveway with stylish Italian Cypress trees lining the development which in turn provides access to a garage with electric door. To the rear, and a particular feature of the property is the larger than average south-west facing gardens that have been professionally turfed with a large terrace leading off both the living room and kitchen. Offering very well planned accommodation, this brand new home offers an extremely high specification including under floor heating to the ground floor, ceramic tiled floors to kitchen/dining/utility/cloakroom and bathrooms, wiring for Sky Plus & FM connections, burglar alarm system and high levels of insulation creating a very efficient family home.
