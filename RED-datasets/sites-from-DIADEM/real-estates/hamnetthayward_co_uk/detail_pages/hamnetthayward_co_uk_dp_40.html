Sold STCHamnett Hayward - Aylesbury Vale and Chilterns - 01296 62505000000565
Situated in a sleepy no through lane this detached bungalow offers plenty of space and the potential to put your own stamp on it. The location is quiet and traffic free yet the village amenities of a small parade of shops and three very nice pubs are all within walking distance. The accommodation in the bungalow has a good balance between living and bedroom spaces . There is a very generous sitting room separated by an ornamental chimney breast from the dining room. As well as the kitchen there is also a breakfast room/utility. All four bedrooms are decent doubles and the bathroom is also spacious. The double garage is probably big enough to take a couple of Range Rovers or similar plus space for a workbench. This would suit a car enthusiast down to the ground. The gardens are manageable wrapping all the way round the property and offer a good degree of privacy without needing too much in the way of maintenance. For peace of mind the gas fired central heating boiler has recently been replaced and there are sealed unit double glazed windows and patio doors all round
