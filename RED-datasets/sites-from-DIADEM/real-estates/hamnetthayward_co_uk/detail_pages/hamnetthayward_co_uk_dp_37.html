A wonderful four/five bedroom detached village house enjoying a superb position at the end of a single track lane. Constructed originally in the 1960's, 20 Clifden Road has been extended in more recent years to provide substantial and adaptable internal accommodation with further scope to generate further first floor accommodation. The ground floor currently enjoys a practical layout, with the living room located on entering the property from the entrance hall and fitted throughout with a timber floor. The living room has a dual aspect to the front and an open fireplace for those cosy winter evenings. Further accommodation includes a superb kitchen/breakfast room measuring 18' and recently fitted with a range of hand made solid oak units with integrated appliances and lovely granite work surfaces. Access is gained directly into the garden from the kitchen and also has a separate utility room. Further accommodation to the ground floor includes a family room, shower room, four bedrooms and a family bathroom. To the first floor is a further bedroom or study. Outside, the property is located at the end of a single track lane with gated access into a generous area of off street parking with a detached double garage. There is an area laid to lawn at the front with flower and shrub borders and access is available into the rear. The rear gardens are extremely private and are laid predominantly to lawn with a range of flower and shrub borders, a generous terrace extends the width of the property at the rear. The gardens are enclosed with mature hedging and adjoin open countryside for that rural village environment.
