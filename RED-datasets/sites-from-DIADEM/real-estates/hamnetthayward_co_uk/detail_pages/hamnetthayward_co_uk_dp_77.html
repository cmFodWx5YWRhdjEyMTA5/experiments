Sold STCHamnett Hayward - Aylesbury Vale and Chilterns - 01296 62505000000545
These are some of our favorite homes. Plenty of space and character and in a fabulous location right next to Wendover Woods. The cottage is 1920's so you get those lovely gabled elevations and a very pretty look. The character feel is carried over on the inside with an attractive bay in the sitting room and a fireplace with it's gas fire providing the focal point. The room sizes are on the generous size with a decent sitting room, kitchen big enough to eat in and two super double bedrooms with fitted wardrobes and the bathroom upstairs. Outside there are gardens to both front and rear and the distinct advantage of off road parking around in the back. Perhaps the biggest attraction is the outstanding location close to Wendover Woods. You have direct access to miles of nature trails and footpaths with various picnic sites and even a cafe. I know several residents in this area who have even taken to mountain biking since moving in. The village centre with the railway station and the High Street renowned for its independent specialist shops and popular watering holes is only a mile or so away. With spring almost upon us what better time to buy a village home which by its location will encourage you to embrace the sort of lifestyle we all promise ourselves
