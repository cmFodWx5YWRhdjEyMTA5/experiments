The Snug Bar is a very stylish conversion of a Grade II listed former village Inn, providing spacious internal accommodation with a host of original features. Located within a quintressential Buckinghamshire village, this wonderful home is positioned within the heart of the village close to all amenities and the picturesque common boasting the famous windmill. Internally, the property has very well presented accommodation throughout and particular mention is made of the large open plan reception room, incorporating an open plan living and dining area with exposed beams and a bay window to the front. The kitchen extends to 18’ and is fitted with a range of cream fronted units with integrated appliances and a lovely natural stone floor. Accessed is also available directly onto the rear courtyard. To the first floor are two generous bedrooms, the master being vaulted with exposed beams and benefiting from a generous number of fitted wardrobes. A very well appointed bathroom with shower is also located on the first floor.
Outside, The Old Snug has communal landscaped gardens with a private courtyard providing an ideal position for entertaining and a path leads to an area of secure off street parking. The car park is separately accessed via a remotely operated gate leading from Windmill Street which in turn is only a ‘stones throw’ from the beautiful common and famous village windmill.
