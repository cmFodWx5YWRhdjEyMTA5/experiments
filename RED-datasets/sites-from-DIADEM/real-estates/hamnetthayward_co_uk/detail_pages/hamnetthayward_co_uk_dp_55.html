A well proportioned detached village house within large gardens, enjoying a highly sought after location within a small cul-de-sac just a short walk from Haddenham & Thame parkway. Constructed approximately forty years ago, Yolsum Close is a very pleasant residential street comprising just sixteen, three and four bedroom detached homes all differing in style to provide a perfect village location for both professional and family living. 16 Yolsum Close is currently a three bedroom detached property that has been extended in the past, yet offers superb scope to extend further and create a larger four bedroom home. The existing foot print provides three ground floor reception rooms that include a sitting room with fireplace, a formal dining room with sliding doors onto the garden and a separate study/office. Further accommodation includes a kitchen, cloakroom and entrance hall with entrance porch. To the first floor are three double bedrooms and a family bathroom. Outside, the property is located within a generous plot providing a substantial area to the front for ample off street parking, a double length garage and an area laid to lawn. To the rear, the formal garden is undoubtedly a wonderful attribute, extending to approximately 60' in length and laid predominantly to lawn with a mixture of mature trees and shrubs. The rear garden offers an excellent degree of privacy and a generous terrace is located from the dining room to provide an ideal area for entertaining. With the railway station within a five minute walk, together with the many amenities on offer in the village including three reputable schools, this really is the perfect environment to raise a family or commute into London.
