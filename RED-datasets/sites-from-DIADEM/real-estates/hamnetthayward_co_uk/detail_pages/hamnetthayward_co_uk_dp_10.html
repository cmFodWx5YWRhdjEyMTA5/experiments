Sold STCHamnett Hayward - Thame - 01844 21537100000898
A very well designed conversion of a period barn, enjoying stylish accommodation presented to the very highest standard. North Barn in the quintessential hamlet of Crowell is a stunning project completed just six years ago by Greyswood homes to form part of a very exclusive gated development of just five barn conversions formerly part of Manor Farm. Internally, this wonderful home enjoys very modern open plan accommodation and the vaulted reception hall provides a very impressive first impression and links the ground floor accommodation exquisitely. The kitchen/breakfast room is fitted throughout with all the very latest appliances with a black granite work surface with matching work station. The Living room is fitted throughout with a solid oak floor and double glazed, floor to ceiling bi-fold doors not only enjoy the breathtaking back drop, but also open onto a large terrace for outside entertaining. The landing is galleried and provides access to the luxurious bathroom and both the master suite and guest bedrooms. Outside, the property is approached via large electric gates and lead to a very private area of off street parking in front of a double garage. Above the garage is a generous room with an en-suite shower room accessed via an external staircase and ideal for a number of uses, including a home office or self-contained flat. Unlike the majority of barn conversions this superb property enjoys a very generous garden approaching 1/3 of an acre and beautifully landscaped abutting open countryside. As well as the main terrace, a private rear terrace is accessed from the kitchen providing complete privacy for morning coffee. Views are available to the rear and the picturesque Crowell church provides a gorgeous backdrop to the front.
