On MarketHamnett Hayward - Thame - 01844 21537100001013
Mulberry House is the flag ship design of the highly sought after development comprising of only thirty nine large detached family homes. The property offers spacious and adaptable family accommodation set out over three floors, with traditional red brick elevations, sash style windows to represent an attractive Georgian style. Internally a spacious hallway leads to bright and airy sitting room which has an open fireplace and attractive French windows opening onto the conservatory. The kitchen/breakfast area is practical and versatile fitted with a comprehensive range of units and integrated appliances, including a stainless steel range style cooker that now opens directly onto a wonderful 20' conservatory currently used as a family room. The first floor includes a master suite with bedroom, dressing area and en-suite bathroom, and two further double bedrooms sharing a well appointed family bathroom. The second floor guest double bedroom has a large en-suite shower room with a further room which can easily be used as bedroom five/family room/home office, or you can combine with the bedroom to create a self contained area for a teenager/elderly relative. The outside of the property benefits from a larger than average plot size with an attractive front gardens, ample off street parking, generous detached double garage with electric up and over doors and beautifully landscaped rear gardens laid mainly to lawn with well stocked boarders.
