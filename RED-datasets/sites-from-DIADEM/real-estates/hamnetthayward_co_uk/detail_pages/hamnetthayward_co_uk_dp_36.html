On MarketHamnett Hayward - Aylesbury Vale and Chilterns - 01296 62505000000527
An enchanting cottage in a magical sylvan setting. This is one of those homes that seems to appear in the market on a once in a lifetime basis. Applestraw Cottage enjoys the most delightful garden plot. Completely private with no noise at all save the sound of birds. It would be impossible to replicate the planting today with many rare established trees clearly planted many generations ago. The peace and dappled shade needs to be experienced first hand. We are almost certain that we glimpsed flower fairies from the corner of our eyes. The cottage itself is worthy of picture postcard status . It’s balanced and well proportioned. This is strictly one for the purist, one who understands that they are mere custodians , just one chapter in the long history of Applestraw Cottage. The previous owners were here for nearly fifty happy years and it is now time for someone else to provide a little sympathetic refurbishment and updating. You will love the warmth and character inside with all those lovely beams and a nice layout with four bedrooms upstairs and cozy separate receptions downstairs. The garage is in fact a completely clad 17th century cruck barn with all the lovely beams preserved inside . With its quiet location and peaceful and tranquil gardens of more than a third of an acre someone is going to acquire a little bit of heaven
