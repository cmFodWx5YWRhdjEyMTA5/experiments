On MarketHamnett Hayward - Thame - 01844 21537100001047
A wonderful opportunity to acquire a superbly designed, modern home recently constructed to an exceptionally high standard to provide very modern open plan and generous living. Arranged over two floors, the accommodation provides a lovely family home with a mix of traditional and contemporary style interiors and beautifully fitted bathrooms and kitchen. The ground floor comprises a light entrance hall with access to the reception rooms and fitted with an oak floor throughout. The substantial kitchen/breakfast room forms the hub of the house and provides a great family gathering area. The focal point of the room are the bi-fold doors opening out to the garden and therefore extending the living area, whilst bathing the room in natural light. The kitchen has a range of fitted units with integrated appliances including a dishwasher, a stainless steel range cooker and extractor, there is also a large island unit with a granite preparation surface. From the breakfast/dining area, doors open to the garden and accommodation flows into the open plan family room with doors extending into the formal living room. The living room has an impressive bay window and extends to 25’ in length, whilst an open fireplace houses a wood burning stove. At first floor level are four exceptional bedrooms and three bathrooms, the master has views over the lovely formal gardens, built in wardrobes and an impressive en-suite wet room. The guest suite is also impressive with an en-suite shower room and the family bathroom is very well appointed serving the two other double bedrooms.
Outside, this wonderful home benefits from a substantial plot including a large area of gated off street parking to the front, a double garage and glorious formal gardens to the rear extending in excess of 100’. The rear gardens are laid predominantly to lawn with mature plants, shrubs and trees throughout, with a large storage shed and summer house. A generous vegetable garden is also located at the bottom of the garden and a large terrace is ideally located directly from the kitchen to provide a wonderful area for entertaining. This is a wonderful garden for children with an area of lawn for a small football pitch and even your very own miniature railway with track extending to form a loop around the garden.
In our opinion this really is a very impressive family home and a viewing is highly recommended to appreciate the space on offer, coupled with the magnificent gardens and close proximity to Haddenham station for access into London Marylebone.
