Sold STCHamnett Hayward - Thame - 01844 21537100001038
A bright and airy three bedroom mid-terrace house constructed in the 1970’s, that in more recent years has been updated to now provide modern accommodation over two floors. Located in the picturesque High Street accommodation includes an entrance hall with cloakroom, a lovely living room complete with log burner and a modern fitted kitchen/breakfast room with integrated dishwasher and double doors opening onto the rear terrace. On the first floor the property features a spacious 14.5” master bedroom with two windows to the front aspect, two further bedrooms and a modern bathroom suite.
To the rear of the house there is a split level enclosed garden offering a south easterly aspect which has been laid mainly to patio with raised flower and vegetable beds. In turn the gardens lead to a single garage with light and power and metal up-and-over door providing vehicular access from the service road leading off the High Street.
The property also offers potential to extend into the loft space creating a bedroom and a bathroom (*subject to the usual planning constraints)
