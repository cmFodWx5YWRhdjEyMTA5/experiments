Sold STCHamnett Hayward - Thame - 01844 21537100001054
A very well presented three bedroom village house with garage and parking, enjoying a prime position within the village just a short walk from the famous windmill and picturesque common. Constructed circa 1970, this lovely property has been comprehensively updated throughout and has been extended in more recent years to provide an open plan kitchen/dining room, opening directly out onto the rear garden. The ground floor arrangement is both practical and convenient for modern day living with the kitchen/dining room serving as the hub of the house with a separate sitting room located at the front. The kitchen itself is very modern and fitted with a comprehensive range of shaker style units and fitted with a range of appliances including electric ovens, a hob, fridge/freezer dishwasher, washing machine and tumble drier. The first floor accommodation comprises three bedrooms and a recently refitted shower room. The master bedroom is a good size and has a range of fitted wardrobes. Outside, the property has small but very well manicured gardens to the front and rear. The rear garden is a pretty cottage garden with well stocked raised flower beds and a generous paved terrace providing an ideal area for entertaining. A personal door is available at the rear for access into the garage and has power, light and further parking to the front. Windmill Street is a lovely road littered with period property which in turn is only a stones throw from the beautiful common and famous village windmill. NO UPWARD CHAIN
