PLEASE CONTACT OUR OFFICES IF YOU WOULD LIKE AN APPOINTMENT TO VIEW......With a stunning rear garden this 1950s semi detached family home on the edge of Wendover offers huge scope to extend subject to planning. Currently there are two separate reception rooms and a good sized kitchen with three decent sized bedrooms and a first floor bathroom. It seems like everyone in Lionel Avenue is extending at the moment, looking back at the last five houses that we have sold in this lovely road they are either already extended or being extended as I write! You can see why though, the plot sizes are great and it’s one of the most popular addresses in the village. Having said that just because a house has scope doesn’t necessarily mean that it has to be done. The accommodation works just fine as it is with great room sizes and many improvements throughout which have recently been carried out by the current owners. There are UPVC windows and a feature fireplace in the sitting room with its living flame gas fire which adds a nice cozy touch. The dining room opens out onto the rear garden and the kitchen is big enough to eat in. The rear garden is private and secluded with a further secret area at the bottom, ideal for fun with the nippers or perhaps growing fresh vegetables or even keeping a few chickens. Bask in afternoon sun and enjoy lovely sunsets. A great house in a great location
