On MarketHamnett Hayward - Aylesbury Vale and Chilterns - 01296 62505000000568
This is a stunning rural home with a view so diverse it’s breathtaking. Of hills and a distant church of fields and woods. It could have been painted by a fantasy artist. Donkey Hall is situated at the end of a track. It’s an attached cottage originally built as a folly to the main house which has evolved over time into the quirky and delightful gem that you see before you. The eclectic mix of styles are truly refreshing. Both main reception spaces are large enough to impart a touch of grandeur yet are somehow cozy and intimate at the same time. The master bedroom has a balcony from which to enjoy your Sunday brunch. The second bedroom used to be two and could be divided with ease. The gardens extend to almost an acre – unheard of in a property of this size. There are so many fruit trees you can be self sufficient if you bottle and preserve. What an idyllic place to raise children. What is scarcely believable is that all this is within a few minutes walk of a railway station! Why not spoil yourself with this exuberant period home in its own piece of paradise.
