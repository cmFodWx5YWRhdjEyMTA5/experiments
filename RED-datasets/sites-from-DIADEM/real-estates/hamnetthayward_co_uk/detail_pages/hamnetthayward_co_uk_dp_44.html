On MarketHamnett Hayward - Aylesbury Vale and Chilterns - 01296 62505000000526
A lovely detached Victorian villa with over field to the front and the Parish church as a back drop. With lofty ceilings sash windows and open fires this charming home has lost none of its inherent character. There is an abundance of light as many of the rooms have a double aspect. The focus of the downstairs is the super eat in kitchen and there are three good reception rooms. All four of the bedrooms are doubles and there is a large bathroom together with a separate shower room. Outside the gardens are lovely and private and well stocked. One touch we love is the original wrought iron and fence at the front. There is a barn to the rear providing a double garage, an office or garden room and extra storage. Hardwick is a great village. Very unspoilt and picturesque with good walking all around. The local village pub, The Bell, has recently refurbished throughout and serves excellent locally sourced gastro pub food. I'm sure you will be pleased to note that it is in easy walking distance from Sunnybank.
