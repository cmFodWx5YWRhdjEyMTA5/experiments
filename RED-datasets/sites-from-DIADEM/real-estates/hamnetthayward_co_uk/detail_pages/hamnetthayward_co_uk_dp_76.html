Sold STCHamnett Hayward - Aylesbury Vale and Chilterns - 01296 62505000000548
Fabulous location large garden and masses of potential. This one of those super 1920’s traditional homes with plenty of space and character. Many improvements have been carried out but there is further scope and also potential to extend subject to planning permission. The garden is enormous by modern standards – I suppose growing your own was the norm ninety years ago. The garden needs a bit of taming but what a great fun project – a lovely blank canvas. Downstairs you have a front to back sitting room, a big kitchen /dining room and a cloakroom. There are three decent bedrooms and a first floor bathroom. Outside there is off road parking for several vehicles on a large tarmac frontage with a proper dropped curb. With a large rear garden there is of course the potential to put up one of those super garden cabins that give you that extra space at minimal cost . This is a fine character home with space both inside and out and the capacity to put your own mark upon it
