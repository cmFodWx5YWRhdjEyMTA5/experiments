Sold STCHamnett Hayward - Thame - 01844 21537100001004
A superb family house located within this wonderful village, tucked away along a quiet single track lane close to open countryside and enjoying generous well stocked gardens. Internally, the current owners of Greystones have remodeled and refurbished this fine home to provide spacious, adaptable and well presented accommodation arranged over two floors. The kitchen has recently been updated and fitted with a range of high gloss units with a central island providing the hub of the house, flowing through to the dining room and entrance. Glazed doors and windows enjoy both a view of the stunning garden and also bathe the kitchen in naturally light. Further accommodation to the ground floor includes a large utility, a cloakroom, a formal Sitting room with fireplace and a generous family room. To the first floor are five bedrooms in total, comprising a master bedroom with a modern en-suite shower room and four generous bedrooms together with a well appointed bathroom. Outside, a particular feature of this wonderful home are the generous gardens encompassing the property. To the front is a substantial area of off street parking with an attractive oak framed garage block with single garage and car port. Access is available into the large rear gardens, laid predominantly to lawn with mature planting to provide an excellent degree of seclusion and a fabulous paved terrace for entertaining. Properties within Chilton village are rarely available on the open market and we would recommend an early inspection to appreciate both this wonderful home and stunning village.
