On MarketHamnett Hayward - Thame - 01844 21537100001041
A recently constructed four bedroom detached family home forming part of a select development situated on the fringe of a highly sought after Buckinghamshire village, enjoying magnificent views over open countryside to the front and a footpath with direct access to Haddenham Thame Parkway. Internally this well appointed home offers modern open plan living arranged over two floors and a particular attribute is the superb kitchen/breakfast room, extending to some 25' in length and fitted with white 'high gloss' cupboard and drawer units. Further accommodation to the ground floor includes a study/family room, a formal sitting room, cloakroom and utility. To the first floor are four generous bedrooms, the master enjoying a wonderful outlook over countryside to the front, built in wardrobes and an en-suite shower room. Three further bedrooms and a family bathroom complete the first floor. Outside, parking is located to the front of the garage, adjacent to the house and enclosed gardens are laid predominantly to lawn at the rear with a southerly aspect. The property is ideally positioned on the fringe of the development to take advantage of uninterrupted views over countryside, gated access is also conveniently available onto the open space designed around a duck pond and available for recreational use and dog walking. A footpath also provides direct access to Haddenham & Thame Parkway for trains into London Marylebone in just 37 minutes.
