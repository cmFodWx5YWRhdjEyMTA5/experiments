Sold STCHamnett Hayward - Thame - 01844 21537100001025
Located within a short walk of the town centre and enjoying views of St Mary's church to the front, 4 Queens Close, Thame offers a unique opportunity to acquire a lovely three bedroom home that has been both extended and refurbished throughout. Constructed during the 1950's this mature three bedroom family home offers beautifully presented accommodation over two floors and has been extended to provide a further reception room, utility room and cloakroom. The Kitchen has also been re-modelled to provide a stunning open plan kitchen/dining room with a range of integrated appliances. The first floor has three lovely bedrooms and a recently replaced shower room. Outside, the property benefits from a large corner plot providing further scope to extend and large gardens to the front and rear. The rear garden itself is laid predominantly to lawn and has an area of off street parking to the front of the garage. A lovely sunny terrace is located at the side of the property and provides an ideal area for entertaining, enjoying the late afternoon/early evening sun. Located within a short walk of the town centre this lovely home is made for a modern family with the many shopping facilities and schools within immediate reach. With the generous corner plot this wonderful home also offers further scope to extend.
