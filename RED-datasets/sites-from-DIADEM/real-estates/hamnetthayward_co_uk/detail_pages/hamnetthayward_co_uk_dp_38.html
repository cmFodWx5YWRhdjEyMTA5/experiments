Sold STCHamnett Hayward - Thame - 01844 21537100001030
A modern four bedroom link-detached family home located within a short walk of the bustling High Street yet situated in a quiet residential backwater. Originally constructed in the 1980's, in recent years the property has been subject to a major renovation programme to now offer wonderful contemporary open-plan accommodation finished to a very high standard. Particular features of note include the modern high gloss fitted kitchen with extensive range of base and wall units and separate dining area, bright and airy double aspect living room with patio door leading to the rear terrace and gardens and the advantage of a ground floor cloak room. On the first floor the master bedroom features a modern en-suite shower room with the three remaining bedrooms being served by a family bathroom with separate thermostatic shower. Two Mitchell Close also features a single garage with light and power, off street parking and a larger than average rear garden laid mainly to lawn with natural stone terrace, original stone wall and a surprisingly open aspect for a town centre property.
