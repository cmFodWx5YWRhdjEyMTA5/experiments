Sold STCHamnett Hayward - Thame - 01844 21537100001061
A most attractive five bedroom detached family home with accommodation extending to 2,500 sq.ft* to form part of this highly sought after development within the grounds of a Grade II listed former Victorian workhouse just a short walk from the picturesque town centre. Loch Leven is a very well designed home for the modern family, boasting stylish open plan accommodation to the ground floor and five bedrooms to the first floor that includes two principle bedroom suites. The stylish kitchen/dining/family room extends to approximately 23’ in length and is fitted with an extensive range of integrated appliances with painted units in white and black granite work surfaces. The remainder of the ground floor comprises of a large entrance hall that opens directly into a study area, cloakroom, utility room and sitting room. Should further ground floor accommodation be required, the necessary permissions have been obtained to convert the double garage into an additional reception and create a single car port. Further details on request. The first floor generally comprises of five double bedrooms but notably has two bedroom suites, the master enjoying a ‘Juliet style’ balcony overlooking the rear garden.
Outside, the property enjoys off street parking to the front and a double garage. Formal gardens extend to two sides of the house including a paved area to the rear. The gardens are laid predominantly to lawn and enclosed with a combination of post and rail fencing and close board panel fencing. Located within a short walk of the town centre this lovely home is made for a modern family with the many shopping facilities, schools and sports clubs within immediate reach.
