Sold STCHamnett Hayward - Aylesbury Vale and Chilterns - 01296 62505000000571
This is a totally immaculate detached home in Haglis Drive which must be one of the most pleasant places to live in the village. The close is quiet and the outlook is peaceful and pleasant. Presentation is superb and it is a real credit to its very house proud owners. You will love the light and airy feel in the sitting/dining room which leads into the conservatory – a great place from which to enjoy your handiwork in the south facing rear garden. The kitchen has been refitted in a tasteful shaker style. There is access into the garage which would lend itself to conversion into another reception room and also a utility. It’s all there, it would just need converting and some building regulation approval. Upstairs there is a smart refitted white bathroom and three double bedrooms. All three bedrooms have fitted wardrobes. Outside there is oodles of driveway parking. The garden is a real joy. A nice size, perfect aspect, private and well stocked. Manicured throughout. All you have to do is maintain as all the hard work has been done. The house is one to literally move into and not need to do anything except decide where to place your furniture and enjoy.
