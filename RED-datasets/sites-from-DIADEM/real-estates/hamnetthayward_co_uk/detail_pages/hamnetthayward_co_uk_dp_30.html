This property is one you could move straight into without needing to do a thing. The layout is genuinely flexible with a bedroom and en-suite both upstairs and downstairs you can choose exactly how you want to live. The current owners have improved this property beyond all recognition and have recently added a top quality conservatory from which to enjoy the lovely garden. In all there are four separate reception rooms and three of the four bedrooms are good doubles. The double garage is integral and so potentially could become further accommodation. It would be a shame to lose the electric roller door though – I love those. We love this location because of the many lovely walks. The local reservoir is a nature reserve and also has a sailing club and you can walk along the canal to both Wendover and Halton. You can ride a bike to Stoke Mandeville station or hop on the bus and be in Wendover in 5 minutes. Perfection.
