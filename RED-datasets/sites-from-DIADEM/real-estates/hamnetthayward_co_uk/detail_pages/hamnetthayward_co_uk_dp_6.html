SPACIOUS FAMILY HOME WITH PADDOCK - A beautiful period house offering substantial character accommodation with seventeenth century origins located in the picturesque High Street. In more recent years the property has been both extended and modernised to now offer spacious living accommodation including a large reception hall, stunning drawing room, separate informal sitting room, dining room and a delightful modern kitchen/breakfast room with contemporary fitted units, granite worktops and an Aga. The first floor includes a lovely master suite overlooking the rear grounds including a dressing room and large en-suite shower room. In addition there are three further double bedrooms and an Attic bedroom 5. Externally the property benefits from a large private gated drive with space for numerous vehicles that in turn leads to beautiful mature gardens laid mainly to lawn with well stocked borders and specimen trees including: a mulberry, quince, walnut and selected fruit trees. The gardens offer a very good degree of seclusion with views over adjoining farmland, and in turn provide access to an enclosed paddock of about 1.4 acres. The Old Nelson offers a rare opportunity to acquire a generous family home offering a wealth of character including a number of open fireplaces, exposed beams and latched doors.
