PLEASE CALL THE OFFICE FOR AN APPOINTMENT .....This is a really good village home. It is steeped in local history having been the village post office in the 1950s. You can enjoy the best of both worlds here with character features contrasted with sleek modern presentation and tasteful pastel colours. You will be impressed by just how light the house is. The sitting room/dining room is bright and spacious whilst the kitchen is practical and well fitted. Upstairs there are three bedrooms, all with a lovely aspect and a contemporary white bathroom with bath and a separate shower cubicle. The outlook to both the front and rear are excellent with views over fields to the rear which is a conservation area, and to the front the property is well set back with pretty cottages and village homes making a really appealing street scene. The current owners have made numerous improvements during their ownership to enhance the appearance, security and energy efficiency of this fine home. The property benefits from a newly built 24ft detached garage/workshop which is supplemented by driveway parking for a good number of vehicles. The rear garden which faces near enough due south is a fine place to relax and spend time and to enjoy the country views.
