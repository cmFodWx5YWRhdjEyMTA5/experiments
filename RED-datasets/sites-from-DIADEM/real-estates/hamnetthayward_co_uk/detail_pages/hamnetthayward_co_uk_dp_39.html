Sold STCHamnett Hayward - Aylesbury Vale and Chilterns - 01296 62505000000522
This pretty cottage is in an enviable rural location with stunning views over the Chequers Estate and stands in grounds of nearly half an acre. Muircroft has been extended and reconfigured to take full advantage of the outlook both front and rear. We were really impressed by the kitchen/dining/family room which overlooks the landscaped rear terrace and gardens beyond. Flooded with light from it’s large windows and well over thirty feet in length this is exactly the sort of space vibrant modern families crave. The separate sitting room to the front by contrast has a real aura of peacefulness helped to a large extent by the timeless sylvan view. Upstairs all the bedrooms are well proportioned with a stylish en-suite shower room to the principal bedroom and a smart family bathroom fitted to the same standard. The garden. If outside space and gardening are your passion then look no further. To the rear is a wide paved terrace with raised beds and a delightful water feature providing gentle soothing sounds the perfect outside entertaining area ideal for those large family parties. Beyond is an area of lawn and then widening into a small paddock . There is boundless potential for growing vegetables and you could even keep chickens or a Shetland pony. In terms of location accommodation and outside space this is genuinely one of the best combinations we have seen for some time.
