WHAT A STUNNER.....This is a warm and welcoming traditional period village home. Appleacre has classic Georgian lines. When you step inside you will love the delightful atmosphere and sheer originality. There are lovely quarry tiles and inviting open fireplaces of hand made brick. The layout works well for families with separate spaces with the all important hub being the comfortable vaulted kitchen and breakfast room which opens onto the stunning rear garden. All four bedrooms are good with the en-suite to the principal and a lovely family bathroom. Houses of this age are not usually noted for their parking but with a double garage and parking for four or five on the driveway that’s definitely not an issue here. So step back in time in this warm and welcoming village home which is un-spoilt and full of fabulous features.
