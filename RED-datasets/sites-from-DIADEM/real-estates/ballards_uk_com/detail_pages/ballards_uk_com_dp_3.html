Brief Description

A ground floor retail premises forming part of an attractive Georgian building located in the centre of Henley on Thames. Approximately 1200 sq/ft (112 sq/m). Rent is subject to VAT.

Full Description

Description

An attractive retail premises, forming part of a Grade II listed building, located in Hart Street, Henley on Thames being close to the central traffic lights and short walk from the River Thames.

The Premises comprise: of double fronted sale room to the front (approx 300 sq/ft) with arch leading to a rear sale room (200 sq/ft) with access to a shared rear courtyard. Two further sale rooms (150 sq/ft and 240 sq/ft) are located to the rear with windows over looking the courtyard. There is a walk in store of the rear sale room plus a further store room/kitchen area of approximately 120 sq/ft which is currently divided into two with a stud partition wall.

PLEASE NOTE THAT THE BUILDING IS VAT ELECTED AND THEREFORE VAT IS PAYABLE ON THE RENT.

Terms:
New Lease to be granted.
Repair responsibility. Effective Full Repairing and Insuring.
Rent payable quarterly in advance plus VAT.
Deposit: Atleast one quarters rent or more, subject to approval of references by the Landlord.
References: The Landlord will require the following references: Bank, accountant, current/previous Landlord references and two further trade references.
Legal fees: Each party will be responsible for their own legal costs incurred for granting a lease.

Location

The premises are located on Hart Street, a busy thoroughfares leading from Henley bridge up to the central market place.   Neighbour premises include Foam and Rive Gauche (Ladies Fashions) Cafe Rouge (Cafe Bar) and The White Garden Florist as well as Zizzi's resturant, the Catherine Wheel hotel, and Barclays Bank. The main public car parks in Greys Road and Kings Road are both within a 5 minute walk with longer term parking available at Greys Road and the railway station (approximately 10 minutes walk).

Directions

The premises are located on the right hand side as you head up Hart Street from the river bridge.
