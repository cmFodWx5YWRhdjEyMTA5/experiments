Brief Description

First Floor office suite of approx 1121 sq.ft (104 sq.m) forming part of an attracive listed building, located in the centre of Henley on Thames within a short walk of the shops, River Thames and Railway station.

Full Description

Description

A first floor office suite comprising of three rooms (over looking Friday Street) at the front of this attractive Georgian period building.
Communal entrance with security entryphone system. Gas fired heating via radiators.
Cloakroom facilities are available on each floor.
Available to rent of short term flexible licence or longer term lease if required.
Some onsite parking is available for small cars on a strictly first come first serve basis.
Further terms on applications.
Service charge will apply for utilities, insurance and building maintenance and running costs.
All inclusive rental terms available.

The premises are currently undergoing refurbishment as suites will become available from December 2013. Further space available on the ground and first floors.

Location

Situated in a side road off one of the main thoroughfares in Henley on Thames just a short walk from the River Thames, railway station the excellent variety of shops, cafes, bars and resturants.
There is all day parking available in Greys Road and at the railway station (permits available) as well as short term pay and display parking also in Greys Road and in Kings Road (All within a 5 to 10 minute walk.

Directions

From our office close to the bridge in Hart Street, Henley, turn right into Riverside and at the junction turn right into Friday Street. Continue up Friday Street 35 is on the right hand side just before the no-entry signs
