A stunning and unique property with flexible accommodation across more than 4500 sq ft. Upon entering the Barn, a beautiful feature oak staircase forms the main focal point of the entrance hall.

The main accommodation then comprising drawing room overlooking open countryside, dining room, contemporary styled kitchen by Siematic with steps then opening to a stunning oak conservatory, on the lower ground floor is a cinema and gym.

The first floor galleried landing then leads to the four bedrooms with the master and guest bedrooms both featuring ensuites.
