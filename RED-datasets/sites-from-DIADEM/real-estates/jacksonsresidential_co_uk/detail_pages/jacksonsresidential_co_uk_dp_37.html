The Property
Clare Cottage is a beautifully presented older style detached family home, enjoying a lovely secluded position. Originally built as two cottages and believed to date back to the mid 1800`s, the property has undergone much improvement and refurbishment by the current owners and now enjoys four spacious bedrooms with two full bathrooms, two reception rooms together with a large bright family kitchen and utility room. The gardens and grounds are well maintained and extend to just under two thirds of an acre, enjoying fabulous westerly views across paddocks and attractively secluded adjacent woodland.

Situation
Stoke Row lies approximately six miles to the west of Henley on Thames, an attractive South Chilterns village which offers good village amenities including Post Office and General Store, garage and two public houses including the renowned ‘Crooked Billet` restaurant all within walking distance, as well as church and local primary school. The surrounding countryside affords many bridleways and footpaths offering a great variety for walking, cycling and horse riding. The village also boasts its own sports club and recreation ground and for a nominal family membership you can enjoy the benefits of 7 acres of park and woodland, two tennis courts, pavilion and cricket ground.
Directions
Leave Henley on the A 4130 Wallingford/Oxford road. Upon reaching Nettlebed, turn left at the roundabout and proceed into Highmoor, take the first turning just before the church towards Stoke Row. Carry on down into the valley then up the woodland road towards Stoke Row and the property is situated on the right hand side just past the Stoke Row village sign.
