Situation
Situated in the highly enviable Fisheries estate on the outskirts of the picturesque village of Bray, this unique property enjoys a suburban position on the banks of the River Thames. This individual property offers well-presented living space across two floors in a highly sought after area, close to Maidenhead`s entertainment amenities and transport links to London.

The Property
Downstairs, there is a large kitchen/ breakfast room with granite work surfaces and double doors giving access to the patio area. The ground floor of this property also benefits from family reception room and a downstairs shower room. Upstairs, there is scope for development as the flexible accommodation offers prospective buyers an opportunity to make this space their own. With an upstairs conservatory giving access to a balcony, this property takes full advantage of its panoramic riverside views, in particular from the impressive bay window in the drawing room. In addition to the master bedroom, with an en suite and built in wardrobes, there are a further five potential bedrooms, which are also suitable for use as additional reception rooms.

Externally, this desirable detached home has a driveway with ample parking and three garages one of which provides a utility room and useful storage. The rear of this property provides private mooring of approximately 50 ft and glorious views over the River Thames which can be enjoyed from the patio, the laid to lawn garden or from the built in swimming pool.

of The Avenue turning left onto Fishery Road where the property will be found immediately on your right hand side.
