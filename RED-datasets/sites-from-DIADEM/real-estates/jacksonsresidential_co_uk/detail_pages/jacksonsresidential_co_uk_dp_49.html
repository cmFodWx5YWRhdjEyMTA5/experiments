A substantial country house, superbly appointed with flexible family living together with leisure facilities, all set in gardens and grounds which extend to approximately 6 acres.

Situation
Heathwood is situated in the Hambleden Valley, one of the most desirable addresses in this part of Oxfordshire, within a stunning country setting and with easy access to the River Thames at Hambleden Lock. There is easy access to Henley-on-Thames, Marlow, Maidenhead and Reading with regular train services to London. The M40 J4 and the M4 J8/9 are both within easy access via the A404, providing access to London Heathrow.

Accommodation
The ground floor accommodation comprising a sitting room, dining room, study and snooker room along with a kitchen/breakfast room which is superbly appointed and opens into a conservatory affording views over the gardens. There are three bedrooms and two bathrooms (one en-suite) plus a further study/bedroom. There is a further staircase to the south end of the house which leads to the magnificent part-panelled drawing room with windows to three aspects.

Also on the ground floor is a wonderful full-size snooker room and access to the music room, with double doors leading to the cinema/family room which in turn has access to a cloakroom. Also from the reception hall are doors leading to the magnificent indoor swimming pool with hot tub as well as a wet room, changing room and steam room: the room has Pulsar lighting together with an integral sound system and dehumidifier.

On the first floor, there is a galleried landing off which is a stunning master bedroom and en-suite bathroom. There are two guest suites, together with a gymnasium and a galleried landing overlooking the swimming pool. The annexe is to the north end of the main house, comprising sitting room, kitchen, bedroom and bathroom/w.c.

Outside
The property is approached through electrically operated gates leading to a gravel driveway which leads in turn to the double garage. The main gardens are to the rear of the house where there is a large terrace. The gardens are mainly laid to lawn raised beds and woodland areas. There is an orchard and a small copse adjacent to the all-weather tennis court. The paddock is surrounded by post-and-rail fencing and hedging.
In all the grounds extend to approximately 6 acres.
Directions
Leave Henley on the A4155 towards Marlow. Upon entering Mill End turn left signposted to Hambleden, after approximately 200 yards turn right towards Rotten Row. Proceed up the lane and after approximately 1.9 miles the entrance to Heathwood will be found on the right hand side.
