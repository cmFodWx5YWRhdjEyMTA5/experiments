Situation


Shiplake train station and the amenities of the village are a short walk away. Shiplake is a very popular village with a range of amenities including a post office and shop, butchers, pub and very well regarded primary school. The house is located on the Thames Path, a short walk from Shiplake Lock offering delightful riverside walks to Henley and Sonning. Details of the Thames Path around Shiplake can be found on:

The railway station provides a direct service to London Paddington (42 mins.) and Henley is 5 mins. away by train. The commuter is serviced by both the M4 (J8/9) and M40 motorways approximately 8 miles.
