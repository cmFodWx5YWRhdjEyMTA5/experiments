HENLEY-ON-THAMES, Oxfordshire

UNDER OFFER £239,000



Please click an image to enlarge


* Porch * Two Double Bedrooms * 16' x 11' Living Room * 11' x 8' Kitchen/Breakfast Room * Bathroom * Gas Central Heating * Double Glazing * GARAGE * Front Garden * Long Lease *

A spacious two bedroom first floor maisonette, set in a tranquil cul de sac location, close to local shops.

Situation


The property is located in a tranquil spur off the main development. Local shops are only moments away and the comprehensive shopping facilities of Henley Town Centre are nearby and include a wealth of shops, bars and restaurants, two supermarkets, cinema, theatre as well as pleasant river walks and the railway station that provides a service to London Paddington (55 mins.). The commuter is serviced by both the M4 (J8/9) and M40 motorways approximately 7 miles.

Accommodation in Brief


From the entrance porch with a storage cupboard, a glazed front door leads into the hall with a staircase up to the main landing with two storage cupboards, one housing the gas central heating boiler, access to both double bedrooms with cupboards, family bathroom and into the light and bright living room. From the living room a glazed door leads into the kitchen/breakfast room.

Garden


Only to the front, open plan and laid to lawn with path to the front door.

Garage


Single length with up and over door and potential for storage within the roof joists. (First garage on the left hand side.)

Directions


From the centre of Henley proceed through the Market Place and turn left into and through Greys Road Car Park. At the end turn right onto Greys Road and continue up this road for about 1 mile. At the mini roundabout by the parade of shops turn right into King James Way. Follow the road around to the right and the property will be found on the left hand side.
