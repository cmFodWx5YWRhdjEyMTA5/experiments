The site has outline permission to be redeveloped to provide either both office or industrial buildings within two detached units. The buildings can be constructed to suit individual requirements to include adapting office contents and hi-tech style occupiers requirements. The buildings will be provided with good levels of car parking and lorry access. The proposal is to provide two units comprising the following; Unit 1: 5382 sq ft and Unit 2: 12,055 sq ft.

Directions

The site is within striking distance of both Oxford and Bicester which are approximately 9 miles respectively.
