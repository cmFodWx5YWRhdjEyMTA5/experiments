Enslow Wharf

Kidlington

Mixed offices and storage on self contained site with plentiful car parking. Short or long term lease considered.

Mixture of cost effective storage and office accommodation with plentiful external areas for loading and parking. The offices are mainly within a self contained portacabin style building with integrated WC's and kitchen facilities. The storage accommodation is seperated into three main bays with some ancillary covered storage accommodation linking two of the bays. The buildings are suitable for storage or production facilities.

Directions

The site is located in a prominent position within the hamlet of Enslow which is accessed directly from the A4095 and close to the village of Kirtlington. The site is within striking distance of both Oxford and Bicester which are approximately 9 miles respectively.
