Situated on an established and growing business park to the west of Ely with good access to the A14 and M11. The property comprises a detached, five bay building with car parking for approx 39 vehicles. Loading access is to 4 ground level bays to the rear of the unit. Eaves height of 6 & 6.5 metres. 2 office pods with admin, meeting rooms, staff rest rooms, wc's. with elec panel heating and perimeter trunking. B1, B2 & B8 planning uses.

Directions

Ely is a cathedral city aproximately 15 miles north east of Cambridge on the A10 and A142. Lancaster Way lies approximately 1.5 miles from Ely. Mainline rail services run from Ely to London Kings Cross.
