Unit 6 The Terrace

Harwell

Self contained two storey offices on established and expanding science/business park

Unit 6 The Terrace comprises a self contained two storey office building which benefits from the following: • Lobby entrance area • Suspended ceiling with integral Cat 3 lighting • Disabled WC and unisex WC • 6 car parking spaces • Raised access floors • Gas central heating

Directions

The Terrace is located within the Harwell International Business Centre which is Oxfordshire’s largest business and technology park. The Terrace is situated opposite the Diamond Synchrotron which is accessed off Fermi Avenue. Harwell Oxford is located off the A4185 less than a mile from the A34 Chilton junction providing excellent access to both the M4 (10 miles) and M40 (23 miles). A comprehensive public transport system serves the Campus with regular bus services to Abingdon, Didcot, Oxford and Wantage.
