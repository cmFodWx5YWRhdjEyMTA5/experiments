    THE PROPERTY
    An exceptional family home offering substantial accommodation in this sought after North Oxfordshire village, just a short walk from the village school. The property has been tastefully extended and improved to provide flexible accommodation on the ground floor with reception hall, lovely drawing room open through to a sun room with French doors to the garden to the rear and open to a family room/bedroom five to the front. The large beautifully fitted oak kitchen/dining room forms the heart of the house leading through to a most attractive sitting room with wood burning stove and French doors to the gardens. There is also a utility room, boot room and cloakroom on the ground floor. There is ample scope to divide the living space to provide a ground floor self contained annex if required. Upstairs there are four good size bedrooms, the master bedroom suite approached by it's own staircase is particularly impressive with a high vaulted ceiling, large dressing room and full bathroom. Outside there is ample off street parking and lovely private gardens.


    LOCATION
    Steeple Aston is a sought after North Oxfordshire village, set c.15 miles from Oxford City Centre. The village is built along a lane that runs round a valley with an orchard and grazing horses in it and is notable for its use of different coloured stone. Steeple Aston has a thriving and friendly village community and local facilities including a general store/post office, a primary school, a church and a pub which serves food. There is a railway station in the neighbouring village of Lower Heyford which provides a service to Oxford and London.
