    LOCATION
    Southmoor is situated c.6 miles to the west of Abingdon, just off the A420 and 10 minutes from the A34. It's an ideal location for access to the city of Oxford (9 miles), Witney (9 miles) and Swindon (20 miles).
    The village of Southmoor enjoys a pub, a country house hotel and restaurant plus village hall, primary school, post office and shops. Local clubs include a bowls club, tennis club and both a cricket and football club plus numerous other clubs and societies.
    A mainline station where London (Paddington) is reachable in c.40 minutes is available at Didcot.
