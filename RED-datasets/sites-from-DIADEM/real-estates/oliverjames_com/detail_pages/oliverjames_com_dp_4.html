    A truly spacious detached family home c.2700 SQ.FT (main house only) offering flexible accommodation throughout. The property is built within the grounds of the old Spring Road Hotel that was built as part of Isambard Kingdom Brunel's proposed railway link with Bristol. The Birches can be found at the end of a small private lane within walking distance of Albert Park and boasts six double bedrooms and a family bathroom, 20ft sitting room with adjoining family room and conservatory with its own grape vine as well as an 18ft dining room, spacious kitchen/breakfast room with attached utility room and downstairs bathroom.

    There is also a workshop and boiler room located on the ground floor.

    The main house does offer the opportunity to create a separate annexe facility if required.
    Outside the house there is a double garage with further parking for several vehicles, gardens that surround the house, summer house and outbuilding. The house is offered for sale with no onward chain.
