    THE PROPERTY
    Enjoying a pretty courtyard setting on the edge of this lovely stone village this an attractive period barn built of local Hornton stone in the 1820's and converted in 2002 to provide flexible living accommodation. The property comprises three separate dwellings, Pretty Bush Barn has three reception rooms and five ensuite bedrooms, Pretty Bush Cottage has three/ four bedrooms and three bathrooms and The Milking Parlour is a delightful one bedroom property. Pretty Bush Barn provides a fabulous opportunity as a business venture to provide Bed & Breakfast accommodation and holiday lets.


    LOCATION
    Pretty Bush Barn is located in the small stone built Oxfordshire village of Wigginton which lies between the "Ride-a-cock-horse" town of Banbury and Chipping Norton - the gateway to the Cotswolds. Legend has it that the Romans came across Wigginton in the 3rd Century AD and it is said that stones from a local Roman Villa were built into the present church during the 13th Century.
