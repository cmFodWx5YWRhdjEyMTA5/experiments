    LOCATION
    Ambrosden is a well placed village just c.3 miles South West of Bicester and c.13 miles North East of Oxford. Bicester North railway station provides a frequent train service into Oxford, London (Marylebone) and to Birmingham. The M40, junction 9 is also just c.4.5 miles away.

    Ambrosden has a Norman Church and a village pub which serves food. There are also other good local amenities with village shops including general stores, a post office, hair salon, garage and craft shop. The former school house is now the village hall and there is now a more modern building housing the Five Acres Primary and pre school. There are secondary schools in Bicester and preparatory schools at Ashfold, Oxford and Banbury and independent schools at Chesterton, Stowe and Oxford. Bicester provides extensive additional amenities including the shopping outlet, Bicester village and Aylesbury is also within easy reach.

    THE PROPERTY
    A well presented detached house built for the present owners in 2002 to a high specification to provide a large comfortable family home. The property offers substantial accommodation featuring a lovely bright sitting room with french doors to the garden and an open fireplace with double doors leading through to the dining room, again with french doors leading out to the garden. The kitchen/breakfast room is beautifully fitted and there is also a cloakroom and utility room on the ground floor. Upstairs there are five double bedrooms over the top two floors, with two bedrooms being ensuite. The bedroom on the top floor is particularly impressive being 35' in length making it an ideal bedroom and 'den' for a teenager. Outside there is a garage and additional parking on the driveway and a pretty garden enjoying a Southerly aspect.
