    SUMMARY
    An established four bedroom executive style detached family home situated in the popular village of Bampton. The property benefits from a mature plot with double garage and is situated in a quiet cul-de-sac of similar high quality properties.

    The property comprises; Entrance hall with ground floor cloakroom, living room, dining room, study, kitchen and utility.

    To the first floor are four bedrooms, an en-suite shower room and re-fitted family bathroom.

    Outside the property stands on a mature plot with private established gardens and a double garage.

    LOCATION
    The village of Bampton sits on the southern edge of the Cotswolds, around 18 miles south-west of Oxford, and 5 miles from Witney.

    Bampton is a wonderful mixture of the ancient and the new with many of its buildings being centuries old. It boasts an excellent array of amenities for a village of its size Including a Fire Station , Community Shop, post office, bank, library, five function facilities, a sports ground and pavilion, an art gallery, two churches, primary school, and a market square.

    The Recreation Ground offers many sports , Cricket , football , archery , tennis , netball and there is a floodlit multisport court. There are also thriving local Beavers and Brownies groups.

    For keen outdoor walkers there are numerous walks around Bampton's footpaths . Dog owners can walk around the grounds of the sports fields and the Millennium wood , where 18,000 trees have recently been planted.

    Bampton also offers a large number of groups and societies with a wide range interests from the West Oxon Arts Gallery, the Classical Opera , the Morris dancers , the Gardening club , the Zimbabwe group , the British Legion , the SPAJERS , who organise charity races around the village and many more.
