    THE PROPERTY
    A beautifully presented stone built cottage quietly tucked away in a small no though road in this pretty village. The property which was built to a high standard by Berkeley Homes successfully combines character with contemporary living. On the ground floor there is a lovely bright sitting room with open stone fireplace, dining room with French doors to the garden, a well fitted kitchen and cloakroom whilst upstairs there are three bedrooms, the master ensuite and a family bathroom. Outside there is an adjoining stone built garage which could provide further living accommodation/annex (subject to any necessary planning consents) and a lovely well established garden enjoying a South Westerly aspect.

    LOCATION
    Weston On The Green is a pretty North Oxfordshire village just c. 8 miles from Oxford and c. 4 miles from Bicester.The village is surrounded by open farmland with delightful country walks and equestrian activities close by. Wisteria Cottage is situated in the heart of the village just a short walk from the village shop/post office and the Church of St Mary the Virgin. There are also two excellent village pubs, a luxury hotel, a lovely ice cream parlour/restaurant and an antiques centre which also has a fabulous caf�/restaurant.The village is conveniently located for easy access to the A34 and for junction 9 of the M40. Bicester North railway station runs frequent trains to London, Marylebone c.50 minutes and the Bicester Village outlet shopping centre is just a short drive away. There is also a cinema at Bicester and excellent leisure and sports facilities at Bicester, Kidlington and Oxford.
