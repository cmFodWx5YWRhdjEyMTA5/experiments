    This is a most characterful and handsome Victorian house offering extended accommodation, ideally suited to the growing family. The property has a through living / dining room with stripped floors and striking fireplace, cloakroom and extended kitchen / family room opening out onto pretty west facing gardens on the ground floor. On the first floor are three good sized bedrooms and family bathroom. There is also a stylish dormered loft conversion providing a further double bedroom and en-suite bathroom. There are many characterful features throughout the property. Grandpont is a highly sought after area, due to the wide range of local amenities and proximity to Oxford City centre.
