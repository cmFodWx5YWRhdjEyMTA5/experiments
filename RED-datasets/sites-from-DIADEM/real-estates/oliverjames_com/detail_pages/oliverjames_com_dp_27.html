    • This is a well presented and most characterful Victorian bay fronted house on the favoured west side of the road with accommodation arranged over three floors.


    This is one of the larger style, bay fronted Victorian houses occupying a quiet position on the much favoured, west side of the street and having the benefit of a large, west facing rear garden which has been beautifully landscaped by the present owners and will appeal to those purchasers who would value a c.100ft rear garden. The accommodation is arranged over three floors and features three double bedrooms including a stylish, dormered loft conversion. There is a large, feature bathroom on the first floor with roll-top bath and separate shower. On the ground floor, there is a through living/dining room and kitchen/breakfast room. This could be extended (subject to planning) across the side return to add further value.
