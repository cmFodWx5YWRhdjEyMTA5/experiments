    Full Details:

    A spacious detached 1930's bungalow which has been extended by the present owners to provide an impressive family home with a self contained annex. The main property offers three double bedrooms (one with ensuite w.c.), sitting room,dining area, well fitted kitchen, large family bathroom, utility room and outside entertaining room. The annex offers sitting room, kitchen/dining room, shower room and bedroom to incorporate a dressing room area. Outside there is ample off street parking space to the front and a large garden to the rear with various outbuildings.

    LOCATION
    The property is situated close to Kidlington's excellent local amenities including a wide range of shops, banks, a library, doctors and dentists surgeries and schooling for all ages. Gosford Sports Centre has an indoor pool, a well equipped gym and squash courts. The village is particularly well placed for easy access to the centre of Oxford with a frequent 24 hour bus service. It is also well positioned for the M40 to the North and the M4 to the South.
