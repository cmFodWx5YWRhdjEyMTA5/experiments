    • A stunning extended family house overlooking fields to the front and rear, enjoying a 213ft south facing garden. The house comprises four bedrooms, en-suite, 19ft sitting room, vaulted 24ft family room, kitchen and study. Potential to convert the garage to a 5th Bedroom/annexe. End of Chain.


    A stunning extended family home that has been completely renovated, modernised and extended by the current owners, enjoying a 213ft south facing rear garden and views over open fields to the front and rear of the property. This spacious and well presented house is situated less than 1 mile from Abingdon and only 3 miles from Oxford with easy access to the A34.

    The ground floor accommodation comprises 19ft sitting room complete with log burner and adjoining 24ft vaulted family room both with doors leading to the garden and patio area, study, family kitchen and cloakroom. The garage can be accessed from the family room providing potential to further extend the living accommodation to provide an additional room such as a 5th bedroom/annexe.

    On the first floor are four good sized bedrooms with the master bedroom benefiting from built in wardrobes and an en-suite shower room. There is also a family bathroom. To the front of the house is a lawned area as well as a double width driveway leading to a 15ft garage/workshop integral to the house.

    The attractive rear garden measures c.213ft x 50ft and has large lawned areas with an abundance of mature plants, shrubs, wildlife pond and orchard containing mature apple trees. There is also a formal koi pond, pergola with grape vine, 2 garden sheds and 12ft x 8ft summerhouse located at the bottom of the orchard.
