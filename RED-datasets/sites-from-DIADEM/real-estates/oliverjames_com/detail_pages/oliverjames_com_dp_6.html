    This individual architect designed house is located in the tree lined Norman Avenue in the North of Abingdon well within walking distance of the local schools, shops and bus routes to Abingdon and the city of Oxford. Situated on a plot of 0.38 acre this detached house offers scope for further extension to the side or rear (subject to consents).
    The main accommodation downstairs comprises 19ft sitting room with open fireplace and views over the rear garden, 14ft study/family room, dining room and 18ft kitchen/breakfast room. There is also a refitted cloakroom, porch and hallway.
    Upstairs are three double bedrooms (potential to make 4 bedrooms) all with built in/walk in wardrobes and a refitted shower.
    Apart from the Prime location the house boasts one of the largest plots in the road with the front laid mainly to lawn and driveway parking leading to a 19ft garage while to the rear is a c225ft Southwest Facing garden laid to lawn with mature plants and shrubs. There is also a 19ft x 17ft summer house in the garden.
