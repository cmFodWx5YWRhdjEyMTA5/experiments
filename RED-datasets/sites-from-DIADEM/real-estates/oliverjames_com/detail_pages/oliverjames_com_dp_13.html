    LOCATION
    Charlton On Otmoor lies to the North East of Oxford and has excellent commuting links via the A34 and link roads to Oxford. Junction 9 of the M40 is c.9 miles. There are also main rail connections from Islip to Oxford and Bicester to Marylebone and Birmingham. There is a local primary school in the village, which has a very good reputation. There is also a church and some excellent recreational walks over Otmoor and the surrounding countryside. Oxford is c.8 miles away.
