    Full Details:

    This is a recently renovated and extended box bay fronted Victorian end of terraced house, offering particularly light and airy four bedroomed accommodation to include a light loft conversion with en-suite shower room. The property has a spacious sitting room and kitchen dining room with ground floor cloakroom. Gas central heating and tasteful double glazing is installed. The gardens are a particular feature as the property occupies a corner plot position and the off street parking at the rear accessed from Norreys Avenue.


    New Hinksey is an area very popular with families due to an excellent range of family based local amenities including Hinksey Parks, with a tennis court, children’s’ playground, splash pool and heated swimming pools. There is also a playgroup and primary school. There is a good range of local shops and Oxford City Centre is within easy walking distance.
