    • A pretty stone built cottage forming part of the original Almshouses which date from 1851. Boasting spacious accommodation comprising a fitted kitchen/breakfast room, 27ft sitting/dining room, three double bedrooms, bathroom and attic room. Outside is a pretty south facing garden and to the front there is off road parking for two/three vehicles.

    LOCATION
    Islip is a sought after village located only c.5 miles North of Oxford with the benefit of a railway station and easy access to the M40 (junction 9 c.3 miles). There is also a village school with pre-school, a church, a village hall with an excellent community shop general store, two pubs (which both serve food) and a health centre with pharmacy.
