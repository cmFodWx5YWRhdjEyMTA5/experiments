WIT130222
Oxford Hill, Witney, Oxfordshire, OX28   3JU (Sold STC)
Guide Price £270,000
Bedrooms: 2


Set over three full floors this very spacious two bedroom stone town house has a single storey extension to the rear which opens directly onto a delightful timber decked area and enclosed lawn beyond. The property is presented in good decorative order throughout and benefits from two exposed stone chimney breasts, the front of which has an open fire and the rear of which is currently set to book shelving. The extension creates a good size kitchen fitted with a range of timber units and further benefits from a walk-in larder. There is a good size double bedroom to the front and re-fitted bathroom with a real boutique feel to the rear whilst the second bedroom on the top floor is a very good size thanks to the three storey nature of this property. The front garden sets the house back from the Oxford Hill very nicely and the rear is enclosed to both sides by timber fencing and has a stone outbuilding useful for storage. Overall, a delightful home offered for sale on an end of chain basis and available for immediate occupation. An internal viewing is highly recommended.

Witney is a small country town steeped in history, but with good modern amenities and a bustling community life. The Leisure centre caters for a variety of sports and there are excellent schools, including a number of church schools. The Woolgate centre and other smaller shops have been supplemented by a new central shopping precinct with major names such as Marks & Spencer, Debenhams and in addition a multi-screen cinema. Public transport is available from outlying villages and from Witney to Oxford and on to London.
