Brook Hill, Woodstock, Oxfordshire, OX20   1XH (Sold STC)
Guide Price £350,000
Bedrooms: 2
Bathroom: 1
Reception room: 1


A beautifully presented late Victorian mid terrace cottage, located in a no through road close to the town centre, approached via steps leading up to a paved courtyard with seating area. The current owner has refurbished the property to a very high standard of presentation and both the main and extension roofs have been renewed, along with the damp proof course. It comprises a sitting room with an open fireplace incorporating a gas fire, extended kitchen/dining room with skylight roof, two double bedrooms and a beautifully fitted bathroom. To the rear is a south facing tiered garden with patio and shed.

Woodstock has a charming centre with two banks, shops, hotels and restaurants as befits an historic town adjoining one of Britain's finest World Heritage Sites. There are Churches of several denominations, a Woodstock Under Fives Association (WUFA), which provides a lovely playgroup for children aged 3 to 5, schooling from 5 to 18 and a regular bus service to Oxford and local market towns of Chipping Norton and Witney.
