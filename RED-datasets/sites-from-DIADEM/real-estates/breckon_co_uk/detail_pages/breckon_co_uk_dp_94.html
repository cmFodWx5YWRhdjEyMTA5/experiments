If you are looking for a home with development potential then this one could be for you. The location itself is probably one of the most popular in Witney and the position of this home on a corner plot brings with it a larger than average rear and side garden that offers high levels of seclusion and privacy. Additionally there is plenty of scope to extend, subject of course to the usual consents whilst still retaining a good deal of outside space. Currently the spacious property benefits from a dual aspect sitting room, separate dining room and kitchen (with granite worksurfaces) that overlooks the rear garden. A good size cloakroom completes the ground floor picture. There are three double bedrooms to the first floor and family bathroom and there is gas fired radiator central heating throughout. The front garden is laid to lawn and is of open plan design with a pathway to the front door whilst a herringbone block driveway to the side provides off-road hardstanding for three cars that leads to a detached garage. The rear garden has been well landscaped over the years with lawn, shrub borders and paved terrace adjacent to the rear of the property. Available for immediate occupation and offered on an end of chain basis a truly charming detached family home that really is a 'must see' property.

Witney is a small country town steeped in history, but with good modern amenities and a bustling community life. The Leisure centre caters for a variety of sports and there are excellent schools, including a number of church schools. The Woolgate centre and other smaller shops have been supplemented by a new central shopping precinct with major names such as Marks & Spencer, Debenhams and in addition a multi-screen cinema. Public transport is available from outlying villages and from Witney to Oxford and on to London.
