Bear Close, Woodstock, Oxfordshire, OX20   1JT (Sold STC)
Guide Price £398,500
Bedrooms: 3
Bathroom: 1
Reception rooms: 2


Well presented semi-detached house situated close to the town centre. The property has been significantly improved by the current owner and comprises entrance hall, double aspect sitting room, modern conservatory with electric underfloor heating, kitchen/breakfast room, landing, three bedrooms and bathroom. Outside to the front is gravelled providing ample off-street parking whilst to the rear is a delightful and private garden.

Woodstock is an historic town adjoining one of Britain's finest World Heritage Sites and has a bank, shops, hotels and restaurants, cafes and public houses and a vibrant community. There are Churches of several denominations, a museum, open air swimming pool, tennis and bowling club, playgroup for children aged 3 to 5 and schooling from 5 to 18. Regular bus services to Oxford and local market towns of Chipping Norton and Witney. Rail to Oxford and London from nearby Long Hanborough.
