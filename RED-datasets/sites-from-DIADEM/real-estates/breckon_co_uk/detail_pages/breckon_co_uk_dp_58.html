A truly charming town house located in the centre of Witney in a quiet no-through road. The property was built in 2007 to Barratt Homes premier specification with quality fittings throughout. These have been further enhanced by the current owner; the combined result is a wonderful spacious property within a moment's walk of the wide range of facilities available in the town centre. The eat-in kitchen/breakfast room has a tiled floor and glazed French doors that open directly to the paved terrace. The dual aspect sitting room also has French doors to the terrace and a feature fireplace to the front. There are three good size bedrooms to the first floor with a house bathroom and a master suite to the second floor with walk-in wardrobe and ensuite shower room. The easily maintained rear garden has is fully enclosed by timber fencing to each side and Bradstone wall to rear. This then has gated access to the rear which leads to the garage with additional parking space.

If you would like to take a look around this beautiful property please call the Witney Office on 01993 776775.

Witney is a small country town steeped in history, but with good modern amenities and a bustling community life. There are fine buildings surrounding the ancient Buttercross in Market Square and a twice weekly market. Countryside is all around and the river Windrush flows through the town providing bankside walks and fishing. The Leisure centre caters for a variety of sports and there are excellent schools, including a number of church schools. The Woolgate centre and other smaller shops have been supplemented by a new central shopping precinct with major names such as Marks & Spencer, Debenhams and in addition a multi-screen cinema. Public transport is available from outlying villages and from Witney to Oxford and on to London. Woodstock 10 miles, Oxford 13 miles, Burford 7 miles and London 68 miles.
