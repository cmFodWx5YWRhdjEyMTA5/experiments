Moorland Road, Witney, Oxfordshire, OX28   6LF (Sold STC)
Guide Price £270,000
Bedrooms: 3


Positioned towards the end of a private no through road this well proportioned character home is filled with natural light creating a lovely spacious feel.

Internally the property is well presented with the accommodation spread across three storeys. The ground floor is bright, light and airy and comprises a kitchen with dining area, a living room with a feature fireplace and a separate conservatory. To the first floor there are two bedrooms and a house bathroom, with a good sized landing area currently housing a desk. On the top floor you'll find the Master Bedroom with fitted wardrobes and a window that has views across the roof tops of Witney to St Mary's Church.

Outside there are two parking spaces, one of which is under a covered carport. There are also two separate gardens to the front and back, both of which offer excellent degrees of privacy and plenty of space.

The property is close to amenities with only a few minutes stroll to Marriot's Walk shopping Centre, is within the catchment area of a number of primary schools, and offers easy access to both Oxford and the Cotswolds.

Witney is a small country town steeped in history, but with good modern amenities and a bustling community life. The Leisure centre caters for a variety of sports and there are excellent schools, including a number of church schools. The Woolgate centre and other smaller shops have been supplemented by a new central shopping precinct with major names such as Marks & Spencer, Debenhams and in addition a multi-screen cinema. Public transport is available from outlying villages and from Witney to Oxford and on to London.
