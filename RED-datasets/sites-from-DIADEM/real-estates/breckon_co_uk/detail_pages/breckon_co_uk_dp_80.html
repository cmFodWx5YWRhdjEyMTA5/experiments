Greenfield Road, Stonesfield, Witney, OX29   8EQ
Guide Price £350,000
Bedrooms: 3
Bathroom: 1
Reception rooms: 2


Recently reduced to stimulate additional activity and now offered at exceptional value for money. Having been in the same ownership for many years this super detached house provides spacious accommodation across two floors with plenty of opportunity for extension (subject to regulations); perfect for a growing family! The dual aspect sitting/dining room has a working fireplace with log burning stove and the dining area has ample space for table and chairs with a serving hatch to the kitchen. Doors from the dining area open through to the garden room which has a tiled floor, windows to three sides and a door that opens to the garden. The eat-in kitchen sits to the rear and a useful lobby with cloakroom completes the ground floor picture. There are three bedrooms and three piece bathroom suite on the first floor. Additional benefits include the solar panels to assist with electricity costs, air conditioning units to both floors and the oil fired central heating to radiators. Externally the front lawn with flower and shrub borders sits alongside a driveway that provides parking and in turn leads to the garage which has light and power connected. The mature rear garden has been well tended and is planted with a host of shrubs and flowers. It is fully enclosed and enjoys a good deal of privacy and seclusion. A delightful property that has not been seen on the market for many years and one that I would have thought would not be returned to the market for years to come once a fresh sale has been completed.

Stonesfield is a meandering, predominantly stone village, approx 4 miles west of Woodstock and 5 miles north-east of Witney. It has shops for day-to-day needs, a primary school, 13th century parish church and a pub. A bus service operates to Oxford via Woodstock and to the local market town of Chipping Norton. There is a mainline railway station on the Worcester-Oxford-London (Paddington) line at Charlbury (4 miles).
