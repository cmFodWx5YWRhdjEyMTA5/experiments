Venvell Close, Coxs Lane, Enstone, OX7   4FL
Guide Price £299,950
Bedrooms: 3
Bathrooms: 2
Reception rooms: 2


An attractive and practical family house with three double bedrooms, in a small no through road on the edge of this well placed north Oxfordshire village. Well designed with a good sitting room, separate dining hall, fitted kitchen and utility room, inner hall and cloakroom. The principal bedroom has an en-suite shower room, with fitted wardrobes to the other two doubles, and a family bathroom. The rear garden has a sunny, westerly aspect; to the front is off road parking in front of the integral garage, with a further parking space nearby.

Enstone is a village approx 4 miles south of the market town of Chipping Norton. It has a village shop/post office, a primary school, a 16th century parish church and a choice of pubs. There is a bus service to Chipping Norton and to Oxford via Woodstock. There is a BR station on the Worcester - Oxford - London (Paddington) line in nearby Charlbury (4miles).
