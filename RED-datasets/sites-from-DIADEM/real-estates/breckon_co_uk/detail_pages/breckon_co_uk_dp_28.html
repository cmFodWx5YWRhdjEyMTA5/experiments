Cote Road, Aston, Bampton, OX18   2DU (Sold STC)
Guide Price £250,000
Bedrooms: 2
Bathroom: 1
Reception room: 1


This beautiful stone cottage dates back to the early 1800's and as you would expect is filled with an abundance of character features. The log burning stove together with the beamed ceiling gives a lovely cosy feel to the sitting room which is positioned at the front. The contemporary kitchen brings old and new together with gloss floor tiles and granite work surfaces. Upstairs there are two bedrooms and the house bathroom. The bi-fold doors to the rear of the kitchen allow natural light to fill the room and give an uninterrupted view over the large garden which in turn leads to the Garden Office which is fully insulated and has the added benefit of light and power. There is off street parking to the front on a gravelled driveway. An additional benefit is the current Planning Permission for a garden room which would lead from the kitchen and incorporate the current patio area. In all this really is a delightful cottage where viewing is strongly advised.

Aston is a small village located some five miles south of Witney. Facilities include a private nursery school, excellent primary school, repair garage, St James Church, Baptist Church and local pub. The village shop and post office provides a large range of groceries and is open daily. The village boasts a traditional working pottery with country style cafe and gift shop. Bampton is a former market town with additional facilities including a doctor's surgery and is approximately two miles distant. A bus service runs to Witney with its wider range of shopping, schooling and recreational facilities.
