Homes like this are hard to find with delightful views over countryside yet within a short walk of the town and all amenities. The airy ground floor accommodation offers two receptions, an eat-in kitchen, separate utility, ground floor shower room and bespoke conservatory. The conservatory opens directly to the absolutely magnificent garden beyond. As you pass the traditional end line of the house, the adjoining garden was acquired some years ago creating a fabulous additional area of outside space. A Pergola with grape vine and paved area makes an ideal space for alfresco dining, this in turn leads to the remainder of the extensive rear gardens which are divided into manageable sections. The borders are well stocked with a host of maturing shrubs, bushes and flowers and there is an ornamental fish pond. A brick paved pathway leads to a lawn area and the path winds on eventually to a vegetable patch that has been well maintained over the years by the current owners. To the rear boundary is a useful large timber shed with light and power connected. Internally the first floor has two good size bedrooms, the master of which is to the front and again enjoys far reaching views across the Windrush Valley plus large house bathroom. The second floor has two further bedrooms which have been created from the former roof space. An additional benefit is the driveway to the front that offers considerable off-street parking. This is a truly lovely property with marvellous gardens and enjoying an idyllic location close to the Cotswold countryside.

Witney is a small country town steeped in history, but with good modern amenities and a bustling community life. The Leisure centre caters for a variety of sports and there are excellent schools, including a number of church schools. The Woolgate centre and other smaller shops have been supplemented by a new central shopping precinct with major names such as Marks & Spencer, Debenhams and in addition a multi-screen cinema. Public transport is available from outlying villages and from Witney to Oxford and on to London.

OUTSIDE REAR

The extensive rear gardens are divided into sections and start with a good size terrace adjacent to the rear of the property with incredibly well stocked borders to both sides. Ornamental fish pond.
