WIT130060
Burford Road, Witney, Oxfordshire, OX28   6DR (Sold STC)
Guide Price £360,000
Bedrooms: 3


As you alight from your car on the gravel drive you are immediately taken by the stunning views across the Windrush Valley to one side and the attractive front elevation of the property to the other. Crossing the threshold takes you into this fine family home where you are greeted by delight after delight. The light and airy sitting room to the front has a square bay window with inset seating and storage under and a magnificent cast iron fireplace. A second reception room to the centre of the property is suitable for a number of purposes and leads directly to an open kitchen/breakfast room. The deep slate floor creates a feeling of warmth enhanced by the gas fired twin oven Stanley range with bespoke timber units to floor and eye level. A brick faced arched doorway leads to a stunning dining room with exposed timber vaulted ceiling. Glazed French doors open directly onto a paved terrace with timber Pergola and mature Wisteria providing a natural covered shade. Beyond this terrace the rear garden is approaching 190' in length and has been divided into a number of sections, each and every one a pure delight. To the first floor the master bedroom also has a square bay window from which the best views across the Windrush Valley can be enjoyed. There are two further bedrooms and a refitted bathroom to the rear with panelled bath and separate shower cubicle. Whilst our photography shows the property in the best of lights this is one of those homes that can really only be fully appreciated after a guided tour. The property is offered on an end of chain basis, please contact the Witney office at your earliest convenience.

Witney is a small country town steeped in history, but with good modern amenities and a bustling community life. The Leisure centre caters for a variety of sports and there are excellent schools, including a number of church schools. The Woolgate centre and other smaller shops have been supplemented by a new central shopping precinct with major names such as Marks & Spencer, Debenhams and in addition a multi-screen cinema. Public transport is available from outlying villages and from Witney to Oxford and on to London.
