£375,000
Bedrooms: 2

A quaint, picturesque terraced cottage situated in the heart of Headington with excellent transport links into the centre of town and London. On the ground floor the property comprises entrance hall with kitchen leading off facing the front of the property. The spacious living/dining room is to the rear of the house and has patio doors leading out into the low maintenance south facing garden.

Upstairs are two good sized double bedrooms filled with light (one with built in storage) and a modern bathroom with a shower over the bath and a heated towel rail.

The property is double glazed throughout and has gas central heating. On street parking is available through the Oxford City Council residential permit scheme. The property benefits from no onward chain.

Please contact sole agents Breckon and Breckon on 01865 750200 to arrange your viewing.
