Positioned in a residential cul-de-sac of similar properties on the northern edge of Witney this link-detached family home is offered in very good decorative order throughout. The kitchen has a wide range of matching base and eye level units including an electric double oven and five ring gas hob with Wok burner which is set into a central island. The separate dining area has ample space for table and chairs and leads through to the sitting room which enjoys views over the garden through double opening patio doors. The useful study/landing provides access to all the first floor accommodation with the master bedroom having an ensuite area and the two further bedrooms being served by the house bathroom. The delightful and good size rear garden affords a high level of privacy and has been landscaped with raised flower beds, shrub borders and gravel area with space for table and chairs. A further private patio area is ideal for alfresco dining and the whole is enclosed by 6' close board fencing. The property has a single garage with light and power connected and driveway parking for two cars. Overall, a spacious family home where an appointment to view will not disappoint.

Witney is a small country town steeped in history, but with good modern amenities and a bustling community life. The Leisure centre caters for a variety of sports and there are excellent schools, including a number of church schools. The Woolgate centre and other smaller shops have been supplemented by a new central shopping precinct with major names such as Marks & Spencer, Debenhams and in addition a multi-screen cinema. Public transport is available from outlying villages and from Witney to Oxford and on to London.
