Fritillary Mews, Ducklington, Witney, OX29   7AB (Sold STC)
Guide Price £295,000
Bedrooms: 3


With WOW factor in abundance, this spacious semi detached property has been immaculately maintained both internally and externally is ready to move straight into with no work required whatsoever. The stunning sitting room has an engineered Oak wooden floor and a large picture window and open archway through to a newly fitted conservatory which in turn looks over the delightfully landscaped rear gardens. When the current owner moved back to the UK from her Spanish home, she clearly brought back a sprinkle of Mediterranean Magic for her outside space. The gardens have been her pride and joy with hours of thought going into the beautiful scheme which creates depth and colour of a low maintenance design. The rear garden also offers a good degree of seclusion and privacy and opens directly onto the garage to the rear. The recently re-fitted kitchen is fully equipped with integrated appliances including fridge freezer, dishwasher, washer dryer and dual fuel Rangemaster oven. A cloakroom for everyday convenience completes the ground floor picture. The first floor has two doubles and a large single bedroom, the master of which has ensuite shower room. The house bathroom is well appointed and the property as a whole has been superbly maintained throughout. Overall a stunning property in a very popular village location.

Ducklington is one of the first Saxon parishes to be recorded in Oxfordshire in AD 958. The village has two public houses, a beautiful historic centre which is a designated Conservation area, a thriving primary school, Baptist Chapel, Church and active sports club. The village is two miles from Witney with its many amenities and twelve miles from Oxford with easy access to the A40.
