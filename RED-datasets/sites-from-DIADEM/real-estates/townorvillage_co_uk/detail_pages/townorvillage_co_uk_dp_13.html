Property Description

This beautiful detached family home is situated in the most idyllic environment of Milton under Wychwood, Oxfordshire.
Having 4 bedrooms, an office and 3 receptions, a family eat in kitchen, 2 bathrooms and is set within the most glorious landscaped garden.
This property offers wonderful family living with areas to suit all needs. Having one formal reception with an open fire which adjoins a good size office with views to the garden.
It also has another family room ideal for the growing family, a formal dining area and a fully equipped kitchen which includes an Aga and seating area for the informal dining.
There are numerous utility areas as well as an outdoor shed with extra storage facilities.
Upstairs are 4 good size bedrooms, a large family bathroom and an en suite bathroom with large corner bath.
The property is situated within a beautiful landscaped garden with outdoor entertaining areas that lead to a superb lawn that backs onto fields. The rent also includes a gardener.
There is also plenty of off street parking.
Milton under Wychwood is a well sought after village with excellent facilities, including excellent schools, and is close to the mainline station at Chalbury, which makes this property ideal for the commuter to London who desires rural Oxfordshire living and also excellent commuting facilities to all the schools and colleges of Oxford.
