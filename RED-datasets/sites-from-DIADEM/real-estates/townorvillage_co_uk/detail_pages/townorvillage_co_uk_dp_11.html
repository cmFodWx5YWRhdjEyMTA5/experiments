Property Description

This beautifully presented cottage is set within the heat of St Clements and is walking distance to the centre of Oxford and all the colleges.
It situated at the end of a quiet turning and overlooks Bat Willow Meadow.
The property had been refurbished to an excellent standard with well thought usage of space.
There are 2 double bedrooms and a further small single bedroom that can be used as an upstairs study.
The main bedroom has beautiful views over the Meadows which gives the room a rural aspect, although in the heart of Oxford.
There is a very pretty family bathroom upstairs and a further small shower room downstairs

There is a sitting room combined with a dining area which opens onto a small patio area with garden shed.
The kitchen is fully equipped.
This cottage has loads of appeal and is ideal for the visiting academic or a professional couple
