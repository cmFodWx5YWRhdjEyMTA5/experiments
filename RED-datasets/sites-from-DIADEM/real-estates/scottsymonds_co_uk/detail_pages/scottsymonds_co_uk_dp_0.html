Property Description:

ACCOMMODATION (All dimensions approximate)

ENTRANCE HALL
Video security entry phone, coved ceiling, night storage heater, airing cupboard housing foam insulated hot water tank with electric immersion heater and linen shelf over, built-in cupboard.

LIVING ROOM
Attractive room with views over the Glyme Valley, sealed unit double glazed windows, coved ceiling, night storage heater, convector heater, telephone point.

KITCHEN
Sealed unit double glazed windows to front, one and a half bowl single drainer stainless steel sink unit with mixer tap, range of natural wood fronted wall and base units, laminate work surfaces, (Zanussi) double oven, (Zanussi) electric hob, plumbing for automatic washing machine, part-tiled walls, convector heater.

BEDROOM ONE
Sealed unit double glazed windows to front, coved ceiling, built-in wardrobes, convector heater, telephone point.

BEDROOM TWO
Sealed unit double glazed windows to front, coved ceiling, built-in wardrobe, convector heater.

BATHROOM
Comprising panelled bath with mixer tap and shower attachment, pedestal wash hand basin, low-level wc, part-tiled walls, heated towel rail, wall mounted fan heater, extractor fan.

OUTSIDE

GARDENS
The property over-looks the communal gardens to the front, which are laid to lawn with flower and shrub borders and trees.

PARKING
There is underground parking for one car.

TENURE
The property is offered for sale leasehold with a 999 year lease with an annual service charge payable to Beechcroft Trust, a non profit making trust which manages the site on behalf of all the occupants. The current service charge includes maintenance of the grounds, the arrangement of refuse collection and external window cleaning. The salary of the secretary, who is permanently available to offer advice and assistance and to be on hand in an emergency, is also included, and an alarm system enables help to be contacted immediately at any time of night or day.

Although the owner of the apartment can be of any age, the principle occupier must be aged 55 or over.
