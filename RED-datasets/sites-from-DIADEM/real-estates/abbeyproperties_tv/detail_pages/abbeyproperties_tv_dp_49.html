  • DESCRIPTION

    This detached family house is situated in this cul de sac position within this popular Cotswold village. The property has been extended over the years and offers versatile family accommodation. On the ground floor the living room has an open fireplace and adjoins the impressive conservatory that sits to the rear of the property overlooking the very well-tended rear gardens. The kitchen is also set to the rear of the house and from this leads to a very practical utility/boot room that gives access to the rear gardens. The dining room is set to the front of the house and is open to the living room creating an open L shape. The reception hallway to the front of the house gives access to the useful cloakroom and to the single garage together with the dining room and kitchen. On the first floor the property excels with 5 good sized bedrooms certainly 4 doubles and a very good single. From another practical point of view is that there is a family bathroom and completely separate family shower room both have been re fitted with white suites. Other benefits to the property are the double glazed windows and the gas fired radiator heating system. Outsides the property has a driveway to the front for c.3-4 cars and an attractive open plan garden area. Both flanks of the property also provide rear access to the well planned and well stocked rear gardens that measure c. 60’ x 45’.

  • LOCATION

    Shipton-Under-Wychwood and Milton-Under-Wychwood are adjoining villages and between them they have various amenities and facilities. These include post offices, public houses/restaurants, school, churches, hairdressers, library, convenience stores and petrol stations. The main Rail links via Worcester to London runs past the village with regular stops at Ascott-Under-Wychwood (1.2 Miles) Charlbury (6.4 miles) and Kingham (3.2 miles). There is a bus service to Charlbury town and railway station, Witney, Chipping Norton and Moreton-in-Marsh. Situated c.4 miles north of Burford there are good road links to Chipping Norton and Stow-On-The-Wold. Access to Oxford and Cheltenham is excellent from Burford via the A40.
