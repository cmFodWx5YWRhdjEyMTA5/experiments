    • DESCRIPTION

      This is a truly unique opportunity to purchase a property that has not seen the open market in c.90 years. The original dates back some c.350 years and then has had later additions. The house is grade II listed and will require extensive modernisation and renovation so buyers will need to comply with the full regulations that come with a grade II listed property. The property has 3 reception areas on the ground floor and a cellar. On the first floor there are 3 bedrooms a bathroom and separate W.c together with a good sized landing which provides access to 2 further separate stairs that access 3 further attic rooms with open roof beams. All over this property character features can be found and we believe from the current vendors that the property many years ago was a public house called the Black House. Evidence of this can be found in the cellar and from an old safe found in one of the reception rooms. Another sought after benefit with this property is the archway to the side that would provide vehicular access and thus off road parking which is again unusual for a house this close to the town.       From our Corn Street office enter the town centre and turn left into Market Square. Follow this road that becomes High Street and then Bridge Street.     • LOCATION

      The vibrant and rapidly expanding Town has a wide range of shops centred round the sympathetically designed Woolgate Centre, traditional High Street and new central pedestrian shopping mall with major shops including Marks & Spencer, Debenhams and Multi-Screen 3d cinema. The Market Square opens onto the delightful Church Green with St. Mary’s Church dominating the south side. Other amenities in the Town include supermarkets, banks, two Secondary schools and numerous Primary schools, a vast array of restaurants and public houses, a very good daily bus service into Oxford and just on the outskirts of the town is Witney lakes Resorts with golf course, health club and swimming pool. There is also a central community sports centre within the town itself.
