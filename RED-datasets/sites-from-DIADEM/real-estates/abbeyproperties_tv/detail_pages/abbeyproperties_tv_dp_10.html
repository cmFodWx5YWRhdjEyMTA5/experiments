  • DESCRIPTION

    A charming stone built end terrace Cottage tucked away in a side road of period properties a mere stone's throw from The Square and old village centre. The Cottage is surprisingly spacious and larger than expected from first view of the front with a rear extension and well proportioned rooms along with a very pleasant walled courtyard garden. Internally the property has been well maintained and benefits from double glazing and gas central heating. The accommodation consists of a 16' x 15' Sitting/Dining Room, Kitchen, Bathroom and a good sized double Bedroom with Juliet balcony. The Cottage is offered for sale with no onward chain and viewing is highly advised to those seeking an older character property in this very sought after village 6 miles from Oxford.

  • LOCATION

    Eynsham is a very sought after West Oxfordshire village with an excellent range of local facilities and a fantastic daily bus service into both Oxford and Witney, each some 6 miles distant. Shops & Facilities in the village include CO-OP and Spar shops, Butcher, Bakery, Greengrocer, Off-Licence, Post Office, The Eynsham Emporium and Cookshop, Electrical shop, Library, modern Health Centre and a handful of traditional pubs. The village also has toddler groups, a children's centre, a Primary School, and the highly reputable Bartholomew Secondary School. The active and friendly local community is buoyed by sports clubs and a large number of clubs and societies catering for all age groups and interests.
