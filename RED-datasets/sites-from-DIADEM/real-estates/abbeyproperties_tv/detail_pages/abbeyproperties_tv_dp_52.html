  • DESCRIPTION

    If you are looking for an individual Family Home with an independent self-contained Annexe this has to be the property for you! Dating from the 1950's this lovely mature detached house stands in a large and beautifully maintained plot and has the benefit of a very comfortable self-contained single storey 1 Bedroom Annexe. The spacious Flat is attached to the rear of the house but is entirely separate internally and absolutely ideal for an elderly relative, an older child wanting independence or alternatively to let as a rental income. With some internal modification one could incorporate the whole property into one to create a very sizeable dwelling, subject to consents. The main House offers well proportioned family sized accommodation which includes a lovely Breakfast Kitchen, a 24' x 15' Lounge/Diner with a striking stone fireplace, a Study/Garden Room, Utility, Cloakroom, 3 first floor Bedrooms and a second floor Attic Bedroom complete with access to a large area of boarded roof space. The property has gas central heating and leaded light double glazing almost throughout. Outside there is a large gravelled driveway, garage and a very good sized established rear garden with southerly aspect. The Annexe has it's own front door access, Kitchen, Sitting Room, Shower Room, 1 double Bedroom, gas central heating and a small private patio area. It is separately metered for services. The village of Freeland lies c. 6 miles from Oxford, c. 4 miles from Witney and c. 2 miles for Long Hanborough and its popular railway station. The village itself has a highly reputable primary school, a play group, Church, sports field and village pub. A bus service operates to Oxford, Woodstock and the local market town of Witney and the A40 is within 2 miles, giving access to Oxford, Cheltenham and the west. The nearby village of Long Hanborough has good amenities to include a new Co-op store, post office, Medical Centre and the aforementioned train station.
