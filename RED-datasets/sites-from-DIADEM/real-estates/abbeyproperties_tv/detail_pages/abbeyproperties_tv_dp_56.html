  • DESCRIPTION

    A stunning detached and extended village House dating originally from the Victorian era boasting a beautifully presented, stylish, and highly versatile interior with an established plot approaching half an acre in this lovely small hamlet some 2 miles north of Witney. Originally a modest Victorian property the house has been considerably extended over the years and now comprises a very substantial family sized house with large south facing secluded gardens. The centre section of the property retains the charm of the original period property with exposed beams and an old stone fireplace whilst the later additions have been carefully carried out and finished to a high standard with a stylish contemporary feel. Particular features of the property include solid wood flooring throughout a large part of the ground floor, double glazing, large stone fireplace with wood-burning stove, a number of modern period style radiators, white sanitary ware, fitted appliances in the Utility Room, terracotta tiled flooring to the Kitchen & Utility, ledged & braced latch doors and a high quality oak-framed double car-port with loft room above. The layout of the accommodation is extremely versatile with the ground floor in particular having a very pleasant flow of rooms. On entering the house you have a Family Room/Snug leading into the Sitting Room which is partly divided by three open arches. There is a small Computer Room/Den, a second Study, Cloakroom, a good sized Breakfast Kitchen with free standing units and a "Smeg" range cooker (both available by negotiation) and a well fitted Utility Room with integrated appliances. The fabulous 28' Conservatory overlooks the garden and is an integral part of the living accommodation linking the ground floor rooms. There are two staircases to the first floor with a secondary double Bedroom suite with En-Suite Shower accessed by stairs from the Family Room. This would be a fantastic guest suite or an independent Bedroom for older children. The sizeable Master Bedroom has fitted wardrobes and a funky boutique style En-Suite Shower Room. The second double Bedroom has two doors from the Landing so could easily be divided if required. A further single Bedroom and Family Bathroom complete the accommodation. The property stands in a large plot approaching half an acre with the majority of garden lying at the rear of the house facing south. To the front and side of the property is a gravelled driveway providing parking for several vehicles. This leads to a substantial oak-framed double car-port with pitched roof, log-store, light and power and an external staircase to a very useful loft room above giving great storage or with studio possibilities. The large established garden enjoys a high level of seclusion and is arranged in two sections with a hedge divide. The garden is mainly laid to lawn and has a large variety of mature trees and neatly tended beds and borders. The small hamlet of New Yatt is close to undulating and un-spoilt swathes of open farmland. The larger village of North Leigh is less than a mile away and has a Primary School, two pubs and a church. New Yatt is some 2 miles north of the thriving Market Town that is Witney. This has a vast selection of shops and facilities including a Waitrose and Sainsbury's supermarket, numerous Primary Schools, two Secondary Schools, Leisure Centre, multi-screen Cinema and many High Street names in the Woolgate and Marriotts Walk shopping areas. An excellent regular bus service runs daily to Oxford, some 12 miles distant.
