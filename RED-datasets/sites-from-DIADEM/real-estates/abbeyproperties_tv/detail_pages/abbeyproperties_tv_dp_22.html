  • LOCATION

    The semi-rural attractive small Hamlet of Sutton lies on the very edge of Stanton Harcourt, off the B4449 between Eynsham and Standlake. Facilities in neighbouring Stanton Harcourt village include The Harcourt Arms Public House that also runs the village shop/post office, Primary School, Parish Church, a good bus service to Eynsham and Oxford, modern Village Hall and a central village green. The village is surrounded by open farmland and offers idyllic countryside walks. The larger village of Eynsham with its excellent range of facilities and daily bus services to both Oxford and Witney is some two miles distant. Sutton falls within the catchment area for the highly reputable Bartholomew Secondary School in Eynsham.

  • DESCRIPTION

    A detached Bungalow in a lovely semi-rural setting dating, we understand, from the 1960's with reconstituted stone elevations under a tiled roof. The Bungalow stands on a wide plot with gardens to front & rear and has a pleasant open rear aspect. Whilst some of the other Bungalows in this location are built to a non-standard construction, this property replaced an earlier Bungalow on the site and is of conventional construction. The property has a Sitting Room with open fireplace, Kitchen, 2 double Bedrooms, a third Bedroom/Study and a fully tiled Bathroom with white suite. Other features include double glazed windows throughout, oil fired CH, fibre-optic broadband connection, a double length garage and ample driveway parking. The property has had some recent re-decoration but there remains scope for further updating.
