  • DIRECTIONS

    From the Eynsham roundabout on the A40 follow the signs for Long Hanborough and after about 2 miles turn left for Church Hanborough. Continue through the village passed the Hand & Shears Pub and on into Long Hanborough, approx.1 mile. On entering Long Hanborough turn left into Churchill Way and then left again into Isis Close. Long Hanborough can also be approached from Witney or Woodstock on the A4095, lying mid-way between the two Towns.

  • DESCRIPTION

    A detached Chalet style House with surprisingly spacious and extremely versatile accommodation over two floors along with the added advantage of a south facing garden with wonderful open views across adjoining paddock land to the rear. Although having scope for some internal updating the property has been well maintained over the years and benefits from replacement double glazing, solar powered water heating and more recently installed photo-voltaic panels, gas central heating, garage, driveway parking and a well tended south facing garden. The rear garden enjoys slightly elevated uninterrupted views over paddock land towards Church Hanborough. The accommodation consists of a Sitting Room overlooking the garden and paddocks beyond, Dining Room, Kitchen, ground floor Bathroom, Study/Bedroom 3 and two first floor Bedrooms. This is certainly a property that needs to be viewed in order to get full appreciation of the accommodation and the superb views from the rear garden.

  • LOCATION

    Long Hanborough is a large village with a widely spread layout which lies approximately mid-way between Witney and Woodstock and some 11 miles North West of Oxford. The village has a variety of both old stone cottages and more modern, mainly post war development. Facilities include a Post Office, modern Co-op store, Hairdresser, Dental Surgery, two Public Houses, Fish & Chip Shop, Repair/Petrol Garage, Primary School and Railway Station with train services to Oxford (London), Hereford and Worcester. Witney, some 5 miles to the west offers excellent shopping, sports and facilities and there are regular bus services to Oxford, Witney and the historic Woodstock. The nearby Blenheim Estate offers fabulous walks around the extensive grounds of Blenheim Palace.
