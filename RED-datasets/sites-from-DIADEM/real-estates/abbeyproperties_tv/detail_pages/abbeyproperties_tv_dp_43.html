  • DESCRIPTION

    This modern detached house has undergone many improvements over recent years and offers very flexible and versatile accommodation with an annex/separate living space/work from home area to the rear. The property is currently set as a 4 bedroomed layout with 3 first floor bedrooms and an additional ground floor room which is to the rear of the property. The reception space currently consists of a Living Room, Study and Dining room with vaulted ceiling with electric skylight windows. Also to the rear of the property within the annex area is a good sized wet room and kitchenette. The family kitchen/breakfast room is finished to a high standard with American Limed Oak and sits to the front of the house. The property has an abundance of features to include hard wood panelling in the hallway, living room and study, Wood burner to the dining area but to list a few. Outside there is ample off road parking to the driveway at the front which also gives access to the side area of driveway with electric gated access to the converted garage again with an electric roller shutter. This area has been converted into a gym and workshop area that also provides access to the rear garden which has a useful timber summer house and additional storage. To conclude this property offers a good level of flexible accommodation and a higher level of features that have been well finished and so viewing is highly recommended.

  • DIRECTIONS

    Upon entering Carterton from the Witney side via Brize Norton Road continue to the very centre to the traffic lights. At the lights go straight over into the Alvescot Road. Follow this road almost out of the town in a Westerly direction and Corbett Road is the last turning on your left before the mini roundabout. Number 44 will be found after a short distance on your left.
