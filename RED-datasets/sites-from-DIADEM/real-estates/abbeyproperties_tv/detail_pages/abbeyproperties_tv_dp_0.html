A first floor retirement Flat for the over 60's in a small two-storey block well situated in a side road within the older part of central Eynsham and a short level walk from of an excellent range of local shops and facilities.  OFFERED FOR SALE WITH NO ONWARD CHAIN.    

The block has a Communal Entrance Hall with entry-phone access, a residents Laundry and Lounge, communal gardens and residents off-road parking. The Flat is very well presented and maintained and comprises 1 double Bedroom, Shower Room, Sitting Room and Kitchen, double glazed windows and electric heating. As each Flat owner has a share of the Freehold service charges are kept to a minimum and each resident has a say in the day-to-day running of the block.             

The Flat is just a short distance from the excellent range of local village shops and amenities which include a Medical Centre, Post Office, Library, Greengrocer, Newsagent, Butcher, Co-Operative and Spa Stores, a traditional DIY shop and Electrical shop.  A regular daily bus service runs to both Oxford & Witney, each some 6 miles distant.  Eynsham is an ideal location for the independent retired with all day-to-day needs on the doorstep.
