A 3 Bedroom end of terrace House presented in good decorative order with many recent improvements undertaken by the current owners. The property stands in a good sized plot of around 135' in length and has a great deal of scope for further improvement and extension, subject to the necessary consents.

The house has a very pleasant light and bright feel throughout and benefits from a tiled Hallway, refitted Cloakroom and first floor Bathroom with white sanitary ware, an open plan Kitchen/Diner with doors to the rear garden, a cosy double aspect Sitting Room with patio doors, refitted Utility Room, replacement double glazing from 2013 and photo-voltaic solar panels installed November 2013. Heating is mains gas fired to radiators.     

The property is set back from the road behind its front garden and high hedge and has ample driveway parking adjacent to the front garden and an equally sizeable rear garden.  With the larger than average plot, potential to extend and well presented and updated interior early viewing is recommended.
