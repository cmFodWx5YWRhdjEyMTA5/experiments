  • DESCRIPTION

    A Mid-Terrace 3 Bedroom house in a small established cul-de-sac close to the village centre offered for sale with no onward chain. The property has well proportioned accommodation of Hall, Kitchen, 26' Sitting/Dining Room, 3 Bedrooms (2 double) and Bathroom. Eynsham is a very sought after West Oxfordshire village with an excellent range of local facilities and a frequent daily bus service into both Oxford and Witney, each some 6 miles distant. Shops & facilities in the village include both CO-OP and Spar shops, Butcher, Bakery, Greengrocer, Off-Licence, Post Office, The Eynsham Emporium and Cookshop, Electrical shop, Library, modern Health Centre and a handful of traditional pubs. The village also has toddler groups, a children's centre, a Primary School, and the highly reputable Bartholomew Secondary School. The active and friendly local community is buoyed by sports clubs and a large number of clubs and societies catering for all interests.
