      This attractive two bedroom end terraced property that is situated in a quiet cul-de-sac location on the popular Deer Park development. The property benefits from off-street driveway parking, and a garage that is accessible from the driveway and rear garden. The accommodation comprises of an entrance hall with the kitchen to the right on entry, and a sitting/dining room that leads on to a conservatory at the rear of the property. On the first floor is a family bathroom, double bedroom and a large single bedroom. The property has gas central heating and the conservatory is double glazed. It is our opinion that this would make a superb home for a young professional couple or family. Alternatively the property would make an excellent investment property that could generate a healthy rental yield. A viewing of this property is highly recommended.

    • LOCATION

      The bustling Market Town of Witney lies some 12 miles west of Oxford City and is well situated for access to the A40, A34 and M4 & M40 motorways. The vibrant and rapidly expanding Town has a wide range of shops centred around the sympathetically designed Woolgate Centre, traditional High Street and new central pedestrian shopping mall with major shopping brands including Marks & Spencer, Debenhams and Multi-Screen 3d cinema. The Market Square opens onto the delightful Church Green with St. Mary’s Church dominating the south side. Other amenities in the Town include supermarkets, banks, two Secondary schools and numerous Primary schools, a vast array of restaurants and public houses, a very good daily bus service into Oxford and just on the outskirts of the town is Witney lakes Resorts with golf course, health club and swimming pool. There is also a central community sports centre within the town itself.
