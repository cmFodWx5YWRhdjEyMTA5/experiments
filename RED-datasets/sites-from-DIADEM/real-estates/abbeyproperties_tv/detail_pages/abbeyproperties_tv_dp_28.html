A very well situated semi-detached house in a small and rarely available cul-de-sac just off the highly regarded Newland Street close to the old village centre. The property has been extended and benefits from much improved ground floor space that comprises a 31' Sitting/Dining Room with patio doors to the garden and a well fitted 18' Breakfast Kitchen.  There are 3 Bedrooms (2 double) and an upgraded Bathroom with white suite.  Other notable features include a south facing garden with no overlooking houses beyond, garage with parking in front, gas c/h and double glazing. 

This established cul-de-sac is considered a very sought-after position in the village and this is the first property made available for sale in this location for a number of years.  Viewing is highly advised.
