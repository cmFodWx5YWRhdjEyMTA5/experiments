    • DESCRIPTION

      An opportunity to purchase a detached three bedroom bungalow set on a generous plot within a cul-de-sac location on the outskirts of the popular town of Carterton. In need of some cosmetic redecoration the property offers generous room sizes that could offer flexible accommodation with the opportunity for new owners to really put their mark on the property. On entering the property there is a porch area leading into the entrance hall. To the right of this is the Sitting/Dining Room, and further on is the generous sized Kitchen/Breakfast Room. Both of these rooms face out the front of the property. At the end of the hallway is the family bathroom which currently has a large sit in shower unit used by the previous owner. All three bedrooms are towards the rear of the property. The largest bedroom is a well sized double with views over the garden. The second bedroom also benefits from rear garden views and is also a double. Bedroom three is a single bedroom. Externally the property has a well sized driveway that could comfortable accommodate three cars and with redevelopment of the front garden could feasible hold more, there is also a single garage that benefits from rear garden access. The rear garden is very generously sized and could make an attractive garden for children to play, or for those with green fingers. The garden faces in an easterly direction and has the benefit of not being overlooked. The property is double glazed throughout and has gas central heating installed. It is our opinion that this would make a superb family home, or an excellent property for those wishing to downsize. A viewing of this property is highly recommended.

    • DIRECTIONS

      From our office in Corn Street proceed out of the town westwards. At the roundabout take the second exit onto the Curbridge Road. Follow this road to the next roundabout and continue straight over. Proceed over the fly over and immediately turn right signposted Carterton. Follow this road through the village of Curbridge and towards Brize Norton. Upon entering Brize Norton at the mini roundabout take the first exit left and proceed along Manor Road. At the next mini roundabout take the right turn onto Carterton Road and proceed along to the next roundabout taking the first exit into Brize Norton Road. Continue until you reach the crossroads. Proceed straight across along Alvescot Road until you see a left hand turning into Corbett Road. At the T junction turn right into The Crescent where number 27 will be found on the left hand side.

    • LOCATION

      Carterton is a major town in the area with excellent shopping, educational and sporting facilities. There are numerous shops, supermarkets, primary and secondary schools, a public library, public houses and local bus services to outlying areas. The major towns of Witney, Oxford and Swindon are within easy reach by car and there is a regular coach service to Oxford and Witney and a daily service to Swindon, with easy access to the A40.
