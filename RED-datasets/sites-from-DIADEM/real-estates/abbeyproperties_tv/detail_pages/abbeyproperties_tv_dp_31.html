  • DESCRIPTION

    An extended end of terrace house dating from the 1960's offering very flexible and larger than expected 4/5 Bedroom accommodation in a popular side road within easy reach of the old village centre. The property has been extended at both ground and first floor level and now provides considerably improved and extremely versatile family sized space rarely found in this price bracket. Particular features include a 20' x 15' Kitchen/Diner, the central hub of the property, a good sized ground floor Bedroom which would make an equally impressive Home Office/Study, 4 first floor Bedrooms with En-Suite to the Master, and newly fitted carpets on the stairs, landing and all Bedrooms. The property is within walking distance of the excellent local facilities that include Post Office, Library, Off-Licence, Green-Grocer, Medical Centre and the Bartholomew Secondary School. The Vendors' have already identified a property to purchase and early viewing is advised. Eynsham is a very sought after West Oxfordshire village with an excellent range of local facilities and a fantastic daily bus service into both Oxford and Witney, each some 6 miles distant. Shops & Facilities in the village include CO-OP and Spar shops, Butcher, Bakery, Greengrocer, Off-Licence, Post Office, The Eynsham Emporium and Cookshop, Electrical shop, Library, modern Health Centre and a handful of traditional pubs. The village also has toddler groups, a children's centre, a Primary School, and the highly reputable Bartholomew Secondary School. The active and friendly local community is buoyed by sports clubs and a large number of clubs and societies catering for all ages and interests.
