    • DESCRIPTION

      A 1960’s semi-detached property that is nicely located in a quiet and popular area of the town. Benefiting from a new gas boiler and full double glazing throughout the property offers well-presented accommodation that includes two ground floor reception rooms, three bedrooms, driveway parking, a garage, and gardens at both the front and rear of the property. On entering the property there is a hallway with the sitting room off to the left hand side. There is also access to the kitchen directly in front of you along with stairs to the first floor. The Sitting Room is decorated neutrally as is most of the rest of the property. There are views out on to the front garden and road beyond. At the far end of the sitting room is sliding double doors that separate the Sitting Room from the Dining Room. The ground floor accommodation is completed with the kitchen that has duel access from both the Dining Room and The Entrance Hall. Within the Kitchen is fitted base units that run along two sides of the room, and a window faces out to the rear garden. There is also an external door that gives access to the rear garden and driveway. There is also two well sized storage cupboards located in the Kitchen. The first floor accommodation consists of two generous sized doubles and a single bedroom. There is also a family bathroom, and landing storage. Externally there is driveway parking that runs from the front boundary line right along the side of the property and could accommodate approximately three cars. There is also a single garage, fully enclosed rear garden and also a front garden. Both gardens are predominantly laid to lawn. It is our opinion that this would make a superb family home for a couple or growing young family. It could also an excellent investment property that could generate a healthy rental yield with an estimated potential rent of £900.00 per calendar month that could be achieved. A viewing of this property is highly recommended.

    • LOCATION

      The bustling Market Town of Witney lies some 12 miles west of Oxford City and is well situated for access to the A40, A34 and M4 & M40 motorways. The vibrant and rapidly expanding Town has a wide range of shops cantered round the sympathetically designed Woolgate Centre, traditional High Street and new central pedestrian shopping mall with major shopping brands including Marks & Spencer, Debenhams and Multi-Screen 3d cinema. The Market Square opens onto the delightful Church Green with St. Mary’s Church dominating the south side. Other amenities in the Town include supermarkets, banks, two Secondary schools and numerous Primary schools, a vast array of restaurants and public houses, a very good daily bus service into Oxford and just on the outskirts of the town is Witney lakes Resorts with golf course, health club and swimming pool. There is also a central community sports centre within the town itself.
