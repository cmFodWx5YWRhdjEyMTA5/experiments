  • DESCRIPTION

    An individual detached house with flexible and extremely versatile accommodation standing in a good sized established plot with south facing garden. This spacious family sized property has to be viewed internally to get full appreciation of the internal space available and the overall size of the plot. The property is set back from the road on the village edge yet within a short walk of the sought after Newland Street and nearby village centre. The house boasts a large garage with a high roof pitch, currently giving ample storage but also conversion possibilities for a home office/studio, subject to the usual consents. There is also driveway parking for several cars. Internally there is a large L-Shaped reception area with a good sized dual aspect Sitting Room which opens in to the Dining Room giving clear views over the rear garden through patio doors. The Breakfast Kitchen is also a good size with access to the side garden. To the front of the property on the ground floor is the fifth Bedroom with en-suite, currently used as a Study. There are four further first floor Bedrooms with en-suite to the Master and an additional family Bathroom. Heating is gas fired to radiators. The Willows is a very appealing family house with considerably larger accommodation than first view of the front suggests. We strongly recommend an internal inspection for those seeking a more individual home in this highly desirable village setting some 6 miles west of Oxford. Eynsham is a very sought after West Oxfordshire village with an excellent range of local facilities and a fantastic regular bus service into both Oxford and Witney, each some 6 miles distant. Shops & Facilities in the village include CO-OP and Spar shops, Butcher, Bakery, Greengrocer, Off-Licence, Post Office, The Eynsham Emporium and Cookshop, Electrical shop, Library, modern Health Centre and a handful of traditional pubs. The village also has toddler groups, a children's centre, a Primary School and the highly reputable Bartholomew Secondary School. The active and friendly local community is buoyed by sports clubs and a large number of clubs and societies catering for all interests.

  • OUTSIDE

    Ample driveway parking for several vehicles at the front, raised shrub beds, wide planted area at the side of the House with numerous shrubs and semi-mature trees and a width of some 16', cold water tap and gated access to the south facing rear garden. The rear garden can also be accessed from the other side of the property so one has access to all sides of the house. The rear garden measures approx. 85 x 50'. The established garden is well tended and has a paved terrace, lawn, very well stocked borders, mature Cypress screen along the eastern boundary, enclosed by timber fencing on three sides. A large garage at the front of the property with electric light and power, up and over door, personal door to side and a high pitched roof giving a large amount of storage (not boarded). Internal measurement of 16'8 x 14'4. NB. The garage has conversion possibilities (subject to planning) and could make a great annexe/home office/studio.
