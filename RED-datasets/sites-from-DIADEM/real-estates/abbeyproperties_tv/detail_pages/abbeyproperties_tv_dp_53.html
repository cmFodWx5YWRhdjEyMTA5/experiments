  • DESCRIPTION

    A fabulous detached 5 Bedroom family house in what has to be one of the best positions on the edge of this recently built development and within a short walk of the old village centre via a nearby footpath. The property particularly benefits from a private gated driveway with double garage and a larger than average carefully designed rear garden with a pleasant established outlook towards Orchard Close. The house is built to the Frampton design being the largest house type on the development. The accommodation comprises a spacious and welcoming Reception Hall, Sitting Room, Family Room, Study, a superb open plan Kitchen/Diner with doors to the garden and integrated appliances, Utility Room, Cloakroom, 2 En-Suite Bedrooms, 3 further Bedrooms, Family Bathroom. Further benefits include gas CH and the remainder of a 10 year new-build warranty. In our view this property has so much to offer for those seeking a larger family sized house in this very desirable village some 6 miles from Oxford. Viewing is highly recommended. Eynsham is a very sought after West Oxfordshire village with an excellent range of local facilities and a fantastic daily bus service into both Oxford and Witney, each some 6 miles distant. Shops & Facilities in the village include CO-OP and Spar shops, Butcher, Off-Licence, Post Office, The Eynsham Emporium and Cookshop, Electrical shop, Library, modern Health Centre and a handful of traditional pubs. The village also has toddler groups, a Primary School, and the highly reputable Bartholomew Secondary School. The active and friendly local community is buoyed by sports clubs and a large number of clubs and societies catering for all interests and age groups. Further information is available on the community website – www.eynsham-pc.gov.uk.
