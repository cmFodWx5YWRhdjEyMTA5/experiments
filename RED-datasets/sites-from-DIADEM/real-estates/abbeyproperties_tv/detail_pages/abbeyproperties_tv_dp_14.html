  • DESCRIPTION

    A very attractive modern cottage style property in a small courtyard style setting of just eight properties. The Rickyard is a sympathetically designed terrace with a pleasant pedestrian approach at the front and a garage block tucked away at the rear of the properties. The property is offered for sale with vacant possession and does offer some scope for internal improvement. The accommodation includes 3 Bedrooms, En-Suite Bathroom, Cloakroom, Sitting Room opening to a Conservatory and Kitchen. Outside is a small front garden and gated side access to the rear garden. The village of Fulbrook lies just outside the sought after Cotswold Town of Burford and is surrounded by open countryside. The village has a pub, village hall and church and Burford with it's delightful selection of shops, pubs and general facilities can be easily reached on foot. The village is well placed with road links to Oxford, Witney, Cheltenham, Stow and Chipping Norton.
