  • LOCATION

    Eynsham is a very sought after West Oxfordshire village with an excellent range of local facilities and a fantastic daily bus service into both Oxford and Witney, each some 6 miles distant. Shops & Facilities in the village include CO-OP and Spar shops, Butcher, Bakery, Greengrocer, Off-Licence, Post Office, The Eynsham Emporium and Cookshop, Electrical shop, Library, modern Health Centre and a handful of traditional pubs. The village also has toddler groups, a children's centre, a Primary School, and the highly reputable Bartholomew Secondary School. The active and friendly local community is buoyed by sports clubs and a large number of clubs and societies catering for all ages groups and interests.

This style of property is very sought after and always considered an ideal investment buy. This particular house being ideal for that purpose due to the updated and carefully maintained interior. Offered for sale with no onward chain, early viewing is advised.
