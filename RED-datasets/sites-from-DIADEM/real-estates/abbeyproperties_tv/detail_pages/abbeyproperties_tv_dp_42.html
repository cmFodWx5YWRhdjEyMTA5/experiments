  • Description

    A substantial 5 bedroom detached chalet style bungalow that boasts flexible accommodation approaching 2500 square feet. Set on an unusually large plot with generous gardens and outside space, the property is located close to the town centre. Depending on how the accommodation is used it could be classed as 5 bedrooms with 4 reception areas or 4 bedrooms with 5 reception areas. The majority of the bedrooms are located to the front with the reception areas to the rear. Adding to the living accommodation on the ground floor is a good sized bathroom with white suite, a large utility room and the cloakroom is located off. The first floor consists of the main bedroom which has en-suite facility that is still to be completed at the time of writing (Mid April 2014). There is further eve’s storage within the roof areas. Externally the front gardens are mainly laid to lawn with the driveway parking for up to 4 cars that runs to the side of the property achieving vehicle access to the rear of the property that could accommodate Horsebox, Motorhome, or Caravan storage there required. Through large double gates to the rear garden the driveway opens up to a wider area of hard standing and this area in turn gives access to the tandem length garage. Beyond this area is the principal garden which again is mainly laid to lawn with various shrubs and small trees and this area extends c.84’ x 48’. It is our opinion that this property represents highly versatile accommodation with excellent access to local amenities and would best suit those looking for town centre accommodation or large /growing families. A viewing of this property is highly advised as properties of this nature and in this location are fairly rare to the market.
