  • DESCRIPTION

    A stone fronted Cottage style terraced House in a lovely old village location amongst period properties and close to all local facilities including the Library, Post Office and the popular Eynsham Emporium. The property has 3 Bedrooms, Hallway, double aspect Sitting/Dining Room, Kitchen, first floor Bathroom, gas c/h and a pleasant rear garden with back access. The house also benefits from the rare advantage of a garage at the rear that is highly unusual for central Eynsham. This small number of properties offer the location and styling of a cottage coupled with the convenience of a house with more modern construction. These properties aren't readily available on the open market and whilst some updating is now required viewing is advised for those seeking a central Eynsham property. FOR SALE WITH NO ONWARD CHAIN. Eynsham is a very sought after West Oxfordshire village with an excellent range of local facilities and a fantastic daily bus service into both Oxford and Witney, each some 6 miles distant. Shops & Facilities in the village include CO-OP and Spar shops, Butcher, Bakery, Greengrocer, Off-Licence, Post Office, The Eynsham Emporium and Cookshop, Electrical shop, Library, modern Health Centre and a handful of traditional pubs. The village also has toddler groups, a children's centre, a Primary School, and the highly reputable Bartholomew Secondary School. The active and friendly local community is buoyed by sports clubs and a large number of clubs and societies catering for all ages and interests.
