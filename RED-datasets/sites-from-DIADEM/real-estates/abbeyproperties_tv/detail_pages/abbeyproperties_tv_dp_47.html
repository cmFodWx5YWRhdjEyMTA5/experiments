    • DESCRIPTION

      A 4 Bedroom detached property located on a select, exclusive cul-de-sac development within close proximity to local amenities and shops. Decorated and maintained to a very high standard this property offers larger than average family accommodation, well sized front and rear gardens, gated parking and double garage. Viewing of this property is highly recommended. The accommodation comprises of a spacious Entrance Hall with under stairs storage cupboard, solid wood flooring, a dual aspect Sitting Room with a walk in bay window to the front, stone fireplace with gas fire and French doors to the rear Garden. The Dining room can be accessed from the Hall or Kitchen and has a walk in bay window to the front. The handmade Bespoke Kitchen is a wonderful room with slate flooring, granite worktops and a central island with an electric pull out point. The family area can easily accommodate 2 sofas and a coffee table and has French doors to the rear Garden patio area. The Utility room has the same handmade units and the granite worktop as the Kitchen. There is a door to the back garden. The newly updated cloakroom is very modern and contemporary with a window to the rear. Upstairs are 4 double Bedrooms all with fitted double wardrobes. The Master Bedroom Suite has a Dressing area with 2 sets of wardrobes, and an En-suite Shower Room. The second Bedroom also has an En-suite Shower room. The Family Bathroom has both a Shower and a Bath and all of the Bathrooms have slate flooring, heated towel rails and large shower cubicles. Outside the front garden has gated parking for 4 cars outside the Double Garage which has power and lighting. There is a rear door to the back garden. The rear garden can be accessed through a side gate located by the garage. It is divided into several areas including a patio area , large decked area behind the garage and a second area of decking which is currently used for a large trampoline. There is a further gravelled area to the side of the house. The rest of the Garden is laid to lawn and has well stocked borders with shrubs and several trees and is not directly overlooked.

    • LOCATION

      Carterton is a major town in the area with excellent shopping, educational and sporting facilities. There are numerous shops, supermarkets, primary and secondary schools, public library, public houses and local bus services to outlying areas. The major towns of Witney, Oxford and Swindon are within easy reach by car and there is a regular coach service to Oxford and Witney and a daily service to Swindon.
