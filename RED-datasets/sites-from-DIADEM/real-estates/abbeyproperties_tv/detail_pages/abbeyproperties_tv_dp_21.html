  • DESCRIPTION

    A centrally situated detached house in an established non-estate location within a short walk of the excellent village amenities. The property offers 3 Bedrooms, L-Shaped Sitting/Dining Room, refitted Kitchen, replacement double glazing and a garage in a block close by. The house does require some modernisation but has a great deal of scope to update and improve without the need for major refurbishment. The price has been set at a realistic level to allow for said works and represents good value in the current market. There is no onward chain and early viewing is advised. Eynsham is a very sought after West Oxfordshire village with an excellent range of local facilities and a fantastic daily bus service into both Oxford and Witney, each some 6 miles distant. Shops & Facilities in the village include CO-OP and Spar shops, Butcher, Bakery, Greengrocer, Off-Licence, Post Office, The Eynsham Emporium and Cookshop, Electrical shop, Library, modern Health Centre and a handful of traditional pubs. The village also has toddler groups, a children's centre, a Primary School, and the highly reputable Bartholomew Secondary School. The active and friendly local community is buoyed by sports clubs and a large number of clubs and societies catering for all age groups and interests.
