    • LOCATION

      Aston is a very popular village south of Witney with many excellent facilities including a primary school, nursery, garage, village store, post office, village hall, pottery, shop/cafe, Church, playing field and public house. The nearby town of Bampton offers a variety of facilities including a bank, post office, Budgens store, health centre and a variety of Inns. A bus service runs through Aston to Witney Town, which has a much wider selection of shops and general amenities then onward routes to Oxford and London.
