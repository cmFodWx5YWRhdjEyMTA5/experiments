  • Description

    A very well situated end of terrace Cottage tucked away at the end of a lane in a very sought after part of the village. The Cottage has been extended at the rear and also has the significant advantage of off-road parking at the side, a rare commodity for period properties of this type. The accommodation is arranged over three floors and having been tenanted for a number of years the property would now benefit from some updating and general improvement. Sitting Room, good sized Breakfast Kitchen, 3 Bedrooms, Bathroom, gas CH, rear garden, private parking. This property has a great deal to offer in an extremely good location and viewing is advised. NO ONWARD CHAIN.

  • Location

    Eynsham is a very sought after West Oxfordshire village with an excellent range of local facilities and a fantastic daily bus service into both Oxford and Witney, each some 6 miles distant. Shops & Facilities in the village include CO-OP and Spar shops, Butcher, Bakery, Greengrocer, Off-Licence, Post Office, The Eynsham Emporium and Cookshop, Electrical shop, Library, modern Health Centre and a handful of traditional pubs. The village also has toddler groups, a children's centre, a Primary School, and the highly reputable Bartholomew Secondary School. The active and friendly local community is buoyed by sports clubs and a large number of clubs and societies catering for all age groups and interests.
