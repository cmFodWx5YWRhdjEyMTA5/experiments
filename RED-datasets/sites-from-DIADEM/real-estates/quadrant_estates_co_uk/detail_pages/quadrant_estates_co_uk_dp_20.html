Property Details

A well presented two bedroom Character Cottage in a town centre location and offered with no onward chain. The property boasts character features throughout and includes a living room, kitchen/breakfast room, refitted shower room, two bedrooms.
Hardwood door opening to:

LIVING ROOM: 11’5 x 11’9.
Stairs rising to first floor. Exposed beams. Open working fireplace with tiled hearth and wooden surrounds. TV point. Telephone point. Double glazed window to front aspect. Radiator. Access to fuse board. Room thermostat. Door opening to:

KITCHEN/BREAKFAST ROOM: 11’5 x 11’7.
Comprising a range of units to both floor and eye level with matching roll edge work surface. Tiled splash backs. Stainless steel sink unit single drainer. Stainless steel oven with electric hob over. Stainless steel extractor. Space for fridge/freezer. Space and plumbing for washing machine. Ceramic tiled flooring. Radiator. Double glazed window to rear aspect. Door opening to Courtyard.

LANDING:
Doors to bedrooms and bathroom. Access to loft space. Door to airing cupboard housing hot water tank.

BEDROOM ONE: 8’2 x 12’3.
Built in over stair double wardrobe. Double glazed window to front aspect. Radiator.

BEDROOM TWO: 8’11 x 7’10.
Double glazed window to rear aspect. Radiator.

FAMILY BATHROOM:
Refitted suite of low-level WC, Wash hand basin, panel bath with independent shower unit over. Radiator. Double glazed opaque window to rear aspect. Extractor fan.

OUTSIDE THE PROPERTY:

REAR COURTYARD:
Mono block courtyard garden.

FRONT:
Open plan front garden, mainly laid to lawn.
Storage shed with power.

* The current owner rents a garage close by; there would be the option to take over the agreement *
