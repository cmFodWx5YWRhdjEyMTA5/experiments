On MarketPink & Black Property Consultants - 0186551591900000624
    Classical elegance needs little explanation. First impressions count and you won’t be disappointed at this hidden oasis. History, high ceiling and breath-taking views are the order of the day. Steeped in history and beautifully preserved, a gravel drive leads you to a gothic mullioned entrance which looks out across the lawns and mature planting which makes sure you have plenty of privacy when battling to win that game of croquet. There are many original features to discover; lovingly restored and cared for over the years there are stunning fireplaces, oak doors and flooring, cornicing and some truly magnificent stained glass windows. The current owners are a close knit family and there are three generations sharing the house, great care has been taken in the arrangement of the accommodation that if you wanted to return the house for use by one family it would simple. There are 3 kitchens, the farmhouse style kitchen is just as it should be, a large window overlooking the garden with enough room for a farmhouse table for informal kitchen suppers. The kitchen in the second floor apartment has the feel of a luxury London apartment, again room for a large table, this is a room that screams 'serious chef lives here'. The two apartments have a drawing room, 2 bedrooms, family bathroom and cloakroom. There is further accommodation which would provide a kitchen, drawing room and three bedrooms. As a single family home you would have room for six bedrooms on the first floor and five receptions rooms. The grounds are a delight, overlooking paddocks, a glebe and towards woodland, this has to be one of the most stunning positions in Oxford.
