Church Cowley Rd
Florence Pk OX4

£375,000
Sold

Located along a popular residential road, this outstanding four bedroomed semi-detached house benefits from a complete interior refurbishment and generously proportioned rooms.

The property comprises spacious front reception, rear extended contemporary eat-in kitchen with decking area leading to a private garden, Central Island, four well proportioned bedrooms, family bathroom, second shower room with WC, utility area and newly paved off-street parking for two vehicles.

The property is located close to a fantastic range of shops, restaurant and bars in Temple Cowley, while the open green space of Florence Park is close by.

Local transport links are available nearby for access to Oxford (National Rail) train station, while road users have close access to A34 (M40).
