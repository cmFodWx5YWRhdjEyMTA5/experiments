A three bedroom, single storey, barn conversion set in open countryside at the end of a quiet lane. The property boasts many original features such as exposed beams and stonework. Accommodation comprises a spacious sitting room with a solid fuel burning stove set in a charming feature fireplace, a large country kitchen, three double bedrooms, an en-suite and a bathroom. Outside there is a substantial garden with an attractive patio area with far reaching views to the River Cherwell. For those looking for a rural retreat we strongly recommend a viewing.
