A three bedroom modern cottage in a stunning village location with wonderful views of the Oxfordshire countryside beyond. The property is ideal for a professional couple and benefits from a dining room and sitting room separated by a feature fireplace, kitchen, boot room and cloakroom on the ground floor. The first floor comprises a master bedroom, two further double bedrooms and a bathroom with separate lavatory. Outside the property enjoys a substantial rear garden with an apple tree and timber built summer house, while to the front there is off road parking for multiple vehicles. This property is available immediately, and is unfurnished.
