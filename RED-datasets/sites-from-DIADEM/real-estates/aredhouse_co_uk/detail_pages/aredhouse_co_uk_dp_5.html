A charming four bedroom stone country cottage set in a quiet village location. This family home enjoys period features including an Inglenook fireplace with log burner, exposed wall and ceiling beams, exposed internal stonework and wood flooring. The ground floor comprises charcterful living room, light kitchen with exposed brick which opens to the dining area, a study, cloakroom and a lovely garden room. The character continues on the first floor with Velux windows providing extra light to the four bedrooms and family bathroom. Outside there is a private rear garden and off road parking to the front. The village of Freeland provides excellent school catchment and great transport links along with a village pub.
