New to the rental market is this beautiful four bedroom character cottage situated in a pretty no through road within the highly sought after village of Islip. The house, with its abundance of historic features, enjoys modern finishes with a luxury kitchen with all necessary White goods, modern bathrooms with power showers and has just been freshly decorated throughout. On the ground floor there is a large sitting room with an open fireplace and a farmhouse kitchen. Over the next two floors there are three double bedrooms, a single bedroom and two bath/shower rooms. This property is offered either Unfurnished or Fully Furnished. The southerly rear garden has a lovely entertaining area, a well tended lawn and beds and a detached timber build shed/workshop. From the rear of the garden there is direct access to beautiful countryside walks and the nearby Millennium Wood. There are two pubs in the village, a shop and a thriving community.
