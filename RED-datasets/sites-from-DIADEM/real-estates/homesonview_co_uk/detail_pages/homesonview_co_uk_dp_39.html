Description

A beautiful freehold, terrace house located just minutes away from Connaught Village, close to Hyde Park and Kensington Gardens. The property comprises a large west facing terrace, two reception rooms, three en suite bedrooms, further cloakroom and kitchen, with a self- contained studio including kitchen, shower room and studio room. Southwick Street is located in the heart of Church Commissioners Hyde Park Estate, providing easy access to the shopping and amenities of Marble Arch, Oxford Circus and Edgeware Road.
