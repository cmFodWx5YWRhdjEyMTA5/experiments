Description

A recently constructed, beautifully appointed split level bungalow incorporating the finest of design led contemporary fixtures and finishes. The property occupies a sheltered coastal position on a western headland dominated by impressive fortifications, from which the house takes its name. Sitting beside a protected RAMSAR site, the grounds reach down to the water's edge and include gardens, a pine wood and historic sites of military significance
