Description

An impressive substantial country house set at the end of a long undulating driveway in grounds of approximately 10 acres or thereabouts. Attractive covered porch, full height reception hall, drawing room, family room/sitting room, living kitchen, study, walk in larder, side hallway, cloakroom, utility room, shower room, master bedroom with shower room en-suite, 4 further bedrooms, family bathroom, boiler room, attached outbuilding with open garage, garden store, main attached outbuilding with covered front verandah, lobby and 2 rooms.
