Description

Situated within the acclaimed Park Avenue address in central Harpenden and offering spacious accommodation, this 6 bedroom family home sits within a south backing plot which is stunningly landscaped.Accommodation includes 6 bedroom suites, 4 reception rooms, staff accommodation, a spectacular kitchen/family/dining area with Sub Zero and Wolf appliances and a double garage.The area is renowned for its excellent local schooling both in the private and state sectors which are both close by. Harpenden village and train station are less than 0.3 mile whilst the M25 and M1 are also easily accessible.
