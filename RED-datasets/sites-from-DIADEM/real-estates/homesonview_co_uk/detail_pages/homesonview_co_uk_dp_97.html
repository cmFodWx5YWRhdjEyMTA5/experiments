Description

Location, location, location !! So true and it can be found in this magnificent spot, which offers absolute seclusion. Approximately five acres of fabulous gardens and woodland, stepping down to the river Bollin and looking onto Tom Field Bank Brook. This fabulous family home has been upgraded over recent years to provide traditional and spacious accommodation. However there is further scope and potential to develop/extend (subject to the usual consents) and there are substantial outbuildings that could also lend themselves to leisure facilities, granny annex etc. Not visible from Barrow Lane, the property can only be truly appreciated by a proper inspection, so we would encourage viewings in order to understand all that is on offer!!!
