Description

Rarely available, a magnificent 7 bedroom, 6 bathroom character home sitting on a plot of around 1.45 acres and set behind electric gates. The property comprises just under 6000sq ft of spacious and flexible accommodation, including a bespoke custom built, solid oak kitchen, games room, gymnasium, sauna and private covered balcony to the master bedroom suite. This unique property further encompasses extensive and south facing stunning gardens which include a heated outdoor pool and wonderful, uninterrupted views over countryside beyond.
