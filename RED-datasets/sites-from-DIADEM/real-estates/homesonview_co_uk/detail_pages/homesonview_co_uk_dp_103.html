Description

An outstanding and fully extended Victorian family home, finished to the highest specification and located in unquestionably one of the very best areas of Parsons Green. Arranged over four floors this large five bedroom period home, just over 2,700 sq ft (252 sq m), offers superb reception and entertaining space in the heart of Parsons Green. The basement level (with high ceilings) comprises a reception room with a very useful and additional kitchen/utility facility. There's also a good sized bedroom with en suite shower room on this level. Laterally occupying the entire ground floor is an impressive and spacious double reception room that flows across into a fully extended modern kitchen and a private south west facing rear garden, accessed through bi-folding doors from the kitchen. The master bedroom suite is situated on the first floor and includes built in storage and an en suite shower room. There's also an additional double bedroom and shower room on this level with two further double bedrooms and a large family bathroom on the floor above. Located in an extremely desirable street only a stones throw from both Eel Brook Common and Parsons Green, and under 500 metres from Parsons Green underground station (District Line, Zone 2), Parthenia Road is well located in the heart of Parsons Green (Fulham's most desirable area). Property of this type and in this specific location rarely comes available and in our opinion it would make a solid investment for the future. There's also plenty of shops, independent cafés and restaurants all within a very short walk (including a Waitrose supermarket on 'the Green' itself). The large open spaces of Eel brook Common are also only 150 metres away and Fulham Broadway underground station (District Line, Zone 2) and the shops, cinema, gyms (David Lloyd) and bars, including a brand new Whole Foods supermarket are also only 10 minutes walk away.
