Description

A substantial plot comprising of a detached barn conversion providing up to 6 bedrooms and offering the buyer a number of flexible options including a character cottage annexe providing an income of around £1,650.00 pcm or additional family accommodation. It is believed that parts of this property date back to as early as the 1500's and was then extended in the 1700's, and sits on a plot of approximately 10 acres (subject to formal measuring). Set behind gates, this unique property offers privacy and seclusion and benefits from a garage, stable block and extensive grounds further comprising 3 paddocks and additional workshops/storage facilities.
