Description

We are delighted to bring to the market this stunning detached Grade ll listed house, designed by Ernest Newton and built by WIlliam Willett which has gone through a programme of extensive refurbishment and is one of the six original houses that were built in Camden Park Road at that time and has one of the larger plots of just under an acre.Very convenient for the commuter with Elmstead Woods station under a mile, with train times from 17 minutes to London Bridge and 24 minutes to Cannon Street.The refurbishment programme which took some 5 years to complete began in 2007, and includes new extensions, new roof with re-pointed chimney stacks. The kitchen and study have beautiful Chambers fitted furniture, and the bathrooms are of a contemporary design, with the Master ensuite presented on two levels giving a spa like feel, which must be a joy after a busy stressful day. The family room, kitchen, study, TV room and all bathrooms have the benefit of under floor heating. Outside the house is approached by a large sweeping driveway and has a double garage. The gardens to the rear are a delight and a patio laid with Indian sandstone paving has lighting and leads to a well stocked and mature garden incorporating an area where the newly resurfaced floodlit tennis court stands, and from there is a secret garden which at one time had a putting green complete with bunkers which we understand could be reinstated quite easily but a really great space to develop with maybe a swimming pool subject to the necessary consents.This property certainly wouldn't disappoint and should go to the top of your viewing list.
