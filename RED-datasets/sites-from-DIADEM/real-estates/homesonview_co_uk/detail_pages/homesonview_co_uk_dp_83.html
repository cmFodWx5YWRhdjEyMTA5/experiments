Description

Swinsty Hall is a beautiful Grade 1 listed family home of distinction and features a stunning updated interior with detached cottage in its impressive grounds offering reservoir views.Updated to an exacting standard this period character home offers well planned accommodation that works for everyday living!Set in approx 4.5 ares of manicured formal grounds the main house features approx 8000 sq ft plus a detached two bedroomed cottage ideal for relatives or staff. This admired family home is located some 9 miles to the west of Harrogate where there are a wide choice of amenities including schooling for all age groups. Additionally there are good road and rail networks connecting the commuter and traveller to Leeds, York and London. Leeds Bradford International airport is only 12 miles away approx for the Heathrow and other international flights.Impressive covered entrance to Grand Hall with baronial fireplace currently used for guest and family occasion's. Stunning drawing room, study, bespoke living kitchen with Aga with sitting room, dining room, guest cloaks and secondary porch.At lower ground floor there is a laundry room providing a well arranged space for family requirements with additional store rooms and the all important wine cellar!At first floor there are five bedrooms, three bathrooms and a dressing room offering flexibly arranged family accommodation with far reaching views of the grounds and reservoir.At second floor there is a further double bedroom with luxury shower room with separate toilet. The gem at the top of this amazing home is a cinema/leisure room that features electric reclining cinema seats and and a home cinema system for those cosy nights in. The grounds are approached by sweeping driveways and feature manicured lawns and well designed Elizabethan style gardens. There is a wooded copse and plenty of playing space for children on safe open lawns.There is a detached cottage for relatives, staff or guests in the grounds offering two bedrooms and a kitchen with sitting room. Additionally there is a 77 ft long Barn that forms a useful store area or subject to planning leisure facility or further accommodation if required.
