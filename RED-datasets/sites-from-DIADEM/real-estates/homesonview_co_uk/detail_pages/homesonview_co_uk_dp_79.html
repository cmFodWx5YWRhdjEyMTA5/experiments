Description

Situated within the sought after Bickley Park Estate and occupying a plot of just under one acre, this fine example of an Arts & Craft property designed by renowned architect Charles Quennell comes to the market for the first time in nearly 40 years.As one would expect of a property of this era, the accommodation, which is arranged over 3 floors, is well proportioned with high ceilings, large windows and an abundance of charm and character. At the heart of the property is a grand entrance hall with an elegant staircase ascending to the upper floors. To the ground floor there is the bonus of an annex which was formerly a surgery but which could either be incorporated into the main property or refurbished to create independent living for an elderly relative or au pair. The west facing rear garden extends to nearly 200ft and is lovingly tended with well stocked mature borders and lawns and the wide frontage provides plentiful off street parking.With Bickley and Chislehurst stations close by and a number of schools (both private and state) in the vicinity, this property is ideal for a large or growing family. Early viewing of this exceptional house is urged at your earliest convenience to avoid disappointment.
