Description

Originally built as the show house for Keston Park in the mid 1920's, Furzefield is one of the premier properties in this highly desirable private park. The magnificent detached family home sits in approximately 1 acre of landscaped grounds, which include a heated, kidney shaped, swimming pool. The property is approached via two pairs of remote controlled security gates to a carriage driveway, which continues through to the detached garage block, incorporating a pool room with sauna, shower, cloakroom and changing facilities. Well screened from Holwood Park Avenue, the gardens, which have been professionally landscaped, include sweeping lawns, pretty flower beds, borders, mature trees, rockery and water feature.The accommodation with gas fired central heating and double glazing briefly comprises spacious reception hall, large drawing room and dining room, an impressive kitchen/breakfast room with separate utility and conservatory/leisure room. The first floor comprises four double bedrooms, family bathroom and en-suite bathroom with dressing area to the master bedroom.Holwood Park Avenue is situated within walking distance of Locksbottom village.
