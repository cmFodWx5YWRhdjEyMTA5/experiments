Description

A substantial period residence located in one of Alderley Edge's most sought after secluded settings in the heart of the conservation area. Reception hallway, leisure and study/office, living/dining/entertaining room, side hallway, rear reception hallway, playroom, entertaining/drawing room, study, cloakroom, living kitchen, living area, side hallway, utility room, store room off, boiler room, leisure with fully tiled swimming/plunge pool with curved Jacuzzi integrated sitting area, gym, solarium room, playroom/2nd gym, master bedroom suite with bathroom en-suite and dressing room, 2 further bathrooms, 6 further bedrooms, 3 further en-suites, box room/airing room, 2 garages.
