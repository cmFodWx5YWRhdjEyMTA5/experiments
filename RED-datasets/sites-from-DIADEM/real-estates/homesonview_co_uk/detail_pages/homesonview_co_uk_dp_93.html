Description

Magnificent, totally restored 13th/18th century chateau with every modern comfor. Stunning panoramic views of countryside & distant Pyrenees. 20 ha land & forest (further 100ha available). 800m2 habitable area comprising entrance hall, 2 salons, dining room, large kitchen in the former "grand salon", little kitchen, 7 bedrooms each with en-suite bathroom. Numerous beautiful outbuildings. South and east facing terraces, 7x14m heated swimming pool. Beautiful garden with 17th/18thc roses, 700m driveway bordered by old oaks.
