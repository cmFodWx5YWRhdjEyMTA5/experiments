SEARCHING FOR PROPERTIES

Using the form at the top of the page:
  • Enter a search area (a town, county, or the first part of a post code e.g. B91 or SW1) - select an option from the matches that appear as you enter characters into the field.
  • Choose your price range
  • Choose any other search values using the dropdowns and checkboxes - for example the minimum number of bedrooms you require When happy click the 'Search' button
  • To perform a new search at any time, simply enter your details into the form and resubmit.

VIEWING YOUR RESULTS

If we have properties that meet your search requirements they will be displayed on the screen. You can view your results in a number of different results formats:
  • in a list
  • on a map
  • in a photo gallery
  • in a summary grid
  • Move between the results views by clicking on the results view buttons, above.
Having found a property that interests you, click the 'Full Details' button to find out more about a property. Please make use of the 'Contact Agent' form on the property results and full details pages to make direct contact with the estate agent for that property.

SAVE A PROPERTY

You can save properties to your saved property list by clicking on the 'Save' button on a property results or full details page. Your saved properties can then be seen by selecting the 'Saved Properties' tab, above, or through the 'My Site' area of the website.
