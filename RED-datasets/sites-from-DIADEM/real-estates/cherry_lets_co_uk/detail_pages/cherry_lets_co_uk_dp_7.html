Property Description

Immaculate 3 double bedroom Detached house in sought after location in Banbury.  Beautifully presented and tastefully decorated, this lovely house is finished to a very high specification.  Thought has been given to all fixtures and fittings to create a beautiful, stylish contemporary home.  The Large Modern Kitchen Diner has built in Zanussi appliances and a White Kitchen with Corian worktops.  The 24 foot Sitting room has a Wood burning Stove.  From the Kitchen and Living Room glazed doors lead to the large modern Conservatory.   There is also a Utility room.  On the first floor are the three double bedrooms, each with built in floor to ceiling wardrobes and a useful office.  The Large Bathroom is particularly luxurious with double ended bath and thermostatic drench style shower.  The property features Modern Lighting, Solid Oak Flooring and Cream carpets and benefits from Double Glazing and Gas Central Heating.

The large 86 foot garden is fully enclosed and has a good sized sunny Patio with steps to the lawn. The garden has well stocked borders and a path.  There is a screened area at the rear of the garden with shed and green house.  To the front is the driveway with parking for 2 cars and front garden with gated side access to the rear.  There is plenty of on street parking also.


Available:  End May 2014

EPC Rating: D

Council Tax Band: E

Deposit: £1695

**Cherry Lets Tenancy Fees are £285 including VAT for all tenants (one fee for all)** 

 

The market town of Banbury, famed for its Fine Lady and the Cross, has excellent facilities with a large shopping centre, good sporting facilities and a wide array of restaurants and public houses to suit all tastes.   Transport links are also excellent with a large bus depot and train station (London Marylebone 55 minutes;Birmingham 51 mins).  Along with its close proximity to the M40, Banbury is particularly good for commuting.
