1 Bedroom Flat To Rent Price £799 pcm +Fees

Lawrence Court, The Grove, Twyford

A 1 bedroom flat in the centre of Twyford village. Private pathway to own front door, living room, kitchen, double bedroom, bathroom, there is a gated entrance to courtyard private parking area, the flat comes with an allocated parking space.

* 1 double bedroom with wood effect laminate flooring
* Kitchen with electric hob & oven, fridge, washing machine
* Living room with wood effect laminate flooring

* Electric under floor heating
* Gated entrance to courtyard parking - 1 allocated car parking space
* Available unfurnished at £799 pcm - please telephone 0845 6 123 511 to arrange a viewing
