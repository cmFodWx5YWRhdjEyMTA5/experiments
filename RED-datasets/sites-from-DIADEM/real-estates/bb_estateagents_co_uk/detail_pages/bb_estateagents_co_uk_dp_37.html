An extended 3 bedroom detached home situated within walking distance of local amenities in the highly regarded village of Chinnor. The accommodation briefly comprises entrance hall, cloakroom, living room with log burner, dining room, kitchen/breakfast room, family room/large study, landing, 3 double bedrooms, master bedroom with en suite and family bathroom. Outside there is driveway parking, a sizeable garage and good size rear garden. The property also benefits from UPVC double glazing and is offered for sale with no upper chain.


Situated at the foot of the Chiltern Hills, Chinnor is a popular village conveniently situated on the Oxfordshire/Buckinghamshire borders. Within the village there are good local facilities including a variety of shops, churches, several pubs, post office, two doctor`s surgeries, a nursery and two primary schools. M40(Junction 6) is within 3 miles giving access to Oxford(20 miles), High Wycombe(13 miles) and London(48 miles). Princes Risborough station is within 5 miles (Marylebone 35 minutes).
Directions
From our office, proceed along Station Road and turn left at the roundabout into Church Road. Take the second left into Rectory Meadow and then take a slight left into Musgrave Road. The property can be found with one of our distinctive `For Sale` boards outside.
