A very well presented park home, only five years old and situated on a pleasant development in this delightful village with easy access to open countryside walks and Little Kimble station. Situated between Princes Risborough and Aylesbury, the property offers well appointed accommodation and parking. The accommodation comprises: entrance hall, sitting room, kitchen with built in appliances, double bedroom with built in wardrobes and bathroom, gas central heating, double glazing, garden and parking.

Little Kimble is a popular village at the foot of the Chiltern Hills and is situated close to areas of outstanding natural beauty. Within the village of Great Kimble there is a public house and a local village school, Princes Risborough is within 3 miles and Aylesbury 5 miles. There are numerous countryside walks and bridleways in the area.

There is easy access to the M40 motorway at junction 6, giving access to the M4 and M25 motorways. For the commuter there is a halt at Little Kimble station giving access to Princes Risborough station which offers mainline services to London, Marylebone in approximately 40 minutes.
