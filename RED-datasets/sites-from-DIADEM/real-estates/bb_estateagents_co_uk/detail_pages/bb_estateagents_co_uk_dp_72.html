A stunning 3 bedroom property situated in a cul de sac in the sought after village of Bierton offered for sale with no onward chain. The accommodation briefly comprises entrance hall, cloakroom, living room, dining room, fitted kitchen with intergated appliances, landing, 3 bedrooms, master bedroom with en suite and refitted bathroom. Outside there is a garage with driveway parking and enclosed landscaped rear garden. The property also benefits from gas central heating and double glazing.

Only about 1 mile distant from Aylesbury is a private residential area consisting of popular small Victorian cottages, together with larger executive type housing, all blending admirably with one another. There are two public houses and a good bus service connects Bierton with Leighton Buzzard and Aylesbury. The XIV Century Church of St. James is particularly noteworthy and there are views from the village across open countryside to the wooded Chiltern escarpment to the South. Aylesbury offers a wide range of shopping facilities and a mainline rail link to London (Marylebone).
