Dating back over 300 years, this substantial detached home has been extended and improved to provide generous, character accommodation including five bedrooms (one en-suite), three reception rooms and a large kitchen and breakfast room with picture windows taking full advantage of panoramic views across the Chiltern Hills. The main house is linked by a large conservatory to a converted barn providing an additional reception room with two bedrooms and a bathroom above.

Approached through a five bar gate, a long gravel driveway gives access to an extensive parking area. Outside the kitchen a stone paved patio provides outside dining space, again with views and opens onto an area of formal gardens with many mature shrubs and trees. The gardens adjoin a level paddock i total the grounds approach 4 acres (1.62ha). There is additional paddock land across Spriggs Holly Lane of approximately 7 acres (2.83 ha) which may be available separately.

GROUND FLOOR
The original porch opens into the dining hall which has a brick fireplace and beamed ceiling. To one end is access to a double aspect sitting room leading to the conservatory and then on to the barn. The kitchen is behind the dining hall and is beautifully fitted with a range of oak fronted units and work surfaces, integrated appliances and the Aga. An archway opens to the breakfast room with access through to the utility room and entrance hall. The barn provides an ideal games room or similar with glazed double doors onto the garden at the rear.

FIRST FLOOR
The landing gives access to a master bedroom with en-suite bathroom, two further double bedrooms, two single bedrooms and a beautifully appointed shower room. Above the barn are two further bedrooms and another shower room, all enjoying fabulous country views.
Directions
From M40 junction 5 follow A40 through Stokenchurch and after a further 1/4 mile fork left through Radnage, passing the Crown public house on the left. Continue down hill, bearing left and continue to the next road junction, bear left then after a very short distance bear right. At the next junction bear left and follow this lane uphill to a right hand bend at the top. Cabbage Hall Farm will be found after approx. 3/4 mile on the right.
