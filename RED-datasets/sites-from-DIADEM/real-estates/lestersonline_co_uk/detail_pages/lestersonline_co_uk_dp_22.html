Why we like it:
A rare opportunity to purchase a four bedroom semi situated within this sought after South Oxfordshire village with a lovely country pub overlooking the village green and cricket pitch. In an elevated location the large open plan kitchen/breakfast room is at the front. Large living room at the rear. Three double bedrooms, the rear top floor double bedroom overlooks the rear of the property which enjoys a pleasant outlook not overlooked.

What it offers:
The accommodation comprises; open plan entrance hall/dining area, fitted kitchen, 20` lounge, integral garage, four bedrooms and a bathroom with separate W.C. The front offers a drive for parking two vehicles and a garden with access to the rear of the property, this is south facing and of a manageable size. North Moreton offers good access to London via Didcot Parkway station which is within easy driving distance c. 10 minutes. Train time to London c. 45 minutes. The A34, M4 and M40 are also close by. The local primary school is regarded as one of the best in Oxfordshire. The village also offers a delightful public house which is located a short distance from the village recreation ground.

The location:
North Moreton is a vibrant South Oxfordshire village with `The Bear` public house, which has an excellent restaurant; this is a very pretty pub under local ownership and continually receives the Pub of the Year award and they hold all sorts of events within the gardens. Behind there is a large playing field with an active and well-known cricket club, football pitch and children`s playground. The mid-13th century church, All Saints`, described as the most perfect medieval church in Oxfordshire, is also in the village.
Tesco and Sainsbury`s are within five minutes drive, as is Didcot Parkway station, which gives an intercity fast train commuter service to London Paddington that takes 40 minutes. There is also a Waitrose in the market town of Wallingford, which lies on the Thames, which is approximately 10 minutes drive. A farmers` market is held regularly in Didcot within the area of the Orchard Centre which has a number of quality shops and there is also a recently opened Arts Centre and a multi screen cinema complex. The surrounding countryside is noted to be of outstanding rural beauty and footpaths are within easy access enabling very pretty walks through open countryside. This is also excellent riding country with the Downs giving access to gallops, bridle paths and the Blewbury Riding Centre. There is a highly regarded village school in South Moreton. There are lots of restaurants in the area, including The Sweet Olive in Aston Tirrold which serves fine French food, as well as pub meals
