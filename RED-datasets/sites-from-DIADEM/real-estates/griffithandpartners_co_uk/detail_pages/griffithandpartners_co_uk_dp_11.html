4 Bedroom Link-Detached For Sale Guide Price £449,000

Pyrton Lane, Watlington
Available
Contact Watlington Office Office quoting Ref.1681
A well presented link detached house in quite courtyard setting, large reception room and kitchen, four double bedrooms, shower room, bathroom, cloakroom, a stunning, well stocked, South-West facing garden with a backdrop of St Leonards Church, garage and parking.
ACCOMMODATION - GROUND FLOOR:
Part-glazed obscured glass wooden door into:

ENTRANCE HALL:
Doors to cloaks cupboard, cloakroom, lounge, cupboard housing oil fired central heating boiler and kitchen, stairs rising to first floor, ceiling light point, wall light point, power points and radiator.

CLOAKS CUPBOARD: - 1.8m (5'11") x 1.5m (4'11")
Large storage space which could be made into a utility room, electric consumer unit, power points and ceiling light point.

CLOAKROOM:
Fitted with a white suite of wall mounted hand wash basin and low level WC, ceiling light point and UPVC double glazed obscured glass window to the front.

LOUNGE: - 5.4m (17'9") x 4.5m (14'9")
A good size double aspect reception room with UPVC double glazed window to the rear and UPVC double glazed sliding doors to the garden terrace, brick built fireplace with a tiled hearth and multi fuel stove, television aerial point, wall light points, power points and radiator.

KITCHEN: - 4.5m (14'9") x 4.4m (14'5")
A spacious room large enough to take table and chairs, fitted with a range of matching wall and base units housing cupboards and drawers, granite-effect work surfaces, inset white composite 1½ bowl sink/drainer unit with mixer tap, inset halogen hob with extractor hood over, built in double oven, space and plumbing for washing machine and dishwasher, ceramic tiled splash backs, ceiling light points, under unit lighting, power points, radiator, UPVC double glazed window to the front, UPVC double glazed sliding doors to the garden terrace and door to:

BEDROOM FOUR: - 3.3m (10'10") x 2.5m (8'2")
Double room ideal for guests, elderly relative or teenage child, UPVC double glazed window to the front, television aerial point, ceiling light point, power points, radiator and sliding door to:

EN SUITE SHOWER ROOM:
Fitted with a white suite of ceramic tiled walk in shower, pedestal hand wash basin and low level WC, ceiling light point and radiator.

FIRST FLOOR - LANDING:
Doors to bedrooms 1, 2 and 3, bathroom and airing cupboard housing hot water tank, recessed ceiling down lights, power point and radiator.

BEDROOM ONE: - 4.5m (14'9") x 2.6m (8'6")
An lovely bright and airy double aspect double room with UPVC double glazed windows to the rear and side, fitted wardrobes, drawers, dressing table and bed side tables, ceiling light point and power points.

BEDROOM TWO: - 4m (13'1") x 2.7m (8'10")
Double room with two UPVC double glazed windows to the side, fitted wardrobes and drawers, ceiling light points and power points.

BEDROOM THREE: - 3m (9'10") x 2.7m (8'10")
Double room with UPVC double glazed window to the side, hatch to loft space, ceiling light point and power points.

BATHROOM: - 2.2m (7'3") x 1.7m (5'7")
Smartly fitted with a white suite of panel sided bath with electric shower over and glazed side screen, vanity unit with hand wash basin, concealed cistern WC and storage cupboards, recessed ceiling down lights and UPVC double glazed obscured glass window to the front.

OUTSIDE:
The property is approached via a high metal gate with a gravel bordered pathway leading to the front door, mature shrubs, smart bin and log storage and outside lighting.

The most beautifully kept rear garden has a curved raised patio area with plenty of space for outside dining and entertaining, small step down to a neatly manicured lawn bordered by very pretty, well stocked flower and shrub beds, a second paved seating area, large wooden summer house, green house, oil tank neatly hidden behind a rose covered trellis, outside lighting and tap, enclosed by brick walls and wood panel fencing with a gate giving access for a short cut to the church and town.

GARAGE AND PARKING:
Single garage next to the property with metal up and over door. Parking space for one car in front.
