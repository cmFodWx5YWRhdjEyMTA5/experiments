2 Bedroom Cottage For Sale Guide Price £254,995

Shirburn Street, Watlington
Sold
Contact Watlington Office Office quoting Ref.700

SITUATION:
Situated in the heart of Watlington, the property is just a stone`s throw away from the thriving heart of this market town. Watlington lies at the foot of the Chilterns and has a selection of amenities, including primary and secondary schools, doctor`s surgery, library, sporting activities and a range of shops covering everyday requirements. Access to the M40 motorway at Junction 6 for easy access to High Wycombe, London, Birmingham and Heathrow airport. Oxford, Reading, Wallingford and Henley-on-Thames are within a 16 mile radius.

DIRECTIONS:
From our office in Watlington, walk up to the top of the High Street and then left into Shirburn Street. No 30 is a little way up on the right hand side.
