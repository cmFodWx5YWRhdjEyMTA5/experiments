4 Bedroom Detached For Sale Guide Price £309,995

Brinkinfield Road, Chalgrove
Sold
Contact Watlington Office Office quoting Ref.1588
Situated on a corner plot on a popular residential road, this four bedroom detached house has no onward chain, large reception room, kitchen, shower room, bathroom, integral garage, parking, gardens to the front, side and rear, UPVC double glazing and gas central heating.
ACCOMMODATION - GROUND FLOOR:
UPVC half glazed obscured glass door into:

ENTRANCE HALL:
UPVC double glazed obscured glass window with UPVC panel below, doors to shower room and living room, ceiling light point and radiator.

SHOWER ROOM: - 3.4m (11'2") x 1.5m (4'11")
Fitted with a suite of corner shower cubicle with ceramic tiled walls, wall mounted hand wash basin and low level WC, ceiling light point, wall mounted electric heater, extractor fan, ceiling light point and obscured glass window to the side,

LIVING ROOM: - 5.7m (18'8") x 4.6m (15'1")
A bright and airy reception room with UPVC double glazed sliding doors and floor to ceiling window both to the rear garden, door to the kitchen, stairs rising to the first floor, stone built fireplace to one wall with terracotta tiled hearth, wooden mantle and electric fire, ceiling light point, power points, television aerial point, telephone point and radiator.

KITCHEN/BREAKFAST ROOM: - 4.5m (14'9") x 2.6m (8'6")
Fitted with a range of matching floor and base cupboards, roll top work surfaces with inset stainless steel sink unit and mixer tap, ceramic tiled splash backs, space and plumbing for washing machine and dishwasher, space for under unit fridge and freezer, wall mounted Worcester boiler, power points, ceiling strip light, radiator, door to storage cupboard, door to the garage and UPVC double glazed window to the the front.

FIRST FLOOR - LANDING:
Doors to all four bedrooms, bathroom, storage cupboard with shelving and to airing cupboard with water tank and wooden slatted shelving, ceiling light point, power point and UPVC double glazed window to the side.

BEDROOM ONE: - 4m (13'1") x 3.4m (11'2")
Double room with two fitted double wardrobes and overhead storage cupboards to one wall with space for dressing table, ceiling light point, power points, radiator and UPVC double glazed window to rear.

BEDROOM TWO: - 3.9m (12'10") x 2.9m (9'6")
Double room with ceiling light point, power point, radiator and UPVC double glazed window to the front.

BEDROOM THREE: - 3.1m (10'2") x 2.6m (8'6")
Double room with ceiling light point, power points, radiator and UPVC double glazed window to the rear.

BEDROOM FOUR: - 3.9m (12'10") x 2.3m (7'7")
Double room with ceiling light point, power points, radiator and UPVC double glazed window to the side.

BATHROOM: - 2.1m (6'11") x 1.7m (5'7")
Fitted with a white suite of panel sided bath with Mira electric shower and curtain over, pedestal hand wash basin and low level WC, ceramic part-tiled walls, ceiling light point, radiator and UPVC double glazed obscured glass window to the front.

OUTSIDE:
The property is situated on a large corner plot with expanse of lawn to the front, side and all the way round to the rear boundary. Tarmac driveway with off road parking for two cars lead to the garage and paved pathway leads to the front door.

GARAGE: - 5.2m (17'1") x 2.6m (8'6")
Single garage with metal up and over door, power, light, door to the kitchen and personal door to the side of the property.

REAR GARDEN:
Mainly laid to lawn with flower and shrub borders, small paved patio, path leading along the side of the property with gate to the front and door into the garage. Fully enclosed by walls and panel fencing.

SERVICES AND OUTGOINGS:
Mains water, drainage, gas and electricity. Gas central heating. Telecom subject to regulations. South Oxfordshire District Council - Tax Band D, £1,552.20 (2013/14).
