1 Bedroom Flat For Sale OIRO £99,950

Orchard Walk, Watlington
Under Offer
Contact Watlington Office Office quoting Ref.65
A light and spacious first floor flat in a secure retirement development within a few minutes walk of Watlington town centre. The property benefits from one bedroom, kitchen, sitting/dining room, bathroom., and double glazed windows. There are emergency pull cords in the flat and there is a stairlift for aid on the staircase. The flat is surrounded by pretty well maintained communal gardens and a warden is present at all times. No onward chain.

SITUATION:
Watlington lies at the foot of the Chilterns and has a selection of amenities, including primary and secondary schools, doctor`s surgery, library, sporting activities and a range of shops covering everyday requirements including organic Butcher and highly regarded delicatessen and cheese shop amongst others.. Access to the M40 motorway at Junction 6 for easy access to High Wycombe, London, Birmingham and Heathrow airport. Oxford, Reading, Wallingford and Henley-on-Thames are within a 16 mile radius.

DIRECTIONS:
From our offices in Watlington turn right into Chapel Street follow this road passing the Chequers public house and the primary and community college into Love Lane. The second turning on your right near the top of Love lane is the entrance to Orchard Walk.
ACCOMMODATION

ENTRANCE HALL:
Hatch to loft. Ceiling light. Wall mounted heater. Door to airing cupboard housing hot water tank and slatted shelving.

SITTING ROOM:
4.40m x 3.53m (14`5" x 11`7") A well proportioned room, full of natural light from a large south east facing window over looking the front and communal gardens. TV and radio points. 2 ceiling lights, Telephone point. Two wall mounted Dimplex heaters.

KITCHEN
1.78m x 3.26m (5`10" x 10`8") Ample sized kitchen comprising range of fitted wall and base units in birch and white laminate. Stainless steel inset sink and drainer. Single oven and grill with solid plate Philips hob over. Philips extractor fan. Space for washing machine and under counter fridge. Power points, ceiling light. Wall mounted Dimplex heater. Window overlooking the front gardens.

BEDROOM ONE:
2.95m x 3.26m (9`8" x 10`8") Window over looking the front gardens. Built in mirrored wardrobe. Emergency pull cord. TV and Radio point. Telephone point. Double wall socket.

BATHROOM:
Matching suite comprising panelled bath with grab handles to the side, shower mixer tap with wall bracket. Low level WC and hand wash basin. Wall mounted Dimplex heater. Recess area for storage. Heated towel rail. Central ceiling light. Emergency pull cord.

COMMUNAL GARDENS:
Beautifully landscaped and well maintained gardens with scattered seating area. Fully lit. Blocked pathways to all other properties and circling the garden area.

SERVICES:
Mains electricity, water and drainage.
Economy 7 heating.
BT subject to regulations

OUTGOINGS:
South Oxfordshire District Council Tax Band C
Maintenance service charges in the region of £1650 per annum taken in two six monthly payments.
