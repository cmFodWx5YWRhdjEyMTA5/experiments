KITCHEN/BREAKFAST ROOM: - 7.2m (23'7") x 3.1m (10'2")
A large double aspect room with the kitchen area fitted with a range of white gloss fronted wall and floor units housing cupboards and drawers, roll edge work surfaces including a breakfast bar dividing the cooking and eating areas of the room, ceramic tiled splash backs, inset stainless steel 1½ bowl sink/drainer unit with mixer tap, electric oven with 4 ring gas hob and extractor fan over, space and plumbing for washing machine and dishwasher, space for tower fridge/freezer, ceiling spot lights, power points, ceiling spot lights, UPVC double glazed window to the front, UPVC half glazed door to the side, stone tiled floor through to breakfast/dining end. UPVC patio doors to the rear garden, wooden panel glazed doors to the lounge, wall light points, ceiling light point, power points, telephone point and radiator.

A good size reception room with UPVC double glazed window and patio doors to the rear garden, fireplace with coal-effect gas fire in a marble-effect and wooden surround with mantle and hearth, door to study/bedroom 5, ceiling coving, wall light points, television aerial point, power point and radiators.

FIRST FLOOR - LANDING:
UPVC double glazed window to the front, coved ceiling, ceiling light points, doors to all bedrooms, family bathroom and to airing cupboard with slatted shelving and water tank.

MASTER BEDROOM: - 6m (19'8") x 5.4m (17'9")
Double bedroom with UPVC double glazed window to the front and Velux with remote control blind to the rear, 1 x triple and 1 x double fitted wardrobes with sliding doors to the front, ceiling coving, ceiling light points, radiator, power points, television aerial point and door to:
