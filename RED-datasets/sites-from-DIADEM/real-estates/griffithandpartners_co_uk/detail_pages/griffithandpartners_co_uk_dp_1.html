4 Bedroom Town House For Sale Guide Price £850,000

High Street, Watlington
Available
Contact Watlington Office Office quoting Ref.1668
With commanding views of the High Street, The Cross is an elegant Grade II listed town house with 17th Century origins. Spacious accommodation of 4 reception rooms, kitchen, garden room, master bedroom with dressing room & en suite, 3 further bedrooms, bathroom, stunning gardens, parking & garage.
ACCOMMODATION: GROUND FLOOR:
Arched porch way with a tiled roof, quarry tiled step, light, door bell pull and part glazed wooden door into:

ENTRANCE HALL: - 4.7m (15'5") x 3.1m (10'2")
A large open entrance to the property with doors to the study, dining room, shower room and garden room, stairs rising to the first floor, sealed fireplace, ceiling beams, terracotta tiled floor, wall light points, power points and radiator.

STUDY/LIBRARY: - 4.7m (15'5") x 4.1m (13'5")
Sash window overlooking the Memorial, brick fireplace with a terracotta tiled hearth and ornamental stove, wall light points, ceiling light point, power points, telephone point and radiator.

BATHROOM:
Fitted with a white suite of panel sided bath with shower mixer tap, large walk in shower cubicle, pedestal hand wash basin and low level WC, part tiled walls, tiled floor, ceiling light points, wall mounted towel radiator, hatch with ladder to boarded loft space, sash window with obscured glass to the side.

DINING ROOM:
Sash window to the front with views of the High Street, high ceiling with some exposed beams on decorative scrolled supports, Cherry wood flooring, ceiling light point, recessed downlights, radiator, power point, door to small inner lobby with door to the kitchen, doorway to the drawing room and second stair case rising to the first floor.

DRAWING ROOM AND SNUG: - 9.7m (31'10") x 4.4m (14'5") Max
An irregularly shaped, triple aspect room partially divided by the 17th Century timber frame work, large sash window to the front with views of the High Street and windows to either side, large Inglenook open fireplace with Bressumer beam over, ceiling beams, Cherry wood flooring, wall light points, power points, television aerial point, telephone point, radiators and door to large understairs cupboard with gas and electricity meters and fuse board.

KITCHEN: - 4.5m (14'9") x 3.9m (12'10") Max
Another irregularly shaped room fitted with a range of matching solid Chestnut base and wall units housing cupboards and drawers, roll edge work surfaces, inset 1½ bowl sink/drainer unit with mixer tap, ceramic tiled splashbacks, space and plumbing for dishwasher, washing machine and tumble dryer, space for tower fridge/freezer, gas fired AGA boiler for central heating and separate gas fired AGA stove for domestic water, power points, telephone point, ceiling spot lights, tiled floor, two sash windows to the rear, doorway and step up to:

GARDEN ROOM: - 5.6m (18'4") x 2.6m (8'6")
A lovely part of the property to relax and enjoy the garden, glazed to two sides with glazed double doors leading to the terrace and a solid tiled roof, tiled floor, ceiling spot lights, power points, radiator, cloaks cupboard and door to the entrance hall.

FIRST FLOOR - LANDING:
Stairs rising from the entrance hall, doors to bedrooms two, three and four, ceiling light point and hatch with ladder to very large loft space which, subject to planning permission and listed building consent, could possibly be used to provide extra accommodation for the property.

BEDROOM THREE: - 4.7m (15'5") x 3m (9'10")
Double room with sash window to the front, fireplace with wood surround and mantle, ceiling beams, ceiling light point, power points and radiator.

BEDROOM FOUR: - 3.5m (11'6") x 2.8m (9'2")
Double room with window to the rear, door to built in shelved cupboard, ceiling light point, power points and radiator.

BEDROOM TWO: - 4.7m (15'5") x 3.6m (11'10")
Double room with sash window to the front, sealed fireplace with wooden surround and mantle, door to landing two, ceiling light point, power points and radiator.

LANDING TWO:
Stairs rising from first floor, door to master bedroom, bedroom two and ceiling light point.

MASTER BEDROOM: - 5m (16'5") x 3.6m (11'10") Max
A lovely bright double aspect room with sash window with views of the High Street and one to the side, door and steps down to the dressing room and en suite, door to cupboard with immersion heater, ceiling beams, ceiling light points, power points and radiator.

EN SUITE BATHROOM: - 4.3m (14'1") x 1.9m (6'3")
Fitted with a white suite of shower ended bath with mixer tap and curved glazed screen, two pedestal hand wash basins and low level WC, part tiled walls, storage cupboard with fitted shelves, ceiling spot lights, radiator and window to the side.

DRESSING ROOM: - 4.3m (14'1") x 2.6m (8'6")
Dormer window to the side, ceiling spot light, power points, telephone point and door to:

OUTSIDE:
To the right hand side of the property is a large part wooden part brick and flint garage with double doors to the front, pitched roof, power and light. Attached is a pitched roof car port with inspection pit, hatch to storage loft and lighting. Parking for three more cars.

The rear garden has been professionally designed and has developed into a stunning feature of the property. A brick paved terrace area adjacent to the garden room with plenty of space for table, chairs and loungers. Immaculate shaped lawn with rose adorned pagola, edged by gravel pathways and deep planted beds planted with mature trees and shrubs. The garden is enclosed by brick and flint walls and wood panel fencing. Outside lighting and tap.

SERVICES AND OUTGOINGS:
Mains water, drainage, electricity and gas. Gas central heating. Telecom subject to regulations. South Oxfordshire District Council - Tax Band G, £2598.45 (2014/15).
