4 Bedroom Semi-Detached For Sale Guide Price £330,000

Westfield Road, Benson
Sold
Contact Watlington Office Office quoting Ref.1603
This well presented, extended semi detached family home is situated on a large corner plot on a popular residential road in Benson. The property benefits from four bedrooms, two reception rooms, kitchen, utility, cloakroom, shower room, front & rear gardens, plenty of off road parking and a garage.
ACCOMMODATION - GROUND FLOOR:
Wooden panel glazed door into:

ENTRANCE HALL:
Double glazed window to the front, doors to the lounge, cloakroom and utility room, ceiling light point, coved ceiling, radiator, telephone point and power point.

CLOAKROOM:
Fitted with a low level WC and wall mounted hand wash basin, double glazed obscured glass window to the side, ceiling light point and power point.

LOUNGE: - 4.8m (15'9") x 4.5m (14'9")
A good sized bright reception room with large double glazed window to the front, arch way through to the dining room, gas fire with a polished stone back plate and hearth, wooden surround and mantle, obscured glass panel glazed door with stairs rising to the first floor, television aerial point, ceiling light point, coved ceiling, radiator and power point.

DINING ROOM: - 3m (9'10") x 2.4m (7'10")
UPVC double glazed sliding doors to the rear garden, panel glazed sliding door to the kitchen, ceiling light point, coved ceiling, radiator and power points.

KITCHEN: - 3m (9'10") x 2.4m (7'10")
Fitted with a range of matching wall and base units housing cupboards and drawers, roll edge work surfaces with ceramic tiled splash backs, inset stainless steel 1½ bowl sink/drainer unit with mixer tap, space for free standing electric cooker with extractor fan over, space for under unit fridge, door to under stairs larder cupboard, door to utility room, ceiling spot lights, coved ceiling, power points and UPVC double glazed window to the rear.

UTILITY ROOM: - 3m (9'10") x 2.8m (9'2")
A good sized room that, if desired, could be made smaller and knocked through to increase the size of the kitchen. Fitted base cupboards with roll edge work surface, wall mounted recently serviced Potterton boiler, ceiling strip light, coved ceiling, power point, radiator, power points, door to the hallway, UPVC double glazed window and door to the rear garden.

FIRST FLOOR - LANDING:
Doors to all four bedrooms, shower room and to airing cupboard housing hot water tank and wooden slatted shelves, hatch with ladder to partially boarded loft space, ceiling light point.

BEDROOM ONE: - 3.5m (11'6") x 2.7m (8'10")
Double room with two double wardrobed fitting to one wall, ceiling light point, coved ceiling, radiator, power points and UPVC double glazed window to the rear.

BEDROOM TWO: - 4.1m (13'5") x 2.7m (8'10")
Double room with fitted wardrobes, ceiling light point, coved ceiling, radiator, power points, television aerial point and UPVC double glazed window to the front.

BEDROOM THREE: - 3m (9'10") x 2.8m (9'2")
Double room with double glazed windows to the front and rear, ceiling light point, coved ceiling, power points, radiator and hatch to loft space.

BEDROOM FOUR: - 2.7m (8'10") x 2m (6'7")
Single room with UPVC double glazed window to the front, large storage cupboard, ceiling light point, power points and radiator.

SHOWER ROOM:
Fitted with a suite comprising a curved corner shower cubicle, vanity hand wash basin with cupboard below and low level WC, ceramic tiled walls, ceiling light point, extractor fan, radiator and UPVC double glazed obscured glass window to the rear.

OUTSIDE:
Directly in front of the property is laid to lawn with flower and shrub borders. A large brick paved driveway lies to the side providing plenty of off road parking and leads to the single garage with metal up and over door, power, light and personal door to the garden. A tall wooden gate gives access to the rear garden which, with the nature of the surrounding properties, is very private. Paved patio directly adjacent to the housing leading on to the lawn with flower and shrub borders, garden shed, outside light and tap.

SERVICES AND OUTGOINGS:
Mains water, drainage, gas and electricity. Gas central heating, Telecom subject to regulations. South Oxfordshire District Council, Tax Band D - £1,528.40 (2013/14).
