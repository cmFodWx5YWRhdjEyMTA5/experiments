3 Bedroom Semi-Detached For Sale Guide Price £309,995

Love Lane, Watlington
Under Offer
Contact Watlington Office Office quoting Ref.1548
A three bedroom semi-detached house built about 1950, benefitting from UPVC double glazed windows, mature south west facing rear garden, off street parking and within walking distance to the centre of the town for shopping and within a few minutes walk of the schools and recreation ground.
ACCOMMODATION - GROUND FLOOR:
UPVC half glazed obscured glass door into:

LOBBY:
UPVC double glazed window to the front and half glazed door to the hallway, two ceiling light points and power points.

HALLWAY:
Doors to the lounge, kitchen, cloakroom, cupboard housing hot water tank and half glazed door to the outer lobby with window to the side and glazed door to the rear garden. Stairs rising to the first floor, electric storage heater, ceiling light point and power points.

SITTING ROOM: - 5.3m (17'5") x 3.2m (10'6")
Double aspect with UPVC double glazed windows to the front and rear, television aerial point, telephone point, power points, ceiling light point and electric storage heater.

KITCHEN/BREAKFAST ROOM: - 4.5m (14'9") x 3.5m (11'6")
Fitted with a range of matching floor and base units housing cupboards and drawers, roll edge work surface, inset stainless steel sink/drainer unit with mixer tap, inset four ring halogen hob with built in electric oven below, space and plumbing for washing machine, space for tower fridge/freezer, ceramic tiled splash backs, power points, ceiling spot light nests, coved ceiling, electric storage heater, space for table and chairs, UPVC double glazed windows to the side and rear and UPVC double glazed door to the rear garden.

CLOAKROOM:
Fitted with the low level WC, UPVC double glazed window to the side and ceiling light point.

FIRST FLOOR - LANDING:
Doors to all three bedrooms and bathroom, UPVC double glazed window to the side, hatch to boarded loft space, ceiling light point and power point.

BEDROOM THREE: - 2.9m (9'6") x 2.1m (6'11")
A good size single room with UPVC double glazed window to the front, door to wardrobe, electric panel heater, television aerial point, ceiling light point and power points.

BEDROOM ONE: - 4m (13'1") x 3.4m (11'2")
Double room with UPVC double glazed window to the rear, electric storage heater, ceiling light point and power points.

BEDROOM TWO: - 3.6m (11'10") x 3.4m (11'2")
Double room with UPVC double glazed windiow to the rear, door to cupboard with slatted shelving, electric storage heater, television aerial point, ceiling light point and power points.

BATHROOM: - 1.8m (5'11") x 1.6m (5'3")
Fitted with a panel sides bath with shower over, rail and curtain and pedestal hand wash basin, ceramic tiled walls, ceiling light point, extractor fan, wall mounted electric heater and UPVC double glazed obscured glass window to the side.

OUTSIDE:
The property is approached over a wooden fence with gate and paved pathway leading to the front door, with established flower and shrub beds to either side, paved driveway with off street parking and gate giving access to the rear garden.

The south west facing rear garden is smartly kept with paved patio area adjacent to the property leading on to the lawn area and a paved pathway to the garden shed at the bottom. Established flower and shrub beds to either side.

SERVICES AND OUTGOINGS:
Mains water, drainage and electricity. Electric storage heating, Telecom subject to regulations. South Oxfordshire District Council - Tax Band C.
