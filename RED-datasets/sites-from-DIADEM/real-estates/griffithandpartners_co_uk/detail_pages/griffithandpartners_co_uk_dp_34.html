2 Bedroom House For Sale Guide Price £250,000

33 High Street, Watlington
Under Offer
Contact Watlington Office Office quoting Ref.1347
CALLING ALL PROJECT HUNTERS & DEVELOPERS. Currently a 2 bedroom Grade II period house with potential to be a gorgeous home. Watlington, South-facing garden & parking space to the rear, it can`t get much better than that! SEALED BIDS BY 12 NOON 07/12/12.
DESCRIPTION:
33 High Street is a Grade II Listed Building located within the Conservation Area. The property, which adjoins Watlington Branch Library, comprises of two floors with garden area to the rear. There is an entrance lobby, two reception rooms and a kitchen on the ground floor and two bedrooms, bathroom and storage area on the first floor. A garden is located to the rear of the building together with a parking space for one car.

The property has its own direct electricity supply which serves night storage heaters and a water heater in the kitchen. There is no gas supply to the building and currently there is no water supply to the building as this was previously supplied via the library premises and has now been disconnected. Any ingoing occupier will need to arrange for a new supply to be connected to 33 High Street.

A new 125 year lease will be offered.
