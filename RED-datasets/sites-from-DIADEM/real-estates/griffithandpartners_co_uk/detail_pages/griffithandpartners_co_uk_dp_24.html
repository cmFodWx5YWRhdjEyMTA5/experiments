4 Bedroom Detached For Sale Guide Price £299,950

Brinkinfield Road, Chalgrove
Under Offer
Contact Watlington Office Office quoting Ref.1622
Situated in a popular residential road in the village, a spacious detached house comprising of kitchen/breakfast room, lounge/diner, four bedrooms, family bathroom, integral garage, plenty of parking and a good size garden. Updating this property would be most benefitial, viewing recommended.
ACCOMMODATION - GROUND FLOOR:
Double glazed door with UPVC double glazed obscured glass side panels into:

ENTRANCE LOBBY:
Wood panelled walls, wooden flooring, ceiling light point and panel glazed door into:

HALLWAY:
Panel glazed doors to the lounge/diner and kitchen/breakfast room, stairs rising to the first floor and ceiling light point.

KITCHEN/BREAKFAST ROOM: - 4.9m (16'1") x 3.2m (10'6")
Fitted with a range of wood fronted base and wall units housing cupboards and drawers, roll edge work surfaces, inset double sink with drainer and mixer tap, inset electric hob with electric oven under and extractor hood over, integrated fridge and freezer, ceiling light point, power points, multi fuel stove, door to the garage and UPVC double glazed window to the front.

LOUNGE/DINING ROOM: - 5.8m (19'0") x 4.1m (13'5")
A good size reception room. Wood burner in fireplace surround with wooden mantle shelf, ceiling light points, power points, television aerial point, radiator and UPVC double glazed windows and door to the rear.

FIRST FLOOR - LANDING:
UPVC double glazed window to the side, doors to all four bedrooms, bathroom, storage cupboard and airing cupboard with water tank and slatted shelving. Ceiling light point and power point.

BATHROOM: - 2.2m (7'3") x 1.7m (5'7")
Fitted with a white suite of panel sided bath with shower and curtain over, vanity unit with hand wash basin and concealed cistern WC. Ceiling light point, wall mounted electric heater and UPVC double glazed obscured glass window to the front.

BEDROOM ONE: - 3.9m (12'10") x 3m (9'10")
Double room with UPVC double glazed window to the front, radiator, ceiling light point and power points.

BEDROOM TWO: - 3.9m (12'10") x 2.3m (7'7")
Large single room with UPVC double glazed window to the side, radiator, ceiling light point and power points.

BEDROOM THREE: - 3.9m (12'10") x 3.7m (12'2")
Double room with UPVC double glazed window to the rear, radiator, ceiling light point, power points and hatch to loft space.

BEDROOM FOUR: - 3m (9'10") x 2.7m (8'10")
Double room with UPVC double glazed window to the rear, radiator, ceiling light point, power points and door to cupboard.

OUTSIDE - REAR GARDEN:
A good size low maintenance garden with patio areas, fruit trees and spring/summer bulbs planted in raised wood chip filled beds, a large shed across the width of the garden with power and light. Enclosed access to the side of the house with doors to the back and front, door to the garage, power and light.

INTEGRATED GARAGE: - 4.9m (16'1") x 2.6m (8'6")
Single garage with metal up and over door, power and light.

TO THE FRONT:
Wall to the front boundary with personal gate and pathway leading to the front door, double gates to the driveway with plenty of off street parking leading to the garage. Shrub borders and beds.

SERVICES AND OUTGOINGS:
Mains water, drainage and electricity. Solid fuel heating. Telecom subject to regulations. South Oxfordshire District Council, Tax Band E - £1,897.12 (2013/14).
